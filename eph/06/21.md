# Tychicus

Tychicus was one of several men who served with Paul. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# so that he may encourage your hearts

Here "hearts" is a metonym for people's inner beings. AT: "so that he may encourage you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
