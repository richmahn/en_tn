## that the word might be given to me ##

AT: "that God might give me the word" or "God might give me the message" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## when I open my mouth to make known with boldness ##

AT: "when I speak to boldly make known"

## for which I am an ambassador in chains ##

AT: "I am now in prison because I am a representative of the gospel"

## that in my imprisonment I may speak boldly as I ought to speak ##

AT: "that I may speak God's message as boldly as I should here in prison"
