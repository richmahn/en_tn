## there will be made known to the rulers and authorities in the heavenly places through the Church the complex wisdom of God ##

AT: "God will make his complex wisdom known to the rulers and authorities in the heavenly places through the Church" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## rulers and authorities ##

These words share similar meanings. Paul uses them together to emphasize that every spiritual being will know God's wisdom. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## according to the eternal plan ##

AT: "in keeping with the eternal plan" or "consistent with the eternal plan"

## that he intended ##

AT: "that he accomplished" or "that he fulfilled completely"
