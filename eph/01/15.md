## translationNotes

* **Because of this** - AT: “For this reason”
* **your love for all those set apart for him** - Your love for all the believers in Christ. AT: "your love for all the saints in Christ"
* **I have not stopped thanking God** - This can be translated as a positive statement. AT: "I continue to thank God" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
