This book is a letter from Paul to members of the church at Ephesus.

## through the will of God  ##

“chosen by God” or “by the will of God”.

## set apart for God  ##

“morally blameless” or “consecrated” or “holy ones”. Alternate translation: “saints”.

## grace be to you ##

The word "you" refers to all the believers at Ephesus. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## May grace be to you and peace  ##

<b>May grace be to you and peace <b>  This is a common greeting and blessing Paul often extends in his letters.
