## He ascended ##

AT: "He went up"

## he also descended ##

AT: "he also went down" or "he also came down"

## into the depths of the earth ##

Possible meanings include 1) "into the lower regions, the earth" or 2) "into the lower parts, the earth"

## he might fill all things ##

AT: "he might completely fill everything" or "all might be filled with him"
