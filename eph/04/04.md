## one body ##

All believers in the family of God are like the various members of a human body. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## one Spirit ##

There is one Holy Spirit.

## called in one ##

"specially chosen in one" or "appointed in one"

## in one confident expectation ##

"in one sure hope".

## Father of all...and in all ##

"Father of everything...over everything...through everything...in everything"
