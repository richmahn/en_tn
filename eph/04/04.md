## translationNotes

* **one body** - All believers in the family of God are like the various members of a human body. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **one Spirit** - There is one Holy Spirit.
* **called in one** - "specially chosen in one" or "appointed in one"
* **in one confident expectation** - "in one sure hope".
* **Father of all...and in all** - "Father of everything...over everything...through everything...in everything"
