## put away falsehood ##

## Speak truth, each one with his neighbor ##

AT: "believers must speak the truth to their neighbors"

AT: "You may get angry, but do not sin"

## Do not let the sun go down upon your anger ##

AT: "You must get over your anger before night comes"
