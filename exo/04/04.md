## translationNotes

* **take it by the tail** - "pick it up by the tail" or "grasp it by the tail"
* **became a staff** - "turned into a rod" or "changed into a staff"
* ** the God of their ancestors, the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob** - Abraham, Isaac and Jacob were three of their ancestors. They all worshiped the same God.
