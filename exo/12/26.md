# He set our households free

This means that Yahweh spared the Israelites' firstborn sons. AT: "He did not kill the firstborn sons in our houses" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# as Yahweh had commanded Moses and Aaron

"everything that Yahweh told Moses and Aaron to do"
