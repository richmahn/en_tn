## translationNotes

* **Yahweh will pass through** - "Yahweh will pass through Egypt" (UDB); (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **pass over your door** - This means that God will spare or have mercy on the Israelites in homes that are "covered" by blood.
