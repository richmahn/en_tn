## translationNotes

* **You must not mistreat any widow or fatherless child** - AT: "You must treat all widows and fatherless children fairly." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
* **widow** - "woman whose husband has died"
* **fatherless child** - "orphan" or "child with no parents"
