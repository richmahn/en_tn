## gores ##

"injures with its horns"

## the ox must surely be stoned ##

AT: "you must stone the ox to death" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## its flesh must not be eaten ##

AT: "you must not eat its flesh"

## the ox's owner must be acquitted ##

AT: "you must acquit the ox's owner"

## its owner also must be put to death ##

"its owner also must be killed" or "its owner also must die"
