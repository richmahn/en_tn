# has gored

"has injured with its horns"

# thirty shekels of silver

"330 grams of silver." A shekel weighed eleven grams. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bmoney]])

# the ox must be stoned

This can be stated in active form. AT: "you must kill the ox by stoning it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
