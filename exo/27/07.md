# General Information:

Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people should do.

# The poles must be put into the rings, and the poles must be on the two sides of the altar, to carry it

This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must put the poles into the rings and place them on each side of the altar to carry it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# planks

a long, flat piece of wood that is thicker than a board

# you were shown on the mountain

This can be stated in active form. AT: "that I have shown to you here on this mountain" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
