## on the fifteenth day of the second month ##

<b>on the fifteenth day of the second month</b> AT: "on day 15 of the second month"; See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]]

## The whole community of Israelites complained ##

AT: "Almost all of the Israelite people complained" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])

## complained ##

This means to say that you are unhappy about something or do not like it.
