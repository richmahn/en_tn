# General Information:

Bezalel's work crew continues to build the tabernacle and furniture.

# Bezalel made ... gold for it

For 37:25-26 see how you translated many of these words in [Exodus 30:1-2](../30/01.md) and [Exodus 30:3](../30/03.md).

# cubit

A cubit is 46 centimeters. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bdistance]])

# Its horns were made as one piece with it

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "He made the horns as one piece with the altar" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
