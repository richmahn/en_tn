## translationNotes

* **Moses said to the people** - Moses responds to the Israelites fears.
* **for you** - pronoun "you" refers to the Israelites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **For you will never see again the Egyptians** - God is stating that he will slay the Egyptian army. AT: "The Egyptian army will not attack you again." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
