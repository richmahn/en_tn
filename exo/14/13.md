## Moses said to the people ##

Moses responds to the Israelites fears.

## for you ##

pronoun "you" refers to the Israelites. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## For you will never see again the Egyptians ##

God is stating that he will slay the Egyptian army. AT: "The Egyptian army will not attack you again." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
