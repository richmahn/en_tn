# Moses said to the people

Moses responds to the Israelites' fears.

# provide for you

The pronoun "you" refers to the Israelites. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

# For you will never see again the Egyptians

Moses used a polite way of saying God was going to destroy the Egyptian army. AT: "For God will kill the Egyptians" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
