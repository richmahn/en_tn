* **earthen altar** - an altar made of materials from the ground, such as stone, soil, or clay
* **cause my name to be honored** - AT: "cause you to honor my name." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **You must not go up by steps to my altar** - "Do not make an altar that has steps"
* **exposing your private parts** - "showing your naked body"
