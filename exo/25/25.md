# General Information:

Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.

# one handbreadth wide

a measurement of 7 to 8 centimeters

# frame for it

"frame for the table"

# feet were

"legs are"

# The rings must be attached

This can be stated in active form. AT: "You must attach the rings" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# in order to carry

"so you can carry"
