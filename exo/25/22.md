# General Information:

Yahweh continues to speak to Moses.

# It is at the ark that I will meet with you

"I will meet with you at the ark." In 25:22 the word "you" is singular and refers to Moses. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

# atonement lid

This is the lid that sits on top of the ark where the atonement offering was made. See how you translated this in [Exodus 25:17](./17.md).
