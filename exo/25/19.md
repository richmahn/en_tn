# General Information:

Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.

# atonement lid

This is the lid that sits on top of the ark where the atonement offering was made. See how you translated this in [Exodus 25:17](./17.md).

# They must be made

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "You must make them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
