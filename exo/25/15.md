# General Information:

Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people must do.

# atonement lid

This is the lid that sits on top of the ark where the atonement offering was made.

# two and a half cubits ... a cubit and a half

A cubit is 46 centimeters. AT: "2.5 cubits ... 1.5 cubits" or "115 centimeters ... 69 centimeters" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bdistance]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-fraction]])

# hammered gold

"beaten gold"
