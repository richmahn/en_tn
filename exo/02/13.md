# He went out

"Moses went out"

# behold

The word "behold" here shows that Moses was surprised by what he saw. You can use a word in your language that will give this meaning.

# the one who was in the wrong

This was a customary way of saying "the one who started the fight." Alternate translation: "the one who was guilty of starting the fight" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
