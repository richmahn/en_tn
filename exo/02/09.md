## translationNotes

* **she brought him** - "the Hebrew woman brought him"
* **he became her son** - "he became the son of Pharaoh's daughter"
* **drew him** - "pulled him"
* **Because I drew him from the water** - Translators may add a footnote that says "The name Moses sounds like the Hebrew word that means 'pull.'"
