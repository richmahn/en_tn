## translationNotes

* **Thus the people** - "And so the people"
* **behold** - The word “behold” here adds emphasis to what follows. AT: “Indeed”
* **As Yahweh had commanded, in that way they did it.** - AT: "They did it in the way that Yahweh had commanded them to to it."
