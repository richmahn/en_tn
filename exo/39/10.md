## precious stones ##

Twelve very rare and expensive stones are listed. The are almost the same stone that are listed in [Exodus 28:17](../28/17.md). Some languages may not have words of each of these stones. The important fact is that they were valuable, and different from one another. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])
