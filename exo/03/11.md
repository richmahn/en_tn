# Who am I, that I should go to Pharaoh ... Egypt?

Moses uses this question to tell God that Moses is a nobody and no one will listen to him. AT: "I am not important enough to go to Pharaoh ... Egypt!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
