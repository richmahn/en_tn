## wilderness of Sin ##

We do not know the exact locations of these places. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Rephidim ##

This means "the resting place," a place to rest on long journeys through the wilderness.

## Why do you test Yahweh? ##

AT: "You should not test Yahweh!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## To kill us and our children and our cattle with thirst? ##

AT: "You only brought us out here to kill us and our children and cattle because we had no water to drink!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]]) and (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
