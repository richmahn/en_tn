# Rephidim

This was the name of a place in the desert. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# So Joshua fought Amalek

Joshua represents himself and the Israelites that he led into battle. AT: "So Joshua and the men he chose fought against the Amalekites" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# Hur

Hur was a friend of Moses and Aaron. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
