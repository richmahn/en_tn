# General Information:

Yahweh continues telling Moses what Moses must tell the people.

# It must not be applied to people's skin

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "You must not put the anointing oil that is dedicated to Yahweh on a person's skin" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# with the same formula

"with the same ingredients" or "with the same items"
