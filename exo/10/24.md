# not a hoof of them may be left behind

Here the word "hoof" refers to the entire animal. This can be stated in active form. AT: "we cannot leave behind a single animal" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
