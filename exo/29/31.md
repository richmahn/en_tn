Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.

## the ram for the consecration of the priests ##

"the ram you killed to consecrate the priests"

## in a holy place ##

This is not the same as the holy place outside of the most holy place. This refers to a place within the courtyard. AT: "at the entrance to the tent of meeting"

## tent of meeting ##

See how you translated this in [Exodus 27:20](../27/20.md).

## that were given ##

AT: "that you sacrificed" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## It must not be eaten ##

AT: "No one must eat it"

## because it has been consecrated—reserved—for me ##

AT: "because it is holy" or "because it is sacred"
