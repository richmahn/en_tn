# General Information:

Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.

# Then you must kill the ram

The ram was killed by cutting its throat. This can be stated clearly in the translation. AT: "Then kill the ram by slitting its throat" or "Then kill the ram by cutting its throat" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
