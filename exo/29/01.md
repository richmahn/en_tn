## Now ##

"Now" marks a change from garments for priests to consecrating priests.

## you must do ##

Here "you" refers to Moses.

## to consecrate them ##

"to consecrate Aaron and his sons"

## serve me ##

Here "me" refers to Yahweh.

## one young bull ##

a male cow

## rubbed with oil ##

"anointed with oil"

## bread...cakes...wafers ##

These are different kinds of food made from flour.
