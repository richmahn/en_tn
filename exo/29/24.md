## translationNotes

* God continues speaking to Moses.
* **You must put these** - Here "these" refers to the parts of the sacrifice mentioned in the previous verses.
* **it will be an offering made to me by fire** - AT: "it will be a burnt offering to me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
