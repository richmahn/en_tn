## my desire will be satisfied on them; ##

AT: I will satisfy my desire on them. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## sank like lead in the mighty waters. ##

Lead is a heavy metal, commonly used to make things sink in water. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## Who is like you, Yahweh, among the gods? ##

AT: "O Yahweh, no one is like you among the gods!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
