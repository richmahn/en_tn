## translationNotes

* **with your right hand** - The "right hand" represents the power of God. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **and the earth swallowed them.** - This refers to God sending the enemies of Israel into the world of the dead. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
