## translationNotes

* **on you yourself** - This means that even Pharaoh will be hurt by the plagues. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
* **I will do this so that you may know** - The word "this" refers to the plagues that Moses had just told Pharaoh about.
