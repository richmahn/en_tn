## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues to tell Moses what the people do.
* **pomegranate** - a round fruit (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
* **a golden bell and a pomegranate** - This phrase is repeated to show the pattern of the design on the robe.
* **so that its sound can be heard** - AT: "so that the bells make a sound" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
