## Now then ##

"Now" is used here to mark a break in the main storyline. Here God explains why He will take action.

<b>great power...mighty hand<b>- These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
