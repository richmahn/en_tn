## he said to Moses ##

It is assumed that Joshua did not go into the cloud with Moses (See: [Exodus 24:16](../24/16.md)). It is assumed that Joshua joined Moses at this point on the side of the mountain and finished coming down together (see:  [Exodus 24:12](../24/12.md)). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
