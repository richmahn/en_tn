## running wild ##

acting in an unrestrained manner

## to mocking from their enemies ##

"brought them to shame before their enemies"

## go back and forth from entrance to entrance ##

"go in and out from one entrance of the camp to the other entrance." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]]) "entrance to entrance" represents the whole camp. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
