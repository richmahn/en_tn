## wear out yourself ##

This means to get very tired. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## burden is too heavy for you ##

This means that the work is too much for you to do. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## advice ##

"guidance" or "instruction"

## You must show them the way to walk ##

AT: "You must show them how to live"; (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
