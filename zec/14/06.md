## translationNotes

* These verses continue the description of the final war for the city of Jerusalem and of how God will save her.
* **running water** - Some versions translate "living water" or "life-giving water." The Hebrew text says "living water," but this normally means running or flowing water, rather than still or stagnant water. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **the eastern sea** - This refers to the Dead Sea, which is east of Jerusalem.
* **the western sea** - This refers to the Mediterranean Sea.
