These verses continue the description of the final war for the city of Jerusalem and of how God will save her.

## all the peoples that waged war against Jerusalem ##

This refers to the nations spoken of in [Zechariah 14:1](./01.md).

## standing up on their feet ##

AT: "walking around"

## each hand will be raised against its neighbor's hand ##

Each person will attack another. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
