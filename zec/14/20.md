# General Information:

These verses continue the description of the final war for the city of Jerusalem and of how God will save her.

# the bells of the horses will say

"the inscription on the bells of the horses will say"

# the basins in Yahweh's house

These basins were used for boiling meat in the courtyard of the temple. Alternate translation: "the cooking pots in the courtyard of the temple" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# will be like the bowls before the altar

It is implied that the basins will be sacred like the bowls used for catching the blood of the sacrifices. Alternate translation: "will be as sacred as the bowls used at the altar" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
