These verses continue telling about the coming attack against Jerusalem and how God will rescue the city.

## the tents of Judah ##

The people of Judah, where "tents" represents "homes," and "homes" represents the people who live in them. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the house of David ##

The descendants of David, or perhaps the ruling class of people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the angel of Yahweh ##

An angel sent by Yahweh to protect the people.
