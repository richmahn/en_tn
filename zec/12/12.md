# The land will mourn

This represents all the people living in the land of Judah. AT: "All the people in the land of Judah will mourn" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# The clan of the house of David ... The clan of the house of Nathan ... The clan of the house of Levi

Here "house" represents descendants. AT: "The descendants of David ... The descendants of Nathan ... The descendants of Levi" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
