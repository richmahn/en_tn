## translationNotes

* The parable about shepherds and sheep continues.
* **broke it to break the covenant that I had made with all of my tribes** - The prophet is speaking and acting for Yahweh here.
* **had spoken** - AT: "had spoken a message to them"
* **thirty pieces of silver** - 330 grams of silver. A shekel weighed eleven grams. (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
