## From them will come the cornerstone ##

"From them the cornerstone will come" or "Some of their descendants will be very important rulers" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## from them will come the tent peg ##

"from them the tent peg will come" AT: "will come leaders who will hold the nation together" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## They will be like warriors ##

AT: "They will be mighty in battle" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## who trample their enemies into the mud of the streets in battle ##

AT: "who defeat their enemies completely" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## and they will shame those who ride warhorses ##

AT: "and they will defeat their enemies on horses who fight them"
