God continues talking about his people.

## for they will be the jewels of a crown ##

AT: "in our land they will be like beautiful stones in a crown" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## The young men will flourish on grain ##

The young men will live on food they receive from the harvest.

## and the virgins on sweet wine ##

Young women will enjoy new wine. These references to young men and young women represent the entire population of Israel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
