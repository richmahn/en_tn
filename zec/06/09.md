## the word of Yahweh came to me ##

AT: "Yahweh spoke to me"

## Heldai, Tobijah, and Jedaiah ... Jehozadak ##

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Then take the silver and gold, make a crown ##

## Joshua the son of Jehozadak ##

This is not the same man who was Moses' helper; this is the high priest from the book of Haggai.
