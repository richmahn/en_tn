## These are the four winds of heaven ##

The angel explains that the chariots with horses are symbols for the 4 winds of heaven.

## four winds ##

The word "winds" refers to spirits from heaven.  A spirit is like the wind because they are both invisible forces. AT: "four spirits" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## station ##

the place someone or something is supposed to be.
