## in the fourth year of king Darius, on the fourth day of Kislev, the ninth month ##

AT: "During year 4 of king Darius' reign, on day 4 of month 9, which is the month of Kislev"

## Kislev ##

This is the name of a month. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## the word of Yahweh came to Zechariah ##

AT: “Yahweh spoke to Zechariah”

## Sharezer and Regem Melech ##

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]]).

## they said,"Should I mourn ... ##

The word "they" refers to Sharezer and Regem Melech.
