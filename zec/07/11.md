## translationNotes

* **set their shoulders stubbornly** - AT: "they were like cattle that refused to be yoked" or "they were like cattle that refused to obey" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **they stopped up their ears** - "they closed up their ears"
* **They made their hearts as hard as rock** - AT: "They made up their minds to reject Yahweh's messages" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **by the mouth of the prophets** - AT: "through the words of the prophets"
