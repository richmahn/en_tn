# Sharezer and Regem-Melek

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# beg

Here the word "beg" means to plead or ask urgently.

# before the face of Yahweh

Here "face" is a metonym for Yahweh's presence. AT: "in the presence of Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
