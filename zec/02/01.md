# I lifted up my eyes

The word "eyes" represents the person who looks. AT: "I looked up" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# measuring line

a rope with a certain length that a person would use to measure buildings or large sections of land

# So he said to me

"So the man with the measuring line said to me"
