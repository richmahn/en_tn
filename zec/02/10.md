## daughter of Zion ##

This is another name for Jerusalem that refers to the city like a daughter of the heavenly city of Zion. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## encamp ##

to set up and use a camp

## This is Yahweh's declaration. ##

This phrase is translated as "Yahweh says" in the UDB. You can translate this concept whichever way is best in your language. This phrase is used many times in Zechariah.

## nations will join themselves to Yahweh ##

"nations will make become loyal to Yahweh"
