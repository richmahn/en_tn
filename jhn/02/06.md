# two to three metretes

You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "75 to 115 liters." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])

# to the brim

This means "to the very top" or "completely full."

# the head waiter

This refers to the person in charge of the food and drink.
