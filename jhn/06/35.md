## translationNotes

* Jesus continues speaking to the people ([[:en:bible:notes:jhn:06:32]]).
* **I am the bread of life** - Jesus is comparing himself to bread. Just as bread is necessary for our physical life, Jesus is necessary for our spiritual life. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **he who comes to me I will absolutely not drive away** - "I will keep everyone who comes to me." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
