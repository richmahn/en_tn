## Which one of you convicts me of sin? ##

"None of you can convict me of sin." Jesus asks this question to emphasize that he is sinless. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## why do you not believe me? ##

"You have no reason for not believing in me."  Jesus uses this question to rebuke the Jewish leaders for their unbelief. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
