# General Information:

Jesus is now teaching the Jews in the temple.

# How does this man know so much?

The remark appears in the form of a question to emphasize the Jewish leaders' surprise that Jesus has so much knowledge. AT: "He cannot possibly know so much about the scriptures!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

# but is of him who sent me

"but comes from God, the one who sent me"
