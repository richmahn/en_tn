## our law judge a man ##

Nicodemus means that those who follow the law do not judge a man before a trial. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## Does our law judge a man..? ##

"Our Jewish law does not allow us to judge a man..." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Are you also from Galilee? ##

"You must also be one of those inferior persons from Galilee!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
