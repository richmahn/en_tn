## translationNotes

* **He was in the world and the world was made through him and the world did not know him** - "But even though he was in this world and God created all that is here through him, people still did not recognize him."
* **He came to what was his own things, and his own people did not receive him** - "He came to his own fellow-countrymen, and his own fellow-countrymen did not accept him either."
