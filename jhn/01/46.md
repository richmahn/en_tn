# Nathaniel said to him

"Nathaniel said to Philip"

# Can any good thing come out of Nazareth?

This remark appears in the form of a question in order to add emphasis. AT: "No good thing can come out of Nazareth!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

# in whom is no deceit

This can be stated in a positive way. AT: "a completely truthful man" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])
