# General Information:

Jesus shows himself again to the disciples at the Sea of Tiberias. Verses 2 and 3 tell us what happens in the story before Jesus appears. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-background]])

# After these things

"Some time later"

# with Thomas called Didymus

You can translate this in an active form. AT: "with Thomas whom we called Didymus" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# Didymus

This is a male name that means "twin." See how this name is translated in [John 11:15](../11/15.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
