Jesus continues to speak to the Pharisees.

## Truly, truly ##

Translate this as you did in [John 1:49](../01/49.md).

## sheepfold ##

This is a fenced area where a shepherd keeps his sheep.

## thief and a robber  ##

<b>thief and a robber <b>- This is the use of two words with similar meanings to add emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hendiadys]])
