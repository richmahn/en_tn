## translationNotes

* **You are** - The word "you" throughout this passage is plural and refers to the disciples of Jesus. (See: [[ en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_youdual]])
* **Already you are clean because of the message that I have spoken to you.** - Alternate translation: "It is as if you have already been pruned and are clean branches because you have obeyed what I have taught to you."  See:  ([[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
