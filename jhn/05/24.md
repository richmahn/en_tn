# Truly, truly

See how you translated this in [John 1:51](../01/49.md).

# he who hears my word

Here "word" is a metonym that represents the message of Jesus. AT: "anyone who hears my message" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# will not be condemned

This can be stated positively. AT: "will be judged to be innocent" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublenegatives]])
