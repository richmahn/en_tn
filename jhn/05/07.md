## translationNotes

* **when the water is stirred up** - "when the angel moves the water." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]).
* **another steps down before me** - "someone else always enters the water before me." People would go down a staircase to get into the water in the pool.
* **get up** - "stand up"
* **take up your mat** - "pick up your sleeping mat"
