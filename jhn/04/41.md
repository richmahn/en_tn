# his word

Here "word" is a metonym that stands for the message that Jesus proclaimed. AT: "his message" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# world

The "world" is a metonym that refers to all the people who live in the world. AT: "all the people of the world" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
