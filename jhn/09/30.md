# that you do not know where he is from

The man is surprised that the Jewish leaders question Jesus' authority when they know he has the power to heal. AT: "that you do not know where he gets his authority" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# does not listen to sinners ... listens to him

"does not answer the prayers of sinners ... God answers his prayers"
