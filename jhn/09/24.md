## translationNotes

* **they called the man** - the Jews ([[:en:bible:notes:jhn:09:16]]) called the man
* **this man** - This refers to Jesus.
* **that man** - the man who had been blind
* **Whether he is a sinner, I do not know.** - "I do not know if he is a sinner or not."
