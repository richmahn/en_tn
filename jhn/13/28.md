# that he should give something to the poor

You can translate this as a direct quote: "Go and give some money to the poor."

# he went out immediately. It was night

John seems to draw attention here to the fact that Judas will do his evil or "dark" deed in the darkness of the night. AT: "he went out immediately into the dark night" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-background]])
