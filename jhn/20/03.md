## translationNotes

* Mary had just told Peter and John that someone had moved Jesus' body.
* **the other disciple** - John apparently expresses his humility by referring to himself in this manner rather than stating his name.
* **linen cloths** - These were the burial cloths used to wrap the body of Jesus.
