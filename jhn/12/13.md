# Hosanna

This means "May God save us now!"

# Blessed

This expresses a desire for God to cause good things to happen to a person.

# comes in the name of the Lord

Here the word "name" is a metonym for the person's authority and power. Alternate translation: "comes as the representative of the Lord" or "comes in the power of the Lord" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
