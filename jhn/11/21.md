# my brother would not have died

Lazarus was the younger brother. AT: "my younger brother would still be alive" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# Your brother will rise again

Lazarus was the younger brother. AT: "Your younger brother will become alive again" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
