## the light is not in him ##

Possible meaning: 1) "he cannot see"(UDB) or "he has no light"

## Our friend Lazarus has fallen asleep ##

Lazarus is dead, but it is only for a short time. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## but I am going so that I may wake him out of sleep ##

Jesus is revealing his plan to bring Lazurus back to life. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
