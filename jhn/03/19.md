## so that his deeds might not be exposed ##

AT: "so that the light would not show the things he does" or "so that the light does not make clear his deeds" (See:  [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## his deeds may be clearly seen ##

AT: "people may clearly see his deeds" or "everyone may clearly see the things he does" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
