## translationNotes

* **A person cannot receive even one thing unless** - "Nobody has any power unless"
* **you yourselves** - This "you" is plural and refers to all the people John is talking to. Alternate Translation: "You all" or "All of you." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_pluralpronouns]])
* **I have been sent before him** - "God sent me to arrive first" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
