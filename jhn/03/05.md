## Truly, truly ##

You can translate it the same way you did in [John 3:3](./03.md).

## born of water and the Spirit ##

There are 3 possible meanings: 1) "baptised in water" or 2) "born physically" or 3) "born of the Holy Spirit" - AT: "born spiritually by the Holy Spirit" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
