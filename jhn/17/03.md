Jesus continues to pray

## that they should know you ##

"know" God by experience, rather than just "knowing" things about God. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## the work that you have given me to do -  ##

**"The work" refers to Jesus' entire earthly gospel ministry. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
