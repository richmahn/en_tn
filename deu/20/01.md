## translationNotes

* Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **When you march out to battle against your enemies** - "When you go out to fight in a war against your enemies"
* **and see horses, chariots** - If an army has lots of horses and chariots, that shows that the army is very strong. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **he who brought you up out of the land of Egypt** - "Yahweh who led you out from the land of Egypt"
