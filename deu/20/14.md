# the little ones

"the children"

# all its spoil

"all the valuable things"

# booty

These are the valuable things which people who win a battle take from the people they attacked.

# all the cities

Here "cities" represents the people. AT: "all the people who live in cities" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
