## translationNotes

* **may not belong to the assembly of Yahweh** - AT: "may not be a full member of the Israelite community"
* **as far as to the tenth generation of his descendants** - AT: "even after 10 generations of his descendants"
* **they did not meet you with bread and with water** - This was a way to welcome strangers and travelers. AT: "they did not welcome you by bringing you food and drink" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
