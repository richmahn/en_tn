* **You must not detest an Edomite** - AT: "Do not hate an Edomite"
* **for he is your fellow Israelite** - AT: "because he is your relative"
* **you must not abhor an Egyptian** - AT: "do not hate an Egyptian"
* **The descendants of the third generation that are born to them may belong to the assembly of Yahweh** - AT: "If an Edomite or an Egyptian comes to live in the Israelite community, his grandchildren may become full members of that community" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
