## your son ##

This refers to the children of the people of Israel.

## with a mighty hand ##

AT: "with his mighty power" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## and on all his house ##

AT: "and on all of his people"

## before our eyes ##

AT: "in our sight"

## might bring us in ##

Implying the Land of Canaan which God promised to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob.
