## You will consume ##

Israel is to eliminate all the people.

## Your eye will not pity them ##

AT: "You will not show them mercy" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## that will be a trap for you ##

This means worshiping other gods will lead the people of Israel to ruin like an animal caught in a hunter's trap. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
