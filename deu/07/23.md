## victory over them ##

"victory over the armies from the other nations"

## until they are destroyed ##

AT: "until you destroy them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## you will make their name perish from under heaven ##

This means the Israelites will completely destroy all the people from those nations and in the future no one will even remember them.

## stand before you ##

"stand against you"
