# that your eyes saw

Here "eyes" represent the whole person. Alternate translation: "that you saw" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# the mighty hand, and the outstretched arm

Here "a mighty hand" and "an outstretched arm" are metaphors for Yahweh's power. See how you translated this in [Deuteronomy 4:34](../04/34.md). Alternate translation: "and the mighty power" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
