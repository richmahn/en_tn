## If you say in your heart ##

AT: "If you say to yourself" or "If you say"

## how can I dispossess them? ##

Moses uses a question to emphasize that the people may feel afraid of the other nations. AT: "I cannot defeat them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## you will call to mind ##

"you must remember" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## that your eyes saw ##

AT: "that you saw" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## the mighty hand, and the display of power ##

AT: "and the mighty power"  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
