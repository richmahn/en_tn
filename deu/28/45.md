## All these curses will come on you and will pursue and overtake you until you are destroyed ##

Moses describes the curses as a person chasing and conquering the people. AT: "Yahweh will cause all these curses to happen to you until he destroys you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## to the voice of Yahweh your God ##

AT: "to what Yahweh your God said" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## his commandments and his statutes ##

These words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. AT: "all of his commandments" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
