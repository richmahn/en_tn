## curses, confusion, and rebukes ##

These words combined emphasize the distress that Yahweh will cause the people of Israel. AT: "disasters, fear, and frustration"

## until you are destroyed ##

AT: "until your enemies destroy you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## forsaken me ##

Here "me" may refer to Yahweh, rather than Moses.

## cling to you ##

AT: "remain on you"
