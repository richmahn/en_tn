## a nation ##

AT: "people from a nation" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## you will always be oppressed and crushed ##

The words "oppressed" and "crushed" mean basically the same thing. AT: "they will always oppress and crush you" or "they will oppress you continually" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## you will become insane by what you have to see happen ##

AT: "what you see will drive you mad"

## from which you cannot be cured ##

AT: "which no one will be able to cure" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
