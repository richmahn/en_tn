## cause your enemies who rise up against you to be struck down before you ##

AT: "cause you to defeat the armies that attack you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## but will flee before you seven ways ##

Their enemies will run away in fear and panic. AT: "but will scatter before you in various directions"

## Yahweh will command the blessing to come on you in your barns ##

AT: "Yahweh will bless your crops so you have plenty to store in your barns" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## and in all that you put your hand to ##

AT: "and in everything you do"
