## translationNotes

* **cause your enemies who rise up against you to be struck down before you** - AT: "cause you to defeat the armies that attack you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **but will flee before you seven ways** - Their enemies will run away in fear and panic. AT: "but will scatter before you in various directions"
* **Yahweh will command the blessing to come on you in your barns** - AT: "Yahweh will bless your crops so you have plenty to store in your barns" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **and in all that you put your hand to** - AT: "and in everything you do"
