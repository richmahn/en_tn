## translationNotes

* **a proverb, and a byword, among all the peoples where Yahweh will lead you away** - The words "proverb" and "byword" mean basically the same thing. This can be translated as a new sentence. AT: "Yahweh will send you to peoples who will laugh at you and ridicule you"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
