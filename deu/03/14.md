This continues the background information about the land the people of Israel captured. (See: [Deuteronomy 3:12](./12.md))

## Jair ##

This is the name of a man. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## the Geshurites and the Maacathites ##

These are people groups who lived west of Bashan. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Havvoth Jair ##

Translators may make a footnote that says: "The name 'Havvoth Jair' means 'tent villages of Jair' or 'realm of Jair.'"
