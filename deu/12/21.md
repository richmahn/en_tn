Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

## chooses to put his name there ##

This refers to the place Yahweh would choose for the people to come worship him and offer sacrifices. AT: "chooses to dwell" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## within your gates ##

AT: "within your community" or "inside your city" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## as your soul desires ##

AT: "as you desire"

## Like the gazelle and the deer are eaten ##

AT: "Just like you eat the gazelle and the deer" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
