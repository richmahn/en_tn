## translationNotes

* Moses is still talking to the people of Israel.
* **and you will break down their altars** - AT: "you must pull apart the altars of those nations" or "you must destroy the altars of those nations"
* **dash in pieces** - AT: "break into pieces" or "shatter"
* **you will cut down** - "you must chop down"
* **destroy their name** - AT: "get rid of the memory of these false gods" or "destroy anything that represents these false gods" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **that place** - This refers to each place where the nations worshiped their gods.
* **You will not worship Yahweh your God like that** - AT: "You should not worship Yahweh your God like those nations worshiped their gods"
