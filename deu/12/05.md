## translationNotes

* **But to the place** - God will it make clear where they are to worship.
* **God will choose out of all your tribes** - From the territory divided between the tribes of Israel, Yahweh would choose one location for worship.
* **that will be the place where he lives** - AT: "he will make his dwelling there"
* **it is there that you will go** - They will go to worship where God decides.
* **your offerings for vows, your freewill offerings** - These are types of offerings. AT: "your offerings to fulfill a vow, your voluntary offerings"
* **the firstborn of your herds and flocks** - God requires that every firstborn male be set apart for him.
