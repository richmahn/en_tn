Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

## you will eat them ##

"you will eat your offerings"

## before Yahweh ##

AT: "in the presence of Yahweh"

## within your gate ##

AT: “inside your city” (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## everything that you put your hand to ##

AT: "all you undertake" or "all that you do" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## Pay attention to yourself  ##

AT: "Be careful"

## that you do not forsake ##

Moses uses a negative comment to emphasize that the people must take care of the Levites. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])
