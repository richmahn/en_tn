## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **you will eat them** - "you will eat your offerings"
* **before Yahweh** - AT: "in the presence of Yahweh"
* **within your gate** - AT: “inside your city” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **everything that you put your hand to** - AT: "all you undertake" or "all that you do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Pay attention to yourself ** - AT: "Be careful"
* **that you do not forsake** - Moses uses a negative comment to emphasize that the people must take care of the Levites. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
