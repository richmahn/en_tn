## translationNotes

* Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **If there is a dispute between men** - AT: "If there is a disagreement between 2 men"
* **If the guilty man deserves to be beaten** - AT: "If the guilty man deserves a beating as a punishment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **and be beaten in his presence** - AT: "and watch him get a beating" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **with the ordered number of blows, as was his crime** - AT: "with the number of hits appropriate for his crime"
