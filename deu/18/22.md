# General Information:

Yahweh continues speaking.

# a prophet speaks in the name of Yahweh

Here "name of Yahweh" refers to Yahweh himself and his authority. AT: "a prophet claims to speak for me" or "a prophet claims to speak with my authority" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# the prophet has spoken it arrogantly

"the prophet has spoken this message without my authority"
