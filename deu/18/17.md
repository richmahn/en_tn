## translationNotes

* **from among their brothers** - "from among their fellow Israelites"
* **put my words in his mouth** - AT: "tell him the message I want him to speak" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **speak to them** - "speak to the people of Israel"
* **he speaks in my name** - AT: "he speaks with my authority"
* **require it of him** - Here "him" refers to the person who does not listen to the prophet. AT: "I will hold him accountable" or "I will punish him"
