* **comes from any of your city gates out of all Israel** - "comes from any of your cities in Israel"
* **comes with all the desire of his soul** - AT: "comes with a strong desire within him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **his patrimony** - This is what the priest would inherit from his father.
