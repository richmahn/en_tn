# Deuteronomy 33 General Notes #

#### Structure and formatting ####

Some translations set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the poetic lines of Moses' blessing in 33:2-29.

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Families #####
Moses gives a series of prophecies or instructions for each of the tribes of Israel and Israel overall. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/prophet]])
