## translationNotes

* Moses continues blessing each tribe of Israel.
* **dip his foot in olive oil** - This is a sign of blessing and prosperity. Moses prays that the people of Asher will have so many olive trees that they can produce enough oil to dip their feet in.
* **May your city bars be iron and bronze** - Cities had large bars across their gates to keep enemies out. Moses prays that they will be safe from enemy attacks.
