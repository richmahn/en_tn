## Yahweh...rose from Seir upon them. He shined out from Mount Paran ##

Moses compares Yahweh to the rising sun. This emphasizes the brightness of his appearance. (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## upon them ##

"upon the people of Israel"

## ten thousands of holy ones ##

"10,000 angels" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## In his right hand were flashes of lightning ##

AT: "Fire flashed from his right hand"
