Moses continues blessing each tribe of Israel.

## About Joseph ##

This refers to the tribe of Ephraim and the tribe of Manasseh. Both tribes descended from Joseph.

## May his land be blessed by Yahweh ##

AT: "May Yahweh bless their land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## With the precious things of heaven, with the dew ##

AT: "with the precious dew from the sky" or "with the precious rain from the sky"

## the deep that lies beneath ##

This refers to the water under the ground.
