## translationNotes

* Moses continues blessing each tribe of Israel.
* **About Joseph** - This refers to the tribe of Ephraim and the tribe of Manasseh. Both tribes descended from Joseph.
* **May his land be blessed by Yahweh** - AT: "May Yahweh bless their land" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **With the precious things of heaven, with the dew** - AT: "with the precious dew from the sky" or "with the precious rain from the sky"
* **the deep that lies beneath** - This refers to the water under the ground.
