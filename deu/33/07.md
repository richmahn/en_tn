# General Information:

Moses continues to bless the tribes of Israel, which he began to do in [Deuteronomy 33:2](../33/02.md). The blessings are short poems. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

# to the voice of Judah

Here "voice of Judah" refers to the cries and prayers of the people of Judah. Alternate translation: "when the people of Judah pray to you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# be a help

The abstract noun "help" can be translated as a verb. Alternate translation: "help him to fight" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
