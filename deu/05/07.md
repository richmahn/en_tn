# General Information:

Moses continues reminding the people what Yahweh has commanded them. The phrases "you will" and "you will not" are commands. He speaks to the Israelites as if they were one person. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

# You will have no other gods before me

"You must not worship any other gods but me"

# that is in the earth beneath, or that is in the water below

This can be made more explicit. AT: "that is in the earth beneath your feet, or that is in the water below the earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
