# General Information:

Moses continues to remind the people of Israel what happened in the past.

# heard the voice

The word "voice" is a metonym for the sound of the voice or for the person who was speaking. AT: "heard the sound of the voice" or "heard Yahweh speaking" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
