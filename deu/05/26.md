# For who besides us is there ... have done?

This question can be translated as a statement. Alternate translation: "There are no other people besides us ... have done." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

# all flesh

This represents all people or living things. Alternate translation: "all people" or "all living beings" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
