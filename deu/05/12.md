## translationNotes

* **to keep it holy** - This shows that the Sabbath day is dedicated to God.
* **do all your work** - "do all your usual duties"
* **In it you will not** - "During it you will not"
* **within your gates** - Cities often had walls around them to keep out enemies, and gates for people to go in and out. AT: "within your community" or "inside your city" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
