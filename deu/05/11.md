* **take the name of Yahweh** - "use the name of Yahweh"
* **in vain** - AT: "carelessly" or "without proper respect" or "for wrong purposes"
* **Yahweh will not hold him guiltless** - AT: "Yahweh will certainly consider him guilty" or "Yahweh will certainly punish him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
