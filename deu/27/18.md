## May the man be cursed ##

AT: "May Yahweh curse the man" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## fatherless ##

children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them

## widow ##

is a woman whose husband has died and has no children to care for her in her old age
