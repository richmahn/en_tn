# These tribes

Here the metonym "tribes" refers to the people from the tribes of Simeon, Levi, Judah, Issachar, Joseph, and Benjamin. Alternate translation: "The people from these tribes" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# Mount Gerizim

A small mountain north of Mount Ebal. See how you translated this in [Deuteronomy 11:29](../11/29.md).

# Joseph

This combines the tribes of Ephraim and Manasseh, who were descendants of Joseph.
