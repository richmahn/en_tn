## I command ##

Here "I" refers to Moses. The elders are there in agreement with Moses, but he is the only one speaking.

## plaster them with plaster ##

Plaster is a paste that goes on wet and dries to form a smooth surface, suitable for writing upon. AT: "spread plaster on them" or "spread a white substance on them"
