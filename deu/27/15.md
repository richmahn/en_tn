## May the man be cursed ##

This is the statement the Levites must shout to all the people of Israel. AT: "May Yahweh curse the person" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## the work of the hands of a craftsman ##

AT: "something a man made himself" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
