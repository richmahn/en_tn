## I have led you ##

Yahweh is speaking to the people of Israel.

## forty years ##

"40 years" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## You did not eat any bread nor drink any wine or alcoholic drinks ##

Instead of eating and drinking these things, Yahweh provided food and drink for the people through his miraculous powers. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
