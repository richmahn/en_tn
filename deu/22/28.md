# but who is not engaged

"but whose parents have not promised another man that she will marry him"

# lies with her

This is a euphemism. Alternate translation: "has sexual relations with her" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])

# if they are discovered

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "if somebody finds out what happened" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
