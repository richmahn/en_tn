# General Information:

Moses is still speaking to the people of Israel.

# must take proof of her virginity

The abstract nouns "proof" and "virginity" can be translated with verbal phrases. AT: "must take something that proves that she has never had sexual relations" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
