## translationNotes

* Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **If there arises among you** - AT: "If there appears among you" or "If someone among you claims to be"
* **a dreamer of dreams** - This is someone who receives messages from God through dreams.
* **a sign or a wonder** - This refers to a prophecy or miracle that demonstrates God's power.
* **comes about** - AT: "takes place" or "happens"
* **of which he spoke to you and said, 'Let us go after other gods, that you have not known, and let us worship them** - This could be translated as an indirect quote. AT: "and he tells you to serve and worship other gods which you have not known" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_quotations]])
* **love Yahweh your God with all your heart and with all your soul** - AT: “earnestly love Yahweh your God” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
