## translationNotes

* **stork...heron, the hoopoe** - These are types of birds that eat rodents and lizards. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
* **the bat** - animal with wings and furry body which is awake mainly at night and eats insects and rodents
* **all winged, swarming things** - This a a ban on flying insects that move in large groups.
* **they must not be eaten** - AT: "you must not eat them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
