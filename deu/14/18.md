## stork...heron, the hoopoe ##

These are types of birds that eat rodents and lizards. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])

## the bat ##

animal with wings and furry body which is awake mainly at night and eats insects and rodents

## all winged, swarming things ##

This a a ban on flying insects that move in large groups.

## they must not be eaten ##

AT: "you must not eat them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
