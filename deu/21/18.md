## translationNotes

* Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **who will not obey the voice of his father** - AT: "who will not obey his father" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **even though they correct him** - AT: "even though his father and his mother discipline him"
* **must lay hold on him** - "must take hold of him" or "must grab him"
