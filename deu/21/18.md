# General Information:

Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.

# who will not obey the voice of his father or the voice of his mother

Here "voice" is a metonym for what a person says. Alternate translation: "who will not obey what his father or mother say" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# they correct him

"they punish him for his wrongdoing" or "they train and instruct him"
