# running water

This is an idiom for water that is moving. Alternate translation: "a stream" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# a valley that has been neither plowed nor sown

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "a valley where nobody has plowed the ground or planted seeds" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
