# who led you ... in the end

Moses continues to remind the Israelites of what they know about Yahweh ([Deuteronomy 8:14](../08/14.md)). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-distinguish]])

# who led ... who brought ... He fed

# to do you good
