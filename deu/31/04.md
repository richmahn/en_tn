## translationNotes

* **he did to Sihon and to Og** - AT: "he did to Sihon and Og and their armies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **to Sihon and to Og, the kings of the Amorites** - See how you translated these names in [[en:bible:notes:deu:01:03]].
* **Be strong and of good courage** - "Be strong and courageous"
* **fear not, and do not be afraid of them** - Moses repeats this for emphasis. AT: "and do not fear them at all" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **he will not fail you** - AT: "he will always fulfill his promise to you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
