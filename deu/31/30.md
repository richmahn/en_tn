# Moses recited in the ears of all the assembly of Israel

Here "ears" refers to the whole person. Alternate translation: "Moses recited to all the people of Israel" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# recited

Possible meanings are 1) "sang" or 2) "spoke."

# the words of this song

You can make clear the understood information. Alternate translation: "the words of the song that Yahweh taught him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
