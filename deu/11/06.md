Moses continues to remind the adults to teach their children God's mighty deeds.

## Dathan and Abiram, the sons of Eliab ##

Moses is referring to an event in the past when Dathan and Abiram rebelled against Moses and Aaron. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## earth opened its mouth and swallowed them up ##

This means the land split open and the people fell in. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])
