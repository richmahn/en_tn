## translationNotes

* Moses continues speaking to the judges.
* **small and the great alike** - These are the rich and poor, the important and the common people. All are to be treated fairly. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
* **you must not be afraid of the face of man** - AT: "you must not be afraid of anyone"
* **at that time** - AT: "when we were at Horeb" or "when we were at Mount Sinai" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
