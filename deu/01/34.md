## he swore and said ##

God made a vow not to allow those who rebelled against him to enter the land He promised to give them.

## will see ##

"will enter"

## save Caleb ##

"except Caleb"

This is the name of Caleb's father. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
