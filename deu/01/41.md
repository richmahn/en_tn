# General Information:

Moses continues reminding the people of Israel what the previous generation of Israelites did.

# sinned against Yahweh

"we have rebelled against Yahweh by disobeying him"

# we will follow

"we will obey"

# to attack the hill country

Here the metonym "hill country" represents the people that live there. Alternate translation: "to attack the people who live in the hill country" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
