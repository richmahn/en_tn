## within your city gates ##

AT: “within your towns”  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## fatherless ##

children whose parents have both died and do not have relatives to care for them

## widows ##

a woman whose husband has died and has no children to care for her in her old age

## call to mind ##

AT: "remember" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
