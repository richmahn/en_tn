## translationNotes

* **The parents must not be put to death for their children** - AT: "You must not put a parent to death because of something bad that one of his children did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **neither must the children be put to death for their parents** - AT: "and you must not put a child to death because of something bad that one of his parents did" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **everyone must be put to death for his own sin** - AT: "you should only put a person to death because of something bad that he did himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
