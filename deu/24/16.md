## The parents must not be put to death for their children ##

AT: "You must not put a parent to death because of something bad that one of his children did" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## neither must the children be put to death for their parents ##

AT: "and you must not put a child to death because of something bad that one of his parents did" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## everyone must be put to death for his own sin ##

AT: "you should only put a person to death because of something bad that he did himself" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
