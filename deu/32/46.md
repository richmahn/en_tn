## translationNotes

* **He said to them** - "Moses said to the people of Israel"
* **Fix your mind on** - AT: “Pay attention to” or “Think about” (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **that I have witnessed to you today** - AT: "that I am giving today as a declaration of truth to you"
* **your children** - "your children and descendants"
* **this is no trivial matter for you** - AT: "this is not a matter for you to ignore" or "the law is not just empty words" or "you must pay attention to what the law says" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
* **because it is your life** - AT: "because you will live if you obey it"
