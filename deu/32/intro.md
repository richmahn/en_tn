# Deuteronomy 32 General Notes #

#### Structure and formatting ####

Some translations prefer to set apart extended quotations, prayers or songs. The ULB and many other English translations indent the lines of 32:1-43, which is a song.

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Punishment #####
This chapter prophesies a time when Israel will be disobedient to Yahweh and he will be forced to punish them. This is meant to serve as a warning to the nation. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/prophet]])
