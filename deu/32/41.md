## translationNotes

* Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. Moses continues to use parallelism to emphasize what he is saying. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **When I sharpen my glittering sword** - This means God is preparing to judge and punish his enemies. AT: "When I sharpen my shining sword" or "When I am ready to judge my enemies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **when my hand begins to bring justice** - AT: "when I begin to carry out justice" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
