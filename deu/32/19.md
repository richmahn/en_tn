## translationNotes

* Moses speaks a poetic song to the people of Israel. Moses continues to use parallelism to emphasize what he is saying. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **his sons and his daughters** - This refers to the people of Israel whom Yahweh gave life to and made into a nation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **I will hide my face from them** - AT: "I will turn away from them" or "I will stop helping them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **I will see what their end will be** - "I will see what happens to them"
