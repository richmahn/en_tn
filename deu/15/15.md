## translationNotes

* **remember that you were a slave** - The "you" includes their ancestors that were slaves for many years. AT: "remember that your people were once slaves" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **then you must take an awl and thrust it through his ear to a door** - AT: "then you will place his head near a wooden door frame in your house, and then put the point of the awl through his earlobe into the wood"
* **awl** - a sharp, pointed tool used to make a hole
* **forever** - AT: “until the end of his life” or “until he dies”
