## translationNotes

* Moses continues reminding the people of Israel about what happened in the past.
* **At that time** - AT: "After I finished praying"
* **like the first** - Yahweh is referring to the first tablets that Moses had broken.
* **on the mountain** - This is Mount Sinai.
* **chest of wood** - AT: "wooden ark" or "wooden box"
