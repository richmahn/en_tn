## translationNotes

* **heaven...earth** - These words are combined to mean that all things everywhere belong to Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
* **the heaven of heavens** - This refers to the highest heavens where Yahweh dwells.
