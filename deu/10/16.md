## translationNotes

* **circumcise the foreskin of your heart** - Moses is referring to a spiritual circumcision. This means the people must remove the sin from their lives. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **God of gods** - AT: "the supreme God" or "the only true God"
* **Lord of lords** - AT: "the supreme Lord" or "the greatest Lord"
* **the fearsome one** - AT: "the one who causes people to fear"
