## circumcise the foreskin of your heart ##

Moses is referring to a spiritual circumcision. This means the people must remove the sin from their lives. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## God of gods ##

AT: "the supreme God" or "the only true God"

## Lord of lords ##

AT: "the supreme Lord" or "the greatest Lord"

## the fearsome one ##

AT: "the one who causes people to fear"
