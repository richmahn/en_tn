God continues to describe His active support of Esau conquering the land Esau is now inhabiting.

## Avvim...Caphtorim ##

These are the names of people groups. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Caphtor ##

This is the name of a land. It may be another name for the island of Crete located in the Mediterranean Sea. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
