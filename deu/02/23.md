## translationNotes

* God continues to describe His active support of Esau conquering the land Esau is now inhabiting.
* **Avvim...Caphtorim** - These are the names of people groups. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Caphtor** - This is the name of a land. It may be another name for the island of Crete located in the Mediterranean Sea. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **destroyed them** - "destroyed the Avvim"
