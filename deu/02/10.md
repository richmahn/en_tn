## The Emim lived...call them the Emim ##

Here 2:10-11 gives background information about the Emim people who had lived in the land before the Moabites. Your language may have a special way to mark background information. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-background]])

## Emim...Rephaim ##

These are the names of people groups that were considered giants. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
