## I sent ##

Here "I" refers to Moses.

## wilderness of Kedemoth ##

This is the name of an area near the valley of Arnon. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Sihon...Hesbon ##

See how you translated these in [Deuteronomy 1:3](../01/03.md).

## with words of peace ##

AT: "with a message that asked for peace"

## I will turn neither to the right hand nor to the left ##

AT: "I will not change direction" or "I will always stay on the path" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])
