## Who will go over the sea for us and bring it to us and make us to hear it, so that we may do it? ##

AT: “Someone must travel across the sea to learn God's commands and then return and tell us what they are” (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## in your mouth and your heart ##

These words mean basically the same thing. They emphasize that the people already know God's commands and can speak them. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
