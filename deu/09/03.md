## translationNotes

* Moses continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **like a devouring fire** - This emphasizes God's power and his ability to destroy the armies of the other nations. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **he will bring them down before you** - AT: "he will humble them in front of you" or "he will subdue them in front of you"
