# General Information:

Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel if they were one man, so all instances of "you" and "your" are singular. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

# your fathers

This refers to Abraham, Isaac, Jacob, and Jacob's sons.

# with his presence, with his great power

"with the great power that comes from his presence" or "with his great power"
