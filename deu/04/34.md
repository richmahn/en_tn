# General Information:

Moses continues to speak to the people of Israel.

# by a mighty hand, and by an outstretched arm

Here "a mighty hand" and "an outstretched arm" are metaphors for Yahweh's power. AT: "by showing his mighty power" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# before your eyes

Here "eyes" refer to the whole person. AT: "in front of you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
