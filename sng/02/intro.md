# Song of Solomon 02 General Notes #

#### Important figures of speech in this chapter ####

##### Metaphor #####
Women are compared to flowers in this chapter. This metaphor may describe a woman's beauty and delicacy. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

##### Euphemisms #####
It is possible that some of the metaphors used in this chapter are actually euphemisms. These euphemisms would refer to sex or the physical love between a husband and a wife.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
