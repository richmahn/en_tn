## translationNotes

* **watchmen** - the men who have the job of keeping guard of the town at night to keep the people safe
* **as they were making their rounds in the city** - "who were going through the city" or "who were walking around the city"
* **bedroom** - "the room for sleeping"
* **the one who had conceived me** - - "the one who had become pregnant with me" or "the one who carried me in her belly." This refers to her mother.
