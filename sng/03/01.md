## translationNotes

* **I was longing for the one whom my soul loves; I was longing for him** - This is repeated for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **was longing for** - "was wanting to be with" or "was looking for"
* **the one whom my soul loves** - See how you translated "whom my soul loves" in [[en:bible:notes:sng:01:07]].
* **go through the city** - "walk through the city"
* **through the streets and squares ** - The word "squares" indicates the center area of a town where streets or roads come together. It is often an area where people sell items, a market, and a place where people come together to talk.
* **search** - "to look for"
