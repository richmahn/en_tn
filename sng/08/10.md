## my breasts are now like fortress towers ##

The woman's breasts are tall like towers. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## so I am completely mature in his eyes ##

AT: "so he sees that I am completely mature" or "so he understands that I am completely mature." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## in his eyes ##

The word "his" refers to the woman's lover.
