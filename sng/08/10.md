## translationNotes

* **my breasts are now like fortress towers** - The woman's breasts are tall like towers. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **so I am completely mature in his eyes** - AT: "so he sees that I am completely mature" or "so he understands that I am completely mature." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **in his eyes** - The word "his" refers to the woman's lover.
