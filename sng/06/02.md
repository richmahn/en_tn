* **My beloved gone down to his garden** - See the explanation of this image in [[en:bible:notes:sng:05:01]].
* **to the beds of spices** - See how you translated "beds of spices" in [[en:bible:notes:sng:05:13]]. The beloved has come to enjoy the physical attractions of the woman which are wonderful like spices. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **to graze in the garden and to gather lilies** - The woman uses these images to indicate that her beloved is delighting in her. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
