## translationNotes

* The lover finishes speaking to himself.
* **grove** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:sng:04:12]].
* **young growth** - "young plants" or "new shoots"
* **had budded** - "had grown their buds." Buds are the small round parts of plants which open up into flowers.
* **were in bloom** - "were opening their flowers"
* **I was so happy that I felt was riding in the chariot of a prince** - The lover uses this image to express how happy he is. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
