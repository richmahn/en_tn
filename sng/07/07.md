The lover describes what he would like to do with the one he loves.

## Your height is like that of a date palm tree ##

"You stand up like a date palm tree." The woman is tall, stands straight, and is attractive just like a date palm. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## date palm tree ##

a tall, straight tree that produces a sweet, brown, and sticky fruit that grows in groups

## your breasts like clusters of fruit ##

The dates on a palm tree grow in very large and beautiful bunches that hang down from the tree.

## I want to climb...its branches ##

The man wants to hold the woman. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## May your breasts be like clusters of grapes ##

The man wants to touch her breasts that are firm but soft just like grapes that are full of their juice.

## may the fragrance of your nose be like apricots ##

AT: "may the breath coming from your nose smell sweet like apricots."

## apricots ##

The word "apricots" refers to tasty, yellow-colored, sweet pieces of fruit.
