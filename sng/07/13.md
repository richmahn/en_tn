## translationNotes

* The woman continues to speak to her beloved.
* **mandrakes** - This is the name of plants that give off a strong but pleasant scent. The scent is slightly intoxicating and stimulating which increases the desire to make love. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **give off their fragrance** - "produce their scent"
* **at the door** - It is implied that the doors belong to their house. AT: "above the entrances of our house" or "by the doors of our house." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **are all sorts of choice fruits, new and old** - "is every kind of the best fruit, both old fruit and new fruit"
* **stored up for you** - "saved for you" or "protected for you"
