## By what authority do you do these things ##

The words "these things" refer to Jesus turning over the seller's tables in the temple and speaking against the things they did and taught. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## By what authority do you do these things, and who gave you the authority to do them ##

AT: "You do not have authority to do these things because we have not given you authority." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
