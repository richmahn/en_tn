# Connecting Statement:

Jesus teaches in the synagogue of the town of Capernaum on the Sabbath. By sending a demon out of a man he amazes the people in all the nearby area around Galilee.

# came into Capernaum

"arrived at Capernaum"

# as someone who has authority and not as the scribes

There are words left out in this statement that can be added. AT: "as someone who has authority taught and not as the scribes taught" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
