# The Sabbath was made for mankind

Jesus makes clear why God established the Sabbath. This can be stated in active form. AT: "God made the Sabbath for mankind" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# mankind

"man" or "people" or "the needs of people." This word here refers to both men and women. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-gendernotations]])

# not mankind for the Sabbath

The words "was made" are understood from the previous phrase. They can be repeated here. AT: "mankind was not made for the Sabbath" or "God did not make mankind for the Sabbath" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
