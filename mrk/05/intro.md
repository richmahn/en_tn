# Mark 05 General Notes #

#### Possible translation difficulties in this chapter ####

##### "Talitha, koum" #####
The words "Talitha, koum" ([Mark 5:41](./41.md)) are from the Aramaic language. Mark writes them the way they sound and then translates them.
