# Mark 06 General Notes #

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### "Anointed with oil" #####
In the ancient Near East, people would anoint or smear oil on sick people so that they might be healed.

#### Important figures of speech in this chapter ####

##### Rhetorical Questions #####
Mark uses many rhetorical questions in this chapter to show that people did not understand who Jesus was. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
