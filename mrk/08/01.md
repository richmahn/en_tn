## three days ##

"3 days" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## they may faint ##

Possible meanings: 1) "they may lose consciousness temporarily" or 2) "they may become weak" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])

## Where can we get enough loaves of bread in such a deserted place to satisfy these people? ##

The disciples are expressing surprise that Jesus would expect them to be able to find enough food. AT: "This place is so deserted that there is no place here for us to get enough loaves of bread to satisfy these people!" (UDB) (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
