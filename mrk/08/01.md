## translationNotes

* **three days** - "3 days" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
* **they may faint** - Possible meanings: 1) "they may lose consciousness temporarily" or 2) "they may become weak" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
* **Where can we get enough loaves of bread in such a deserted place to satisfy these people?** - The disciples are expressing surprise that Jesus would expect them to be able to find enough food. AT: "This place is so deserted that there is no place here for us to get enough loaves of bread to satisfy these people!" (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
