# the most important of all ... The most important is

"The most important" refer to the most important commandment. Alternate translation: "the most important commandment of all ... The most important commandment says" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-nominaladj]])

# Hear, Israel, the Lord our God, the Lord is one

"Listen, O Israel! The Lord our God is one Lord"
