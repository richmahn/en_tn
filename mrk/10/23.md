## It is easier for a camel to go through a needle's eye, than for a rich person to enter the kingdom of God. ##

It is impossible for a camel to go through the eye of a needle. It is almost as difficult[1]for rich people to decide to let God rule their lives." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])

## eye of a needle ##

The "eye of a needle" is the hole at the top of a needle.
