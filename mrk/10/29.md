## translationNotes

* **there is no one who has left ….who will not receive** - "anyone who has left…will receive." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
* **for my sake** - "for my benefit" or "for my gain"
* **this world** - "this life" or "this present age"
* **the world to come** - "the life to come" or "the age to come"
