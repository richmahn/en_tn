# He is risen!

The angel is emphatically stating that Jesus has risen from the dead. This can be translated in active form. AT: "He arose!" or "God raised him from the dead!" or "He raised himself from the dead!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
