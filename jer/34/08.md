## This is the word that came to Jeremiah from Yahweh ##

After this phrase, the reader will of course expct to see the content of the message from Yahweh. However, the actual message does not begin until [Jeremiah 34:12](./12.md). For this reason, the translator may choose to omit this phrase here, and to follow the example of the UDB.

## word ##

"message" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
