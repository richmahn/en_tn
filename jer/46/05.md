Jeremiah's vision for Egypt continues.

## What am I seeing here?-  ##

**  The word "I" here  refer to Yahweh. Yahweh asks this sarcastic question to emphasize what he has said regarding the nation of Egypt has come true.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## The swift cannot run away, and the soldiers cannot escape. ##

These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. AT: "even the swiftest soldier cannot escape." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
