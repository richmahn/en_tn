## for Egypt  ##

This phrase indicates this message is intended for the nation of Egypt.

## carchemish ##

Carchemish is a city on the west bank of the Euphrates.

## harness ##

This means to ready a horse for pulling a chariot: a two wheeled wagon of war.

## helmet ##

This is armor which protects the head in battle.

## polish the spears ##

This phrase means to "sharpen" the spear.
