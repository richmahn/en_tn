* Yahweh continues speaking about the people of Judah.
* **wormwood** - a plant that tastes bitter
* **Then I will scatter them among the nations** - "Then I will force them to leave here and live in many different countries"
* **I will send out a sword after them** - AT: "I will send an army of soldiers to fight against them." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
