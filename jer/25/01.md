## translationNotes

* **the fourth year of Jehoiakim** - After Jehoiakim ruled for 3 years but before he finished his forth (4) year as king.
* **the first year of Nebuchadnezzar** - During the first year of Nebuchadnezzar's reign.
* **all the people of Judah and all the inhabitants of Jerusalem** - These were the last of the Israelites. The northern kingdom had already fallen. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
