## translationNotes

* **have been completed** - AT: "when their punishment is complete"
* **make it an unending desolation** - God vows to turn Babylon into a desert like wilderness.
* **for their deeds and the works of their hands** - God will cause them to receive the same punishment they inflicted on the nations they conquered. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
