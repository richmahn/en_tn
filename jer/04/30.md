## you dress in scarlet... jewelry... eyes... with paint ##

"you dress like prostitutes"

## adorn ##

"decorate" or "add extras to make something appear more attractive or expensive"

##  men who lusted for you now reject you ##

This emphasizes that other nations Israel has depended on for wealth and business will reject them when they see God's judgment.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## distress as in the birth ##

"distress as in the pain and suffering a woman has while giving birth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## my heart is weary ##

"my life is worn out from grief" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
