## translationNotes

* **This is the word that came to Jeremiah from Yahweh** - "This is the message that Yahweh sent to Jeremiah"
* **this word** - the message that Yahweh is just about to give to Jeremiah
* **Stand...Say** - Yahweh is giving these commands to Jeremiah.
* **to worship Yahweh** - "in order to worship Yahweh"
