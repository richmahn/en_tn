## translationNotes

* **do not pray for this people, and do not lift up a lamenting wail or say a prayer on their behalf, and do not petition me** - These four clauses say the same thing. AT: "do not pray to me asking me to bless these people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **lift up a lamenting wail** - "cry out with sadness"
* **on their behalf** - "for their benefit" or "in their interest"
* **do not petition me** - "do not ask me"
* **will provoke me** - "will anger me"
