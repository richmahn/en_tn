## they have deluded you ##

AT: "the prophets have caused you to believe something that is not true"

## visions from their own minds ##

AT: "visions they imagined" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## from Yahweh's mouth ##

AT: "from Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## Yet who has stood in Yahweh's council meeting? Who sees and hears his word? Who pays attention to his word and listens? ##

AT: "No one advises Yahweh. No one understands what Yahweh says. No one obeys Yahweh's commands." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
