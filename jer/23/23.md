## Am I only a God nearby... and not also far away? ##

AT : "I am a God that is both near and far away." (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Can anyone hide in a secret place so I cannot see him? ##

AT: "No one can hide in a secret place so I cannot see him." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Do I not fill the heavens and the earth? ##

AT: "I am everywhere, both in the heavens and the earth." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
