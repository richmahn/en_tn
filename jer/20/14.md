# General Information:

Jeremiah continues speaking to Yahweh.

# Let the day when I was born be cursed

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Curse the day I was born" or "Let people curse the day when I was born" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# Do not let the day that my mother bore me be blessed

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Do not bless the day that my mother bore me" or "Do not let people bless the day that my mother bore me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# Let the man who informed my father be cursed

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Curse the man who informed my father" or "Let people curse the man who informed my father" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
