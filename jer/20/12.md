## who see the mind and the heart ##

AT: "who knows what people think and desire" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## rescued the soul of the oppressed person ##

AT: "rescued the oppressed person" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## from the hand of evildoers ##

Here the word hand refers to power. AT: "from the power of evildoers" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
