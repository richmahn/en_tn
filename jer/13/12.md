## translationNotes

* **fill with drunkenness every inhabitant of this land** - AT: "cause all the people of this land to be drunk"
* **the kings who sit on David's throne** - "the kings of the nation of Judah"
* **smash each man against the other, fathers and children together** - AT: "each person will fight the other; even parents and children will fight against each other"
* **I will not pity** - "I will not feel sorry for"
* **will not spare them from destruction** - AT: "will not withhold punishment" or "will allow them to be destroyed" or "will allow them to face destruction"
