## Concerning the house of the king of Judah, listen to the word of Yahweh ##

AT: "Listen to what Yahweh says about the king of Judah, his family, and his servants."

## Bring about justice in the morning ##

AT: "Always treat the people whom you rule over fairly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the oppressor's hand ##

AT: "the power of the one who oppresses" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## my fury will go out like fire and burn ##

AT: "I will punish and destroy you in my anger quickly and completely" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
