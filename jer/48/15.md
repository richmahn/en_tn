## translationNotes

* **its finest young men have gone down to the place of slaughter.** - AT: "Their fine young men will all be slaughtered." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Moab's disaster is soon to happen; calamity is hurrying quickly** - These two sentences mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **the strong staff, the honored rod** - These two phrases are referring to Moab. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]], [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
