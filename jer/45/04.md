## translationNotes

* **This is what you must say to him** - Yahweh is telling Jeremiah what to say to Baruch.
* **This is true over all the earth** - AT: "This will happen over the entire earth"
* **But are you hoping for great things for yourself?** - AT: "You do hope for great things for yourself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **For see** - AT: "Be aware"
* **plunder** - things stolen from a place by force
* **your life as your plunder** - AT: "I will allow you to live. That will be the best you can hope for." See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
