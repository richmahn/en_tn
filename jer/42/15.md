## translationNotes

* **the sword that you fear will overtake you** - AT: "You will experience the terrible results of war there." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **The famine that you are worrying about will pursue you to Egypt** - Famine is referred to as something that can pursue something else. AT: "You worry about famine in Israel but if you go to Egypt you will suffer from famine there" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **all the men** - The men are used to refer to all the people because they are the leaders in their families. AT: "everyone" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
