# they went

"Johanan, his companions, and all the people they rescued went"

# Geruth Kimham

This is the name of a place. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# because of the Chaldeans

It is implied that they were afraid that the Chaldeans would attack them. AT: "because they thought the Chaldeans may attack them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# in charge of the land

Here "land" represents the people. AT: "in charge of the people of Judah" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
