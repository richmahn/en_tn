## translationNotes

* **Yahweh brought it about. He did just as he had decreed** - AT: "Yahweh did what he told you he would do" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **since you people sinned against him and did not obey his voice** - AT: "since you Judeans sinned against Yahweh and did not obey his command" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **this thing has happened** - "this disaster has happened"
* **But now look!** - Nebuzaradan says this to bring Jeremiah's attention to the present moment.
* **it is good in your eyes** - AT: "you want to"
