## translationNotes

* **In the ninth year and tenth month Zedekiah king of Judah** - "When Zedekiah had been king of Judah for 9 years and 10 months" ( See:  [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimeyear]] and [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimemonth]])
* **In the eleventh year and fourth month of Zedekiah, on the ninth day of the month** - "When Zedekiah had been king for 11 years, 4 months, and 9 days" (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicaltimeday]])
* **Nergal Sharezer, Samgar Nebo, and Sarsechim** - These are the names of men. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **in the middle gate** - "in the middle entrance to the city." It was common for leaders to sit at the gate of the city to discuss important matters.
