## translationNotes

* **They went out at night from the city by king's garden path** - "They left the city at night going out on a path in the kings's garden"
* **the plains of the Jordan River valley** - This is the flat land at the southern end of the valley.
* **at Riblah in the land of Hamath** - Riblah was a town in the territory of Hamath. They are in Syria. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **pursued them and overtook** - "followed and caught"
* **passed sentence on him** - "decided how to punish him"
