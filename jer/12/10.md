## They have stomped all over ##

"they have crushed under their foot" or "they have destroyed"

## my portion of land ##

"the ground I planted"

## They have made her ##

The word "her" refers to the Promised Land (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## she is desolate ##

The word "she" refers to the Promised Land

## takes it to heart ##

"cares" or "pays any attention" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
