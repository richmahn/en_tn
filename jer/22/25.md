# I have given you to the hand of the ones seeking your life

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "I have made it possible for the ones seeking your life to capture you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# to the hand of

Here the metonym "hand" represents power and control. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# seeking your life

This phrase represents wanting or trying to kill someone. See how this phrase is translated in [Jeremiah 11:21](../11/21.md). Alternate translation: "trying to kill you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
