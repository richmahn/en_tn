## translationNotes

* **Pour your fury on the nations** - AT: "Punish the people of the nations severely" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **devoured Jacob and consumed him ** - "devour" and "consume" are two words with the same meaning used to emphasize or intensify the idea expressed by these two words.(See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) AT: "they are completely destroying us people of Israel" (UDB)
* **destroy him and demolish his habitation** - "destroy" and "demolish" are two words with the same meaning used to emphasize or intensify the idea expressed by these two words.(See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]]) AT: "destroy all our cities and villages"
