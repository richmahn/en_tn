## translationNotes

* **This is the word of Yahweh that came to Jeremiah and said** - "This is what Yahweh said to Jeremiah"
* **Is anything too difficult for me to do?"** - Yahweh uses a question to emphasize that he can do anything. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **over into the hand of the Chaldeans** - Here "hand" refers to power or control. AT: "to the Chaldeans" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
