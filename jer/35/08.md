# the voice of Jonadab

The word "voice" is a metonym for the command that Jonadab spoke. AT: "the command of Jonadab" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# all of our days

"as long as we live." "Days" refers to the span of time.
