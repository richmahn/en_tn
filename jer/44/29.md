## my words will certainly attack you with disaster ##

AT: "what I have said will happen and you will have a disaster." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## I am about to give over Pharaoh Hophra, king of Egypt, into the hand of his enemies ##

AT: "I will allow Pharaoh Hophra's enemies to capture him"  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## Hophra ##

This is the name of a man. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## and the ones seeking his life ##

AT: "and the the people who want to kill him." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
