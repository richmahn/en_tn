## sword, famine, and plague ##

The word "sword" is used to refer to war. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## fugitive or survivor ##

Both of these words refer to the same people.  A fugitive is someone who escapes from someone who wants to kill them or take them captive. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
