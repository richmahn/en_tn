## translationNotes

* **sword, famine, and plague** - The word "sword" is used to refer to war. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **fugitive or survivor** - Both of these words refer to the same people.  A fugitive is someone who escapes from someone who wants to kill them or take them captive. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
