## translationNotes

* **See, I have sworn by my great name, says Yahweh** - Yahweh's "great name" refers to his whole being and reputation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **Every person of Judah ... will perish** - AT: "A very large number of the people from Judah ... will perish" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
* **by sword and famine** - The "sword" represents those who will die in battle and "famine" represents those who will die of starvation. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
