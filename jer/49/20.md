## the inhabitants of Teman ##

the people who live in Teman

## They will certainly be dragged away, even the smallest flock ##

AT: "He will drag them away, even the smallest flock" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## Their pasturelands will be turned into ruined places ##

AT: "He will turn their pasturelands into ruined places" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## pastureland ##

a piece of land covered with grass where animals feed on the grass.
