## translationNotes

* Jeremiah is now speaking to the people of Edom.
* **I have heard** - The word "I" refers to Jeremiah.
* **attack her** - The word "her" refers to Edom.
* **I have made** - The word "I" refers to Yahweh.
* **you** - The word "you" refers to the nation of Edom.
