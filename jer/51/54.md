## translationNotes

* **A shout of distress came from Babylon, a great collapse from the land of the Chaldeans** - The same thought is expressed in two different ways for emphasis.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **enemies roar like the waves of many waters** - The sound of the enemies coming will be very loud. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **her warriors have been captured** - This phrase can be changed to have an active verb. AT: "they captured her warriors." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
