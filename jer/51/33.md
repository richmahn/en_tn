## The daughter of Babylon ##

"Daughter of Babylon" is another name for the residents of Babylon. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## is like a threshing floor ##

Yahweh compares threshing to the time of great pressure upon Babylon. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## time of harvest will come to her ##

The harvest is the result of the actions that the people did before. The result of Babylon's sins will come. AT: "Babylon will be punished."  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
