## translationNotes

* This begins Yahweh's reply to Jerusalem's request in verses 34 and 35.
* **heaps of rubble** - This refers to the buildings becoming piles of trash.
* **den of jackals** - AT: "a home for wild dogs"
* **a horror** - Something that terrifies anybody looking at it. AT: "a terrible place to live"
* **hissing** - This is a sound that shows strong disapproval.
