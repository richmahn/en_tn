## translationNotes

* Jeremiah continues to speak the words of Yahweh.
* **The objects belonging to Yahweh's house are being returned from Babylon now!** - AT: "People from Babylon are bringing back all the gold items that they took from Yahweh's temple!"
* **Why should this city become a ruin?** - AT: "This entire city will be destroyed." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **If they are prophets...** - If what they speak is true, then they should be praying that my words do not come to pass and that the temple objects and leaders remain in Jerusalem.
