## Jehoiakim son of Josiah ##

Some translations use Zedekiah instead of Jehoiakim.

## fetters ##

This is something to keep a person from moving freely.

## Then send them out ##

Jeremiah was to send a set of fetters and yoke to each king listed.

## Give commands to them for their masters ##

Jeremiah was instructed to give a set of chains and yoke to each ambassador and a message for each king regarding the chains and yoke.
