## by my great strength and my raised arm  ##

Both phrases have a similar meaning and are used together to emphasize Yahweh's power. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## I give it to anyone who is right in my eyes  ##

"I give it to anyone I want to."

## subdue him ##

will become strong and defeat Babylon.
