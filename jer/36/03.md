# Perhaps the people

"It is possible that the people"

# to bring on them

"to cause to happen to them"

# his wicked way

Yahweh speaks of a person's lifestyle as if it were a "way" or path along which the person walks. See how you translated this in [Jeremiah 18:11](../18/11.md). Alternate translation: "his wicked way of living" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# forgive their iniquity and their sin

The words "iniquity" and "sin" are metonyms for the persons who commit iniquity and sin. Alternate translation: "forgive them for their iniquity and their sin" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
