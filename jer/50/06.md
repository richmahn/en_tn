## lost flock ##

This compares the people of Israel to a group of sheep that are lost. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## devoured them ##

This compares destroying and taking everything from them to eating eagerly. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## they sinned ##

The word "they" refers to the people of Israel.
