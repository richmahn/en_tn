Yahweh continues telling how to destroy the people of Babylon and Chaldea.

## Kill all her bulls ##

The young warriors are compared to bulls because of their strength. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## her bulls ##

The word "her" refers to Babylon.

## them, their ##

The word "them" and "their" refer to the people of Babylon.

## those, these ##

The word "those" and "these" refer to the survivors from Babylon who will tell others about Yahweh's vengeance.
