## translationNotes

* **Who are you** - Possible meanings are 1) Naomi did not recognize Ruth because it was still dark or 2) this is an idiom that means "How did it go?" or "What happened?" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **do not go empty** - "do not go empty-handed" or "do not go with nothing"
* **finished this thing** - This refers to the decision about who will buy Naomi's property and marry Ruth.
