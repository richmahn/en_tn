## translationNotes

* **gate** - "gate of the city" or "gate of Bethlehem." This was the main entrance to the walled town of Bethlehem. There was an open area by the gate which was used as a meeting place to discuss community matters.
* **called to him by name** - Boaz knew his name but the narrator choose not to use it.
* **elders of the city** - "leaders of the city"
