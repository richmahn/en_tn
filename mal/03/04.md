Yahweh is speaking to the people of Israel.

## as in the days of old, and as in ancient years ##

AT: "as it was in the past" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## draw near ##

"come close"

## oppress the hired worker in his wages ##

"do not pay the hired worker the wages due him"

## turn away the foreigner from his rights ##

deny foreigners living in the community the rights that the local people have
