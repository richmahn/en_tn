# They will be mine

"They will be my people"

# my own treasured possession

Here "possession" refers to one's personal property. This idea can be expressed with a verb. Alternate translation: "they will belong completely to me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

# I act

the time when Yahweh will judge and punish the rebellious Israelites, giving victory to the faithful Israelites
