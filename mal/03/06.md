## translationNotes

* Yahweh is speaking to the people of Israel.
* **From the days of your fathers you have turned aside from my ordinances and have not kept them** - "You have disobeyed my commands ever since the days of your ancestors"
* **"How will we return?"** - "We have never gone away from you, so we cannot return to you." (See UDB)
