## translationNotes

* **you** - “you” refers to the believers in the Thessalonian Church. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **Silvanus** - "Silvanus" is the Latin form of "Silas". He is the same person called Silas who travelled with Paul in the book of Acts.
