## we  ##

refers to Paul, Silas and Timothy but not the Thessalonians. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]]).

## you ##

refer to Thessalonians believers (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## some walk idly ##

"some live idle lives" or "some are lazy"

## with quietness ##

"in a quiet, peaceful, mild manner"
