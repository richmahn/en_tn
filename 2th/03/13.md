## translationNotes

* **But ** - <b>But <b>- "but" contrasts between the lazy believers to the hard working believers.
* **you** - this refers to the Thessalonians believers. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **do not lose heart** - This is an idiom for "do not be discouraged". (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **take note of him** - "publicly identify that person" (UDB).
