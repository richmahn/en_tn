## Then the lawless one will be revealed ##

"Then God will reveal the lawless one". The lawless one is another name for the Antichrist.

## breath of his mouth ##

"by the power of his spoken word"

##  bring to nothing by the revelation of his coming ##

Jesus will destroy the lawless one when he appears at his return.

## The lawless one's coming will be due to the work of Satan ##

Satan will enable the Lawless one to act in all power, signs, and false wonders.
