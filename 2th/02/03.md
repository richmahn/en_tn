## deceive you ##

refers to the Thessalonian believers. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## it will not come ##

"the Day of the Lord will not come"

## and the man of lawlessness is revealed ##

"and God will expose the man of lawlessness." (see: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## the son of destruction ##

"the one who destroys everything he can" or "the Destroyer." He will do his father's bidding, that is, Satan.

## all that is called God or is worshiped ##

"everything that people worship"
