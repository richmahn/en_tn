# Connecting Statement:

The writer ends the list of cities whose elders received gifts from David that began in [1 Samuel 30:27](./26.md). You may be able to combine the list items from [1 Samuel 30:27-31](./26.md) into one list.

# Rakal ... Hormah ... Bor Ashan ... Athak

These are the names of towns. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Jerahmeelites ... Kenites
