## Epaphroditus ##

This is the name of a man sent by the Philippian church to minister to Paul in prison. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## fellow worker and fellow soldier ##

Here “soldier” is comparing a person who fights physical battles with a believer who fights spiritual battles. Paul is emphasizing that a believer has to struggle against evil in order to spread the gospel. AT: "fellow believer who works and struggles along with us" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## and your messenger and minister to my need ##

AT: "and who brings your messages to me and helps me when I am in need"

## he was deeply distressed and longed to be with you all ##

AT: "he was very worried and wanted to be with you all"

## sorrow upon sorrow ##

AT: "sorrow added to the sorrow I already have from being in prison" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
