## translationNotes

* **Have this mind in you, which was also in Christ Jesus** - Here "mind" refers to a person's attitude or how they think. AT: "Have the same attitude as Christ Jesus" or "Think about things the way Christ Jesus did" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
