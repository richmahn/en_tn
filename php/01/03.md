## I thank...I pray...I give thanks ##

Here "I" refers to Paul. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-singularpronouns]])

## you ##

Here "you" refers to the Philippian believers. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## I give thanks for your fellowship in the gospel  ##

Paul is expressing thanks to God that the Philippians are also preaching the gospel. AT: "I give thanks to God for you proclaiming the gospel"

## I am confident ##

"I am sure"

## he who began ##

"the God who began" or "God who began"

## will complete it ##

"will continue to complete it"
