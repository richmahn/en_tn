# a tenth of an ephah

An ephah is 22 liters. A tenth of an ephah is about two liters. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])

# a tenth

This is one part out of ten equal parts. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-fraction]])
