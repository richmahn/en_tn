## translationWords

* [[en:tw:unclean]]

## translationNotes

* **He will require** - Here "he" refers to the priest.
* **all the inside walls of the house to be scraped** - AT: "that the owner scrapes all the inside walls" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **the contaminated material that is scraped off** - "the material with mildew on it"
* **the stones that were removed** - AT: "the stones that they removed"
* **they must use new clay to plaster the house** - "they must cover the stones with new clay"
