# He is clean

Here "He" refers to both men and women in general. AT: "That person is clean" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-gendernotations]])

# He is clean

The person whom other people may touch is spoken of as if he were physically clean. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
