## translationNotes

* **anything in which leather is used** - AT: "anything in which a person uses the leather" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **anything in which the harmful mildew is found** - AT: "anything on which the priest found the harmful mildew"
* **it can lead to disease** - The harmful mildew could cause disease in a person who comes in contact with the item.
* **the item must be completely burned up** - AT: "He must burn the item"
