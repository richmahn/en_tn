## after it was washed ##

AT: "after the owner washed it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## You must burn ##

Here "you" does not mean Moses or Aaron specifically. It just means someone must burn the the item.

## if you wash the item  ##

"if the owner washes it"

## then the item must be washed ##

AT: "then the owner must wash it"
