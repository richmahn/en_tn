## translationNotes

* **to the priest to be sacrificed** - AT: "to the priest so he may sacrifice them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **instead of offering sacrifices in an open field** - Yahweh wants to prevent the people from offering sacrifices out in the fields to false gods.
