## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
* **You must keep my commandments and carry them out** - The words "keep" and "carry out" mean basically the same thing. They emphasize that the people must obey God. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **must surely be put to death** - AT: "you must surely put to death" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
