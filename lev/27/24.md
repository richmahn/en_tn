## translationNotes

* **to the man from whom it was bought, to the land's owner** - AT: "to the land's original owner from whom the man bought it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **All the estimated values must be set** - AT: "The priests must determine the estimated values"
* **by the weight of the sanctuary shekel** - This refers to the official value of the shekel.
* **shekel** - See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]]
* **Twenty gerahs** - "20 gerahs." This equals one shekel. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]] and [[en:obe:other:biblicalweight]])
