## translationNotes

* **Only the firstborn among animals, because the firstborn already belongs to Yahweh, no man can sanctify it** - "No one may set apart the firstborn among animals, since the firstborn already belongs to Yahweh"
* **and a fifth must be added to that value** - AT: "and he must add a fifth to that value" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **If the animal is not redeemed, then it is to be sold at the set value** - AT: "If the person does not buy back the animal, then the priest may sell it at the set value"
