## nothing dedicated to Yahweh by a man from anything he has, whether human or animal, or his family land, may be sold or redeemed. Every dedicated thing is holy to Yahweh ##

AT: "if someone dedicates anything he has to Yahweh, whether a human, an animal, or his family land, no one may sell it or redeem it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## No person who is set apart to be destroyed from among the population may be ransomed ##

"If Yahweh determines that a person should die because of his sin, no one may pay to set him free" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
