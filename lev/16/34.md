# General Information:

Yahweh finishes telling Moses what the people must do on the Day of Atonement.

# This was done as Yahweh commanded Moses

This can be translated in active form. AT: "And Moses did as Yahweh commanded" or "And Aaron did as Yahweh commanded Moses" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
