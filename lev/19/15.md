## Do not cause judgment to be false ##

"Do not judge unfairly." AT: "Judge everyone fairly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

## You must not show favoritism to someone because he is poor, and you must not show favoritism to someone because he is important ##

The words "poor" and "important" can be combined to mean "anyone." AT: "You must not show favoritism to anyone" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])

## judge your neighbor righteously ##

"judge everyone according to what is right"

## Do not walk around spreading false gossip ##

"Do not go around speaking gossip to other people"

## seek to protect your neighbor's life ##

"seek to protect a person's life by telling the truth"
