## Do not use false measures ##

"Do not be dishonest"

## ephah ##

This was a measurement for grain. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/other/biblicalweight]])

## hin ##

This was a measurement for liquids. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/other/biblicalweight]])

## must obey...and do them ##

These mean the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
