* **Do not use false measures** - "Do not be dishonest"
* **ephah** - This was a measurement for grain. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalweight]])
* **hin** - This was a measurement for liquids. (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalweight]])
