# General Information:

God gives Moses instructions about the things in the tent of meeting.

# pure oil beaten from olives

"pure olive oil"

# the lamp

This refers to the lamp or lamps in Yahweh's sacred tent. This can be stated clearly. AT: "the lamp in the tent of meeting" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
