* **walk against me** - "to rebel against me" or "to oppose me"
* **I will walk against you** - "I will oppose you"
* **I will punish you even seven more times as much for your sins** - AT: "I will continue to increase your punishment for your sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
