## If you do these things ##

"If you break my covenant and disobey my commands"

## I will set my face against you ##

"I will turn my face away from you." This means Yahweh will reject the Israelites. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## you will be overpowered by your enemies ##

AT: "your enemies will defeat you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
