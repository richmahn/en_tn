## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.
* **so that it may be accepted before Yahweh** - AT: "so that Yahweh will accept it" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **lay his hand on the head** - This is a sign of dedicating the animal to God.
* **then it will be accepted on his behalf to make atonement for himself** - AT: "then Yahweh will accept it in his place and forgive his sins"
