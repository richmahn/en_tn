* **They must not** - "Priests must not"
* **You must sanctify him** - "You people must treat the priest as holy"
* **for he offers the "food" from your God** - AT: "for he burns the food offering on Yahweh's altar"
* **He must be holy in your sight** - AT: "You must regard him as holy"
* **She must be burned** - AT: "You must burn her to death" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
