# The person presenting

"The person who offers"

# for the purpose of giving thanks

The abstract noun "thanks" can be stated as a verb. AT: "for the purpose of thanking Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

# the meat must be eaten ... it may be eaten

This can be translated in active form. AT: "he must eat the meat ... he may eat it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
