## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.
* **You will become unclean until evening by these animals if you touch a carcass of one of them** - AT: "You will be unclean until sunset if you touch the dead body of one of these animals" (See:[[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **these animals** - This refers to the animals he is about to list in the following verses.
