# General Information:

Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron which animals the people are to consider unclean.

# is to be detested

This can be translated in active form. AT: "you must detest" or "you must reject" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# it must not be eaten

This can be translated in active form. AT: "you must not eat it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
