## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.
* **fins** - the thin, flat part which the fish uses to move through the water
* **scales** - the small plates that cover the body of the fish
* **they must be detested by you** - AT: "you must detest them" or "you must hate them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
