Yahweh continues telling Moses and Aaron what the people can eat and must avoid eating.

## must be detested by you ##

AT: "you must detest" (UDB) or "you must hate" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## locusts...katydids...crickets...grasshoppers ##

These are small insects that eat plants and can jump high. (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])
