# Yahweh has commanded him not to be done

All of the people of Israel were commanded not to sin. This can be stated in active form. AT: "Yahweh commanded the people not to do" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# his sin which he has committed is made known to him

This can be stated in active form. AT: "he becomes aware of the sin he committed" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
