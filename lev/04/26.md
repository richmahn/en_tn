# He will burn

"The priest will burn"

# The priest will make atonement for the ruler

The abstract noun "atonement" can be stated as a verb. Alternate translation: "The priest will atone for the ruler" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

# the ruler will be forgiven

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "Yahweh will forgive the ruler's sins" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
