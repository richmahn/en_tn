## made from two tenths of an ephah. They must be made from fine flour and baked with yeast ##

AT: "which you must make from two tenths of an ephah of flour and then bake it with yeast" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## two tenths of an ephah ##

This represents approximately 4.5 liters.

## that will be raised and presented ##

AT: "that the priest will raise and present"
