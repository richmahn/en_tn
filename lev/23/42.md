# General Information:

Yahweh continues his instructions for the Festival of Shelters.

# your descendants, generation after generation, may learn

"Generation after generation" is an idiom that refers to each generation that lives after another. AT: "your descendants belonging to all future generations may learn" or "all your descendants may learn forever" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
