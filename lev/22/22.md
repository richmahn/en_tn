# disabled, or maimed

These words refer to defects caused by accidents.

# warts, sores, or scabs

These refer to types of skin diseases.

# will not be accepted

This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will not accept" or "Yahweh will not accept" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# deformed or small

These words refer to defects the animal has from birth.
