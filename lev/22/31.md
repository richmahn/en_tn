## keep my commandments and carry them out ##

The words "keep" and "carry out" mean the same thing. They emphasize that the people must obey God's commands. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## You must not dishonor my holy name ##

AT: "You must honor my holy reputation" or "You must honor me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## I must be acknowledged as holy by the people of Israel ##

AT: "The people of Israel must acknowledge me as holy" (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
