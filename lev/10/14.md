## translationNotes

* **The breast that is raised as an offering and the thigh that is presented to Yahweh** - AT: "The breast and thigh that a person raised and presented to Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **for they are given as your share** - AT: "for Yahweh has given them as your share"
* **you and your sons and daughters** - Here "you" refers to Aaron. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **they must bring those together** - "those giving the offering must bring the thigh and breast together"
* **They will be yours and your sons' with you as a share forever** - "This portion will always be for you and your sons"
