# the offerings to Yahweh made by fire

This can be translated in active form. AT: "the burnt offerings to Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# for it is most holy

"for the grain offering is most holy"

# this is what I have been commanded to tell you

This can be translated in active form. AT: "this is what Yahweh commanded me to tell you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
