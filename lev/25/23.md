## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues speaking.
* **The land must not be sold to a new  permanent owner** - AT: "You may not sell your land permanently to another person" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **the right of redemption** - The original owners were legally allowed to buy back any of their land that they sold.
* **you must allow the land to be bought back by the family from whom you bought it** - AT: "you must allow the family from whom you bought the land to buy the land back"
