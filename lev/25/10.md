# fiftieth year

This is an ordinal number. AT: "year 50" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# a Jubilee for you

The Jubilee was a year when the Jews had to return land to its' original owners and set slaves free. AT: "a year of restoration for you" or "a year for you to return land and free slaves"

# property and slaves must be returned

This can be stated in active form. AT: "you must return property and slaves" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
