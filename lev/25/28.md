# the year of Jubilee

"the year of restoration" or "the year to return land." See how you translated it in [Leviticus 25:10](../25/10.md).

# the land will be returned

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "the one who had bought it will return the land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# will return to his property

"will go back to his land"
