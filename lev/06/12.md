# The fire on the altar will be kept burning

This can be translated in active form. AT: "The priest will keep the fire on the altar burning" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# as required on it

This can be stated in active form. AT: "on it as Yahweh demands" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
