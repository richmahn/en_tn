# He cut the ram

"Moses cut the ram"

# produced a sweet aroma

Yahweh being pleased with the sincere worshiper who offered the sacrifice is spoken of as if God were pleased with the aroma of the burning sacrifice. See how you translated a similar phrase in [Leviticus 1:9](../01/07.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# an offering made by fire to Yahweh

This can be translated in active form. AT: "an offering he burned to Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
