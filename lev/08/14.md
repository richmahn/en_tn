## translationNotes

* **laid their hands on** - This is a sign of dedicating the animal to God so the animal will die for their sin instead of them.
* **horns of the altar** - See how this was translated in [[en:bible:notes:lev:04:06]]. This refers to the corners of the altar which are shaped like the horns of an ox.
* **set it apart for God** - "set the altar apart for God"
