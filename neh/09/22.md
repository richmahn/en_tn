In these verses, the Levites continue to praise Yahweh in the presence of the people of Israel.

## You gave them kingdoms ##

Here "you" refers to Yahweh, and "them" refers to the Israelites at the time of Moses.

## you assigned them land in every distant corner ##

"you gave them possession of land in every corner"

## Sihon...Og ##

These are the names of kings. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Heshbon...Bashan ##

These are names of places. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
