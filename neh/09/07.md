# Connecting Statement:

The Levites continue their prayer before all the people.

# Ur of the Chaldees

"Ur, where the Chaldean people group lived"

# You found his heart was faithful before you

The heart, the inner being of the person, represents the person. AT: "You saw that he was completely faithful to you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# Canaanites ... Hittites ... Amorites ... Perizzites ... Jebusites ... Girgashites

people group names (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
