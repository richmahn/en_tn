In these verses, the Levites continue to praise Yahweh in the presence of the people of Israel.

## commandments and statutes and a law ##

Three words are used for emphasis to describe the same thing: God's Law or The Ten Commandments. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## to go in to possess the land ##

"to enter the land of Israel" or "to enter the Promised Land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
