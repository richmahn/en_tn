## translationNotes

* In these verses, the Levites continue to praise Yahweh in the presence of the people of Israel.
* **Now therefore, our God, the great, the mighty, and the awesome God, who keeps his covenant and steadfast love** - "Now, therefore, our God, you are great, mighty, and awesome, and you keep your covenant and steadfast love."
* **do not let all this hardship seem little to you that has come upon us...until today** - Two new sentences should appear here. "Do not let all this hardship seem little to you.  The hardship has come upon us...until today."
* **you are just in everything that has come on us** - AT: "you are just in sending us hardships"
* **and we have acted wickedly** - "but we have acted wickedly"
