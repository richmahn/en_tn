## translationNotes

* **I also took my place** - Nehemiah is speaking here. AT: "I, Nehemiah, also took my place"
* **Eliakim, Maaseiah, Miniamin, Micaiah, Elioenai, Zechariah...Hananiah, Maaseiah, Shemaiah, Eleazar, Uzzi, Jehohanan, Malchijah, Elam, and Ezer** - These are the names of males who were the priests at that time. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Jezrahiah** - This is the name of a male who was the leader of the singers.
* **The singers sang with Jezrahiah as director** - AT: "The singers and Jezrahiah the director proclaimed"
