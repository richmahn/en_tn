## In the days of Joiakim these were the priests, the heads of fathers' families: Meraiah was the leader of Seraiah...Joseph was leader of Shebaniah ##

This means there was a representative relative for each family.

## Joiakim...Meraiah...Seraiah, Hananiah...Jeremiah, Meshullam...Ezra, Jehohanan...Amariah, Jonathan...Malluchi, Joseph...Shebaniah ##
