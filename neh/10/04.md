# General Information:

In these verses, Nehemiah lists the names of the priests who signed the sealed document. (See: [Nehemiah 10:1](../10/01.md))

# Hattush

This is a man's name. See how you translated this in [Nehemiah 3:10](../03/10.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Shebaniah ... Meshullam

These are the names of men. See how you translated this in [Nehemiah 9:4](../09/04.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Malluk ... Obadiah, Daniel, Ginnethon ... Abijah, Mijamin, Maaziah, Bilgai

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
