## Jeshua, Bani, Sherebiah, Jamin, Akkub, Shabbethai, Hodiah, Maaseiah, Kelita, Azariah, Jozabad, Hanan, Pelaiah ##

These are all male names. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## And they read in the book, in the law of God, making it clear with interpretation and giving the meaning so they understood the reading. ##

The sole purpose of the people was met in a very personal way. Levites answered questions and gave clarifying comments.
