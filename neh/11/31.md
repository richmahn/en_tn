## Geba...Michmash...Aija...Bethel...Anathoth, Nob, Ananiah, Hazor, Ramah, Gittaim, Hadid, Zeboim, Neballat, Lod...Ono...Judah ##

These are names of places. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## The people of Benjamin also lived from Geba onward, at Michmash and Aija ##

"The people of Benjamin lived in Geba, Michmash and Aija.

##  Aija ##

This is possibly another name for the town of Ai.

##  the valley of craftsmen ##

This valley was called "Ge-harashim" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

##  Some of the Levites who lived in Judah were assigned to the people of Benjamin ##

"Some of the Judean divisions of Levites were assigned to live with the people of Benjamin."
