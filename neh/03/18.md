# After him their countrymen repaired ... repaired another section

These phrases refer to repairing the wall. Alternate translation: "Next to him their countrymen repaired the wall ... repaired another section of the wall" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

# After him

"Next to him"

# Binnui ... Henadad ... Ezer ... Jeshua

These are names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Binnui son of Henadad, the ruler

Binnui was the ruler, not Henadad.

# Keilah ... Mizpah

These are names of places. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
