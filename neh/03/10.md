# Uzziel ... Harhaiah ... Hananiah ... Rephaiah ... Hur ... Jedaiah ... Harumaph ... Hattush ... Hashabneiah

These are names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# goldsmiths, repaired ... Hur repaired ... Harumaph repaired ... Hashabneiah repaired

These phrases refer to repairing the wall. Alternate translation: "goldsmiths, repaired the wall ... Hur repaired the wall ... Harumaph repaired the wall ... Hashabneiah repaired the wall" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
