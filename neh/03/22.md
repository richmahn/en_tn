## the priests...repaired ##

The implied information here is that the wall was being repaired. AT: "the priests fixed the wall" (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## Benjamin and Hasshub repaired ##

See above note for implied information. AT: "Benjamin and Hasshub fixed the wall"

## Benjamin...Hasshub...Azariah...Maaseiah...Ananiah...Binnui...Henadad...Azariah ##

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## buttress ##

"supporting corner" or "turning of the wall." The corner of a building where a supporting structure is.
