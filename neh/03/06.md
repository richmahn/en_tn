## Joiada...Paseah and Meshullam...Besodeia...Melatiah...Jadon ##

These are all names of males. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## they set its doors ##

"they installed its doors" or "they put its doors in place"

## its bolts and its bars ##

"its locking mechanisms" or "its locks"

## Melatiah the Gibeonite...Jadon the Meronothite ##

Gibeonites and Meronothites are people groups.  (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/other/peoplegroup]])

## Gibeon and Mizpah ##

These are names of places. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## the province beyond the River ##

"the province near the Euphrates River" or "the near province at the Euphrates River"
