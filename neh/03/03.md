## Hassenaah...Meremoth...Uriah...Hakkoz...Meshullam...Berechiah...Meshezabel...Zadok...Baana ##

These are names of men who rebuilt the walls of Jerusalem. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## they set its doors ##

"they installed its doors" or "they put its doors in place"

## its bolts and its bars ##

"its locking mechanisms" or "its locks"

## the Tekoites ##

These are workers from the town of Tekoa.

## their leaders refused to do the work ordered by their supervisors ##

The Hebrew word translated "supervisors" may have been intended to be a reference to the Lord their God. AT: "their noblemen refused to serve their Lord."
