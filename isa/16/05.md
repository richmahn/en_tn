## A throne will be established in covenant faithfulness ##

Here "throne" refers to the power to rule as king over the people. AT: "Yahweh will be faithful to the covenant and he will appoint a king." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## and one from David's tent will faithfully sit there ##

Here "David's tent" means the king will be a descendant of King David. AT: "and he will be a descendant of King David and he will be faithful"
