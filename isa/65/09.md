## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues speaking.
* **my mountains** - This refers to Jerusalem and all of Judea.
* **Sharon** - This was a fertile grazing area.
* **valley of Achor** - This is the name of a valley that possibly ran from Jerusalem to south of Jericho. This was also a fertile grazing area. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
