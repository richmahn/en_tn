## covenant faithfulness ##

See how you translated this in [Isaiah 16:5](../16/05.md).

## given to David ##

AT: "that I gave to David" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## I have placed him ##

Possible meanings are 1) this refers to what King David did in the past, or 2) it refers to what God will do through one of David's descendants.
