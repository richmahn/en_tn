## translationNotes

* The Lord is speaking to Isaiah
* **Make the heart of this people calloused** - "Make their minds dull"
* **and make their ears deaf, and blind their eyes** - "and make it so that they cannot hear, and make it so that they cannot see."
* **and then turn again** - "and follow me again" or "and then start trusting in me again"
* **and be healed** - AT: "and I would heal them." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
