## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues speaking.
* **A sound of battle tumult comes from the city, a sound from the temple** - AT: "a loud uproar like the noise of a battle comes from the city and from the temple" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **the sound of Yahweh paying back his enemies** - This refers to the sound of Yahweh punishing those who contaminated their worship with Canaanite practices.
