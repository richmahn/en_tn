## translationNotes

* **Now, what do I have here** - Yahweh uses a question to make the people pay attention to what he is about to say. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **declaration of Yahweh** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:isa:14:21]].
* **seeing that my people are taken away for nothing** - AT: "I see the enemy taking my people away without cause" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **my name is slandered continually all the day** - Here "name" refers to God's reputation. AT: "those who watch the enemy take away my people are continually saying evil things about me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
