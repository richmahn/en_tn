## The wilderness and the Arabah will be glad; and the desert will rejoice ##

The word "Arabah" means "a dry place." In 35:1-2 Isaiah describes these places as a person who is glad. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## the glory of Lebanon will be given to it, the splendor of Carmel and Sharon ##

AT: "Yahweh will make the desert as beautiful as Lebanon, Carmel, and Sharon" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
