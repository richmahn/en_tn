These verses begin a description of the glorious future for God's people.

## Then the lame man will leap like a deer ##

This means a person who could not walk will be able to jump even more than ordinary people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])
