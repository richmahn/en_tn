## dismayed and ashamed ##

AT: “afraid and put to shame”

## because of Ethiopia their hope and of Egypt their glory ##

AT: “because they had trusted in the power of the armies of Ethiopia and Egypt” (See UDB)

## The inhabitants of these coasts ##

the people who lived in the lands bordering the Mediterranean Sea

## where we fled for help to be delivered ##

"we fled to them so they could save us" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## and now, how can we escape? ##

AT: "Now there is no escape." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
