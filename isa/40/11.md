# He will feed his flock like a shepherd

The writer speaks of Yahweh's people as if they were sheep and of Yahweh as if he were their shepherd. AT: "He will care for his people like a shepherd feeds his flock" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
