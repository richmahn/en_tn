## On that day ##

This refers to the day when God does the things that are described in [Isaiah 10:15](./15.md).

## that has escaped ##

The meaning can be made explicit: "that has escaped from the army of Assyria. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## will no longer rely on him who struck them ##

"will no longer rely on the king of Assyria who harmed them"
