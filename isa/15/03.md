## translationNotes

* This continues the prophecy about the people of Moab. God describes events that will happen in the future as if it is happening in the present. (See: [[en:bible:notes:isa:15:01]])
* **Heshbon and Elealea** - This refers to "The people of Heshbon and Elealea." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Heshbon, Elealeh, Jahaz** - These are names of cities and towns. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **their souls tremble in them** - AT: “they will be completely filled with fear.” (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
