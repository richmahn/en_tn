## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **I have not spoken in secret** - AT: "I have spoken plainly and clearly" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
* **sent me** - Here "me" refers to an unknown servant of Yahweh, maybe Isaiah or Cyrus or the promised Messiah.
