## Holy One of Israel ##

See how you translated this in [Isaiah 1:4](../01/04.md).

## In returning and resting you will be saved ##

AT: "You will be saved if your return and rest" or "I will save you if you will return to me and rest in me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## in quietness and in trust will be your strength ##

"You will be strong if you are quiet and trust in me." Quietness here refers to not being anxious and worried. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
