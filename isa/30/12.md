## Holy One of Israel ##

See how you translated this in [Isaiah 1:4](../01/04.md).

## so this sin will be to you like a broken part...in an instant ##

This emphasizes that God will destroy the people of Judah suddenly because of their sin. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## like a broken part ready to fall ##

"like a broken part of a wall that is ready to fall"

## whose fall will happen suddenly, in an instant ##

"that will suddenly fall, in an instant"
