## but they do not notice ##

AT: "but the wicked people do not notice"

## But they will see your zeal for the people and be put to shame ##

AT: "But when they see your eagerness to bless your people, the wicked people will be ashamed"

## fire of your adversaries will devour them ##

AT: "your fire will devour them"

## for us ##

Here "us" refers to Isaiah and includes all the righteous people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])
