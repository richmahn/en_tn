## wild animals of the field will honor me, the jackals and the ostriches, ##

Animals honor Yahweh as if they were people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## the jackals and the ostriches ##

See how you translated the names of these animals in [Isaiah 13:21](../13/21.md).

## the jackals and the ostriches ##

These animals also honor Yahweh just as the animals in the field. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
