## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.
* **precious and special in my sight** - AT: "you are very precious to me" (UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **therefore I will give people in exchange for you, and other peoples in exchange for your life** - Both phrases mean the same thing. AT: "therefore I will let the enemy conquer other peoples instead of you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
