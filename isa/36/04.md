See how you translated this in [Isaiah 36:1](./01.md).

## said to them ##

"said to Eliakim, Shebna, and Joah"

## What is the source of your confidence? ##

The king of Assyria uses this question to challenge Hezekiah that he does not have a good source for confidence. AT: "You have no reliable source for your confidence." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Now in whom are you trusting? Who has give you courage to rebel against me? ##

The king of Assyria uses questions to ridicule Hezekiah for believing he has the strength to rebel. AT: "No matter in whom you trust, you will not have the courage to rebel against me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
