## there will be a highway ##

"there will be a large road"

## and the Assyrian ##

This refers to people from Assyria. AT: "and the Assyrian people." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## and the Egyptian to Assyria ##

The words "will come" are understood: "and the Egyptian people will come to Assyria." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
