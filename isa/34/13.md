* **thorns...nettles...thistles** - weeds with thorns. Nettles' thorns have poison that causes itching.
* **jackals…ostriches…wild animals…hyenas…Owls** - See how you translated these in [[en:bible:notes:isa:13:21]].
* **Nocturnal animals** - animals that are active at night
* **hawk** - a bird that eats 1) animals it has just killed or 2) animals that have been dead for some time
