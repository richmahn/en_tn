## Wild oxen will be slaughtered along with them, and young bull with the older ones ##

Possible meanings are 1) Yahweh will slaughter all the oxen and bulls along with the people or 2) this refers to the leaders of Edom that Yahweh will slaughter along with the common people like oxen and bulls.
