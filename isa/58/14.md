# General Information:

Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

# I will make you ride on the heights of the earth

This refers to God exalting the nation in reputation and power in response to living righteously. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# for the mouth of Yahweh has spoken

The word "mouth" symbolizes what Yahweh says. AT: "for Yahweh has spoken it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
