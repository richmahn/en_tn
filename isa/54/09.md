## and the hills be shaken ##

AT: "and the hills may shake" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## my covenant faithfulness will not turn away from you ##

Here, "covenant" refers to the promise made to Noah. AT: “I will not stop being faithful to you as I promised in my covenant” or ”I will always be faithful to you because of my covenant” (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

##  nor will my covenant of peace be shaken  ##

AT: “and I will not cancel my covenant of peace” or “and I will surely give you peace as I promised in my covenant”
