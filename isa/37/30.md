## translationNotes

* This continues Yahweh's message to Hezekiah. (See: [[en:bible:notes:isa:37:21]])
* **sign for you** - Here "you" is singular and refers to Hezekiah. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **you will eat...you must plant** - Here "you" is plural and refers to the people of Judah.
