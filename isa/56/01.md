## translationNotes

* **for my salvation is near, and my righteousness is about to be revealed.** - AT: "I will soon save you and show you that I am righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **whoever who holds it tightly** - AT: "whoever is careful to always do this" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **keeps his hand from doing any evil** - Here "hand" refers to the person's actions or behaviors. AT: "does not do evil things" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
