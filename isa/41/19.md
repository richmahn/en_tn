# General Information:

Yahweh continues speaking.

# the myrtle ... the pines and the cypress box trees

These are types of trees. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])

# the hand of Yahweh has done this

Here the word "hand" represents Yahweh himself. AT: "Yahweh has done this" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
