# General Information:

Yahweh continues speaking.

# not one among them

"not one idol"

# their cast metal figures are wind and emptiness

Yahweh speaks of the worthlessness of the idols as if the idols are wind and nothing at all. AT: "their idols are all worthless" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
