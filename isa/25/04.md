## your are a protection...a guardian...a refuge...a shade ##

Yahweh is compared to a place that the people can go to find safety and comfort. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## when the blast of the ruthless is like a storm...the ruthless ones will be put down ##

Those who try to harm the poor and needy will fail. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## the song of the ruthless ones will be put down ##

AT: "You will stop the song of the ruthless ones" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
