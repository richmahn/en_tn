Yahweh continues speaking to the people of Israel.

## those nations will be completely destroyed ##

AT: "I will completely destroy the people of those nations" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## The glory of Lebanon will come to you ##

This refers to Lebanon being known for its beautiful trees, especially cypress and cedar. The exact identification of all the trees is not known.

## the place of my feet ##

This refers to Yahweh's temple. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
