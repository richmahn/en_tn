## translationNotes

* **the land of Sinim** - This probably is a region in the south of Egypt near Aswan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Sing, heavens and be joyful, earth; break into singing, you mountains!** - Isaiah turns his attention from the people of Israel and speaks to the heavens, earth, and mountains as if they were people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]])
