## I said that halfway through my life I will go through the gates of Sheol ##

"I said that before I was old I was going to die." Also, 38:10 may be translated as questions. AT: "I said "will I die before I am old?'"

## I am sent there for the rest of my years ##

"before I have lived all of my years I will go to the grave." AT: "will I go to the grave before I have have lived all my years?"
