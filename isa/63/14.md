The people of Israel continue speaking.

## As the cattle that go down into the valley ##

This may refer to cattle going into a valley where there is green grass and water. This emphasizes that God led the people of Israel and took care of them. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## to make yourself a name of praise ##

Here "name of praise" refers to honor and one's reputation. AT: "so you would have an honored name" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
