## I have trod grapes ##

Here "I" refers to Yahweh. This imagery refers to Yahweh destroying his enemies. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## For I looked forward to the day of vengeance ##

Here "day" refers to a period of time. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## vengeance ##

Yahweh will punish in an appropriate way to bring about justice. His kind of vengeance differs from man's.
