Yahweh continues speaking.

## his ways ##

Here "his" refers to the people of Israel. AT: "their ways"

## comfort and console those who mourn for him ##

"I will soothe and calm those who feel sad for the people's sinful behavior"

## and I create the fruit of the lips ##

AT: "I cause them to praise and thank me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## Peace, peace to those who are far off ##

The word "peace" is repeated for emphasis. AT: "I have made peace with them"
