## virgin daughter of Babylon ##

The "virgin daughter" of a city means the attractive and delicate people of the city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## without a throne ##

Here "throne" refers to their power to rule. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
