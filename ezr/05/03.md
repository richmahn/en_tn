## Tattenai, Shethar Bozenai ##

These are names of people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## God's eye was on ##

A figure of speech to express God watching over. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## They were waiting for a decree to come from Darius ##

The Jews restarted building the temple while waiting for King Darius to state if they were to stop.
