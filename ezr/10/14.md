## translationNotes

* **Jonathan son of Asachel and Jachzeiah theson of Tikvah opposed this** - Possible meanings are, 1) these men opposed the delay, having the city officials investigate the offenses, or 2) they opposed the investigation. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Meshulam and Shabbetai the Levite supported them** - These men agreed with Jonathan and Asachel in their opposition. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
