## translationNotes

* **Funds from the king's tribute beyond the River** - King Darius supports the temple building from taxes collected from the nations east of the Euphrates River.
* **Do this so they will ... pray for...the king, and my sons.** - Darius has respect or does fear the God of the Jews.
