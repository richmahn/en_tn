# We ourselves heard this voice brought from heaven

With the word "We," Peter is referring to himself and to the disciples James and John, who heard the voice of God. Alternate translation: "We ourselves heard this voice that came from heaven" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])

# heard this voice brought from heaven

"heard the voice of the one who spoke from heaven"

# we were with him

"we were with Jesus"
