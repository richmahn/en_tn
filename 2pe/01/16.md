## For we did not follow cleverly invented myths ##

For we apostles did not follow cleverly made up stories. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])

## our  ##

all believers including us apostles. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])

## We heard this voice that came out of heaven ##

Peter is referring to himself and the other disciples, James and John, that heard the voice of God. See: ([[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## While we were with him on the holy mountain ##

Peter is referring to the time when Jesus glowed with bright light in front of Peter, James and John. (See Matthew [Matthew 17:1](../../mat/17/01.md)).
