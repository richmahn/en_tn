## translationNotes

* **Now, I write to you, beloved, this second letter to arouse your sincere mind** - Peter changes his discussion and now reminds his audience that this is his second letter to them to excite their faithful thinking and actions.
* **so that you may recall the words said before ** - The reason is to refresh their memories of the teachings by the apostles, the words of holy prophets and commands of Jesus.
