## translationNotes

* **all these things will be destroyed in this way** - "all things will be destroyed by fire"
* **what kind of people should you to be in respect to holy living and godliness?** - Peter challenges the  Believers to think how they should be living a holy and godly life knowing that the earth and the deeds of people will be judged. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **However, we expect the new heavens and the new earth that he has promised** - Believers have hope and can be sure that the new heaven and new earth that the Lord has promised will come.
