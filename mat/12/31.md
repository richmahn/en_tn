This continues an account of the Pharisees saying that it was by the power of Satan that Jesus healed a man.

## to you ##

to the Pharisees

## every sin and blasphemy will be forgiven to men ##

"God will forgive every sin and blasphemy men commit" or "God will forgive every person who sins or blasphemes" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## blasphemy against the Spirit will not be forgiven ##

"God will not forgive blasphemy against the Spirit"

## And whoever speaks any word against the Son of Man, that will be forgiven him ##

"God will forgive any word spoken against the Son of Man"

## this world...that which is to come ##

Alternate translation: "this time...that time which is coming."
