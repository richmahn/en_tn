* This account explains how the actions of Jesus fulfilled one of the prophecies of Isaiah.

* **withdrew from** - "left"
* **not to make him known to others** - "not to tell anyone else about him"
* **what had been said through Isaiah the prophet, saying** - "what God had said through what Isaiah the prophet had written"
