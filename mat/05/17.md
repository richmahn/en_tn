Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in [Matthew 5:1](./01.md).

## not one jot or one tittle ##

"not even the smallest written letter or the smallest part of a letter" or "not even the laws that seem unimportant" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## heaven and earth ##

"everything God has created" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])

## all things shall have been accomplished ##

"God has done everything that was written in the law." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
