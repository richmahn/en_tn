## translationNotes

* Jesus continues to tell a large crowd of people different parables that describe what the kingdom of God is like.
* **your...you** - Jesus is speaking to his disciples.
* **for they see** - "for they can see" or "for they are able to see"
* **for they hear** - "for they can hear" or "for they are able to hear"
* **the things that you see** - "the things you have seen me do"
* **the things that you hear ** - "the things you have heard me say"
