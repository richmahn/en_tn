## translationNotes

* This continues the account of how the people of Jesus' home town rejected him when he taught in their synagogue.
* **They were offended by him** - "The people of Jesus' hometown took offense at him" or "...would not accept him"
* **A prophet is not without honor** - "A prophet is honored everywhere" or "a prophet receives honor everywhere" or "people honor a prophet everywhere"
* **his own country** - "his own region" or "his own hometown"
* **his own family** - "his own home"
* **he did not do many miracles there** - Jesus did not do many miracles in his own hometown"
