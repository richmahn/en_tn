Jesus went into a house with his disciples to explain some parables about the kingdom of God.

## went into the house ##

"went indoors" or "went into the house where he was staying."

## He who sows ##

"The sower"

## the Son of Man ##

Jesus is referring to himself.

## sons of the kingdom ##

"people who belong to the kingdom"

## sons of the evil one ##

"people who belong to the evil one"

## the enemy who sowed them ##

the enemy who sowed the weeds.

## end of the world ##

"end of the age"
