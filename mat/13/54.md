This is the account of how the people of Jesus' home town rejected him when he taught in their synagogue.

## his own region ##

"his hometown" (see UDB)

## in their synagogue ##

The pronoun "their" is referring to the people of the region.

## they were astonished ##

"they were amazed"

## and these miracles ##

"and where does he get the power to do these miracles" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

## the carpenter's son ##

A carpenter is someone who makes things with wood or stone. If "carpenter" is not known, "builder" can be used.
