* Jesus continues to teach his disciples. This event began in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:05:01]].
* Jesus is talking to a group of people about what might happen to them as individuals. All “you” and "your" are plural.
* **do not sound a trumpet before yourself ** - <b>do not sound a trumpet before yourself <b>- do not draw attention to yourself like someone who plays a loud trumpet in a crowd (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
