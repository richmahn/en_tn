## translationNotes

* This continues the account of Jesus healing a boy who had an evil spirit.
* **we** - the speakers, but not the hearers (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
* **cast it out** - "make the demon come out"
* **Nothing will be impossible for you to do** - "you will be able to do anything" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]])
