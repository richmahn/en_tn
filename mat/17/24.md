## translationNotes

* This begins an account of Jesus paying the temple tax.
* **When they** - when Jesus and his disciples
* **half-shekel tax** - a tax on all Jewish males that was first given as an offering to the Lord (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
* **the house** - the place where Jesus was staying.
* **The kings on earth** - leaders in general
* **subjects** - people under a ruler or king
