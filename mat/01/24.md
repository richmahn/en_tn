## translationNotes

* This section returns to the events leading up to the birth of Jesus.
* **commanded ** - The angel had told him to take Mary as his wife and to name him Jesus (verses 20-21 ).
* **did not sleep with her ** - "did not have sexual relations with her" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
* **And he called his name Jesus** - "Joseph named his son Jesus"
