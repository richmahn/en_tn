## translationNotes

* Jesus continues to teach about riches in the world and rewards in heaven.
* **receive a hundredfold** - "receive a hundred times as many good things as they gave up"
* **many who are first now will be last** - Many who are first in the world's eyes, such as those who are rich and those who rule others, will one day be last in the kingdom of God.
