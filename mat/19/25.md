## translationNotes

* Jesus continues to teach about riches in the world and rewards in heaven.
* **they were very astonished** - " the disciples were amazed"
* **Who then can be saved?** - Possible meanings: 1) They were looking for an answer or 2) AT: "then no one can be saved!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **we have left everything ** - “we have left all our wealth” or “we have given up all our possessions”
* **what then will we have** - "what good thing will God give us"
