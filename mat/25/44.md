Jesus continues to tell his disciples how he will judge people at the end of the age.

"for any of the least important ones of my people"

## you did not do for me ##

“I consider that you did not do it for me” or "I was really the one whom you did not help"

## eternal punishment ##

"punishment that never ends"

## the righteous into eternal life ##

"the righteous people will go into eternal life"
