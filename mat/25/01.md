## translationNotes

* Jesus begins to tell a parable about wise and foolish virgins. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_parables]])
* **lamps ** - <b>lamps <b>- These could have been 1) lamps (see UDB) or 2) torches made by putting cloth around the end of a stick and wetting the cloth with oil.
* **Five of them ** - <b>Five of them <b>- "Five of the virgins"
* **did not take any oil with them** - "had with them only the oil in their lamps"
