This continues the account of Jesus' healing two men with demons.

## Now ##

This shows that the author will tell the reader information he needs before the story can continue. The pigs had been there before Jesus arrived. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-events]])

## If you cast us out ##

This can also mean "Since you are going to cast us out."

## us ##

exclusive (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])

## them  ##

the demons inside the man

## The demons came out and went into the pigs ##

"The demons left the man and entered the animals."

## Behold ##

The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.

## rushed down the steep hill ##

"ran quickly down the steep slope"

## perished in the water ##

"drowned"
