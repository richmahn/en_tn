## translationNotes

* This continues the account of what happened when Jesus was born as the King of the Jews.
* **behold** - This marks the beginning of another event in the larger story. It may involve different people than the previous events. Your language may have a way of doing this.
* **who sought the child's life ** - "who were looking for the child in order to kill him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
