# This good news of the kingdom will be preached

Here "kingdom" refers to God's rule as king. This can be stated in active form. AT: "People will tell the good news that God will rule" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# all the nations

Here, "nations" stands for people. AT: "all people in all places" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
