## translationNotes

* Jesus continues his parable about a landowner paying his workers.
* **one of them** - "one of the workers who had worked the longest"
* **Friend** - Use a word that one man would use to address another man whom he is politely rebuking.
* **Did you not agree with me for one denarius?** - AT: "We already agreed that I would give you one denarius." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **one denarius** - "one day's wages" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
* **it is my pleasure to give** - "it pleases me to give" or "I am pleased to give"
