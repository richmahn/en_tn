## translationNotes

* Jesus begins telling his disciples a parable about a man paying his workers.
* **For the kingdom of heaven is like a landowner** - God rules over everything like a landowner rules over his land. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **the kingdom of heaven is like** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:mat:13:24]].
* **After he had agreed** - "After the landowner had agreed"
* **one denarius** - "one day's wages" (See: [[:en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
