## translationNotes

* Jesus continues his parable about a landowner paying his workers.
* **Is it not legitimate for me to do what I wish with my own possessions?** - AT: "I can do what I want with my own possessions." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **legitimate** - "lawful" or "fair" or "right"
* **Or is your eye evil because I am good?** - "You should not be unhappy because I do a good thing for people who have not earned it."
