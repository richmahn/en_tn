## translationNotes

* Jesus continues his parable about a landowner paying his workers.
* **each of them** - "each of the workers who had begun work at the eleventh hour"
* **a denarius** - "one day's wages" (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicalmoney]])
* **they thought** - "the workers who had worked the longest thought"
