## translationNotes

* Jesus continues to responds to the religious leaders with a second parable.
* **person with extensive land** - "a landowner who had much property."
* **rented it out to vine dressers** - "put vine dressers in charge of the vineyard." The owner still had control of the vineyard.
* **vine dressers** - people who knew how to properly take care of vines and grapes.
