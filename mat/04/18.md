This continues the account of the beginning of Jesus' ministry in Galilee.

## casting a net ##

"throwing a net"

## Come after me, ##

Jesus invites Simon and Andrew to follow him, live with him, and become his disciples. Alternative translation: "Be my disciples."

## I will make you fishers of men. ##

Alternative translation: "I will teach you to gather men to God like you used to gather fish." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
