## translationNotes

* Jesus begins to speak against the religious leaders because of their hypocrisy.
* **you do not enter it ** - "you do not allow God to rule over you"
* **devour widows' houses** - "steal everything from women who have no men to protect them"
* **son of hell** - "person who belongs in hell" or "person who should go to hell" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
