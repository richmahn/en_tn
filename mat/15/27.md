This continues the account of Jesus healing the daughter of a Canaanite woman.

## even the little dogs eat some of the crumbs that fall from their masters' tables ##

The Gentiles should be able to have a small amount of the good things the Jews are throwing away. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## her daughter was healed ##

"Jesus healed her daughter" or "Jesus made her daughter well" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## at that hour ##

"at exactly the same time" or "immediately"
