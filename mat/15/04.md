## translationNotes

* This continues the encounter between Jesus and the scribes and Pharisees.
* **Whoever** - "Anyone who" or "If anyone"
* **honor his father** - show respect to his father by taking care of him
* **you have made void the word of God for the sake of your traditions** - AT: "you have elevated the tradition above the word of God"
