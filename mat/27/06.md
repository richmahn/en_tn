## translationNotes

* This continues the account of how Judas killed himself.
* **It is not lawful to put this ** - <b>It is not lawful to put this <b>- "Our laws do not allow us to put this"
* **put this** - "put this silver"
* **price of blood** - money paid for a man to die (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and UDB)
* **Potter's Field** - This was a field that was bought to bury strangers who died in Jerusalem. (See UDB)
* **to this day** - until the time at which the writer wrote
