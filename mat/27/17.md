## translationNotes

* This continues the account of the trial of Jesus before the Roman governor.
* **had handed him over** - "had brought Jesus to him" so Pilate would judge Jesus
* **While he was sitting** - "While Pilate was sitting"
* **sitting on the judgment seat** - doing his duty as an official (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **sent word** - "sent a message"
