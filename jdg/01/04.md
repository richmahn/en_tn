## translationNotes

* **They killed ten thousand** - AT: "They killed about 10,000" or "They killed a large number" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
* **of them** - AT: "soldiers of the Canaanites and Perizzites" or "enemies"
* **Bezek** - an area in the mountains of Canaan (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Adoni Bezek** - This man was the leader of the army of the Canaanites and the Perizzites. The word "Adoni" means "lord of" or "my lord."
