## translationNotes

* **Zebul** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:09:28]].
* **"Where are your proud words now, you who said, 'Who is Abimelech that we should serve him?' Are these not the men you despised?** - Zebul is using Abimelech's previous words as sarcasm.  AT:  "You are not as proud now when you are facing these warrors!"  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **despised** - "hated" or "disliked"
* **Gaal** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:jdg:09:26]].
* **And many fell with deadly wounds before the entrance to the city gate.** - This means that many men died of wounds in front of the city gate.
