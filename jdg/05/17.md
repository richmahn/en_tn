## And Dan, why did he wander about on ships? ##

This question is asked to express anger because the people of the tribe of Dan would not fight for Israel.  AT: "The people of the tribe of Dan thought it was more important to explore the sea in ships."  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## harbors ##

places at the seacoast with deeper water where ships were kept

## Naphtali, also ##

AT: "Naphtali, also, would risk their lives to the point of death" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
