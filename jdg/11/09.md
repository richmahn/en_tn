## leader and commander ##

These two words basically mean the same thing and are used together to emphasize how important Jephtah became. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## When he was before Yahweh ##

AT: "When he was at a special place where people worship Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
