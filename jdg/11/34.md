## translationNotes

* **tambourines** - Musical instruments you hold in one hand and shake to make a sound, often used to celebrate.
* **he tore his clothes** - This is an act that shows mourning or great sadness.
* **You have crushed me with sorrow, and you have become one who causes me pain!** - These two statements show how much Jephthah felt sad about his oath. They are both expressing the same thing in two different ways. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **crushed me with sorrow** - "made me collapse on my knees" or "destroyed me with sadness" AT: "made me extremely sad"
