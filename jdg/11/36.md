## has taken vengeance for you against your enemies, the Ammonites ##

AT: "has defeated your enemies, the Ammonies, for you"

## Let this promise be kept for me ##

AT: "Keep this promise for me" or "Keep this promise concerning me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## grieve over my virginity ##

AT: "weep because I am a virgin" or "cry because I have never been married"
