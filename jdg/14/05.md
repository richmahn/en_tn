## Timnah ##

See how you translated this in [Judges 14:1](./01.md).

## was roaring ##

"made a loud deep noise." This is the kind of noise that a lion makes when it threatens to attack something.

## came on him ##

This refers to Yahweh's Spirit coming to help Samson by giving him strength. AT: "strengthened him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## tore ... apart ##

tore into pieces

## had nothing in his hand ##

AT: "did not use a weapon"
