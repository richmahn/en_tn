## proclaim in the ears of the people ##

The people are referred to by the part of their body used to hear. AT: "proclaim to the people" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## Whoever is afraid, whoever trembles ##

Both of these phrases have the same meaning.  They are used together for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## trembles ##

This word describes fear that causes a person to uncontrollably shake. "shakes with fear"

## let him return ##

AT: "let him return to his home" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## Mount Gilead  ##

This is the name of a mountain in the region of Gilead. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
