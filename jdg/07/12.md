## translationNotes

* **as thick as a cloud of locusts** - The army camp is compared to a swarm of locusts because there are many people camped in a small area. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **they were more in number than the grains of the sand on the seashore ** - AT: "There were a great number of camels." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
