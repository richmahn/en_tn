## judges for them...rescued them ##

The word "them" refers to the Israelites.

## all the days ##

"while"

## pity ##

to have compassion for someone or something.

## groaned ##

The sound made by a person who suffers is used to describe the pain of the Israelites as they suffer. AT: "suffered" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## go after other gods ##

"follow other gods" or "obey other gods"

## give up ##

"stop"

## their evil practices ##

"evil things they did"
