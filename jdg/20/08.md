## None of us will to go to his tent and none of us will return to his house ##

AT: "We will all stay here." These two lines talk about the same thing and are used together to emphasize how committed they were. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

## But now ##

These words introduce the main portion of what the people say after the initial exclamation.
