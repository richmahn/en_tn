## translationNotes

* **wickedness and outrage** - These two words basically mean the same thing and are used together to emphasize how bad this action was. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **Now** - This word is used to introduce the conclusion of the speech of the Levite.
* **advice and attention** - "advice and counsel." These two words refer to the same thing and are repeated to emphasize that it will be the Israelites who will decide what to do. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
