## Ramath Lehi ##

This is the name of a place. It's name means "Jawbone Hill" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## the place was called ##

AT: "he called the place" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## very thirsty ##

AT: "had great need for water to drink"

## I will fall into the hands of those who are uncircumcised ##

The idea of "fall" is to lose in a conflict. AT: "Those uncircumcised men will take revenge on me and defeat me"
