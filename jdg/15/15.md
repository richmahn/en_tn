## jawbone ##

the bone in which the lower rows of teeth are set

## a thousand men ##

"1,000 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## heaps upon heaps  ##

"many heaps." These are piles of bodies from the soldiers that Samson killed. AT: "I have killed enough men to create many heaps of dead bodies" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
