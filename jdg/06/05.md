## translationNotes

* **When they and their livestock** - "When the Midianites and their livestock"
* **they would come as a swarm of locusts** - The Midianites are compared to a swarm of locusts because they came in with a great number of people.  (See:  [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **it was impossible to count** - This is a way to express the number is very great. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
