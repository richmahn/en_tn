## Look ##

To look at something is often associated with asking for agreement on a matter. AT: "Please agree with what I am doing" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## woolen fleece ##

the wooly coat of a sheep.

## dew ##

water that forms on plants during the night.

## I will know that you will ##

Gideon asks God to do what is impossible to prove his intention. AT: "through that miracle, I will know that you will" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
