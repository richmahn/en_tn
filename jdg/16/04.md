## valley of Sorek ##

This is the name of a valley near Samson's home. The name "Sorek" means best vine. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Trick ##

mislead or fool or trap someone into doing something they would not want to do

## to see ##

Seeing something is used to refer to understanding it. AT: "to understand" or "to learn"
