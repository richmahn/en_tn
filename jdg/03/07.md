## in the sight of Yahweh ##

See how this is translated in [Judges 2:11](../02/11.md).

## the anger of Yahweh was set on fire  ##

Yahweh's anger is being compared to a fire being lit. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## sold them into the hand of Cushan Rishathaim ##

Allowing the people of Israel to be conquered is compared to being sold. AT: "allowed them to be conquered by Cushan Rishathaim"

## Cushan Rishathaim ##

This is the name of a man. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Aram Naharaim ##

This is the name of a country. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
