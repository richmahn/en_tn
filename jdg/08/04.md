## he and the three hundred men who were with him ##

Gideon was the leader. This phrase clarifies that the three hundred men also went across the Jordan with him. AT: "he and the three hundred men who were with him crossed the Jordan"

## the three hundred men ##

"the 300 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## kept up the pursuit ##

"continued to chase their enemies"
