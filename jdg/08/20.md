## Jether ##

This is the name of a man. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]]

## for as the man is, so is his strength. ##

A man's son is referred to as his strength. AT: "For as the man is, so is his son." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## crescent ##

a curved shape with two points. This shape is made by the moon when it is mostly covered in shadow.
