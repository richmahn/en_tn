## translationNotes

* **What is this you have done to us?** - They used this question to make Gideon think about what he had done and to show their anger. AT: "You have not treated us fairly." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **they argued with him with force** - "they argued passionately with him" or "they rebuked him severely"
