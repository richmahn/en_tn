## because they said, “You Gileadites are fugitives in Ephraim—in Ephraim and Manasseh” ##

translations may read: "for the Gileadites, who were between Ephraim and Manasseh, said, "you are fugitives from Ephraim"."

## You Gileadites are fugitives ##

This is meant as an insult. AT: "You Gileadites are just people who came here to live."

## Gileadites ##

AT: "people from Gilead"
