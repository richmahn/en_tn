## translationNotes

* **Zaphon** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **passed through ... pass through** - "journeyed through ... journey through" or "traveled through ... travel through"
* **We will burn your house down over you** - "We will burn your house down over you with fire"
* **your house** - This refers to his physical house in which he lived.
