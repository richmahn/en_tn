## Zaphon ##

This is the name of a city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## passed through ... pass through ##

"journeyed through ... journey through" or "traveled through ... travel through"

## We will burn your house down over you ##

"We will burn your house down over you with fire"

## your house ##

This refers to his physical house in which he lived.
