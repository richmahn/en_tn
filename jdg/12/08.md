## Ibzan ##

This is the name of a man. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## He gave away thirty daughters in marriage ##

AT: "He had thirty daughters and arranged a marriage for each of them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## he brought in thirty daughters of other men for his sons ##

AT: "he found thirty women that were the daughters of other men, and arranged for them to get married to his sons" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## from the outside ##

AT: "none of the wives for his sons were from his own family"
