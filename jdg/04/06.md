## translationNotes

* The writer of Judges refers to men, a city, a mountain and a river by their names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Barak...Abinoam** - The names of men.
* **Kedesh** - The name of a city.
* **Mount Tabor** - The name of a mountain.
* **ten thousand men** - "10,000 men" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
* **draw out** - to cause people to come away from a safe location.
* **Sisera...Jabin** - See how you translated the names of these men in [[:en:bible:notes:jdg:04:01]].
* **Kishon** - The name of a river.
