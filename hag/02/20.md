## I will shake the heavens and the earth ##

Yahweh will cause disruption in their world.

## the heavens and the earth ##

This phrase is a figure of speech. AT: "the whole universe".

## for I will overthrow the throne of kingdoms ##

<b>for I will overthrow the throne of kingdoms<b>- Governments will fall apart in the chaos.

## the throne of kingdoms ##

AT: "government ruled by kings".

## the chariots and their riders ##

"Chariots" were horse-drawn carts used in battle.

## brother's sword ##

AT: "the sword on a fellow soldier".
