## Jarmuth ##

A town in the low country of Judah. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Lachish ##

Is generally regarded as the second most important city in the southern kingdom of Judah.

## Eglon ##

A town of Judah in the low country, known as the "city of palm trees".

## Hoham, Piram, Japhia, and Debir ##

These are male names of kings (See:  [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
