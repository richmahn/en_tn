# Libnah ... Lachish

These are the names of cities. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# into the hand of Israel

Here their "hand" represents their control. AT: "Yahweh gave Lachish into the control of the nation of Israel" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
