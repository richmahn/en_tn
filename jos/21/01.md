## Eleazar... Nun ##

These are male names. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## They said to them ##

AT: "The Levites said to them"

## By the hand of Moses Yahweh commanded you ##

AT: "Yahweh commanded you through Moses." The word "hand" here may represent the authority that Yahweh gave Moses. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
