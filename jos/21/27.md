## Gershon; Golan; Beeshterah ##

Names of cities. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## killed another unintentionally ##

Refers to a death resulting from an action not intended to harm a person.

## two cities ##

Refers to the same cities named as a number. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
