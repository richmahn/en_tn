## casting of lots ##

A random method of choosing to remove the choice from the leader's will, often done with the idea that God will decide the outcome.

## Kohathites ##

Translate the same way as in Numbers 3:27, 30, 4:18, 34, 37, 10:21, 26:57, 1 Chron. 6:33, 54, 61, 9:32, 2 Chron. 20:19, 29:12 and 34:12. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## thirteen cities, ten cities ##

A number of cities. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## half tribe ##

Half the tribe because the other half received their inheritance before crossing the Jordan River.
