## translationNotes

* **chest of the covenant** - the word "chest" is an alternate translation for "ark" as this is called in the following verse.
* **now the Jordan overflows all its banks throughout the time of the harvest** - This is background information and it emphasizes the scale of what Yahweh is doing. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
