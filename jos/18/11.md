# The assignment of land for the tribe of Benjamin was given to each of their clans

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Each of the clans of the tribe of Benjamin received an assignment of land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# between the descendants of Judah and the descendants of Joseph

"between the land that belonged to the descendants of Judah and the land that belonged to the descendants of Joseph"

# the descendants of Joseph

This refers to the tribes of Ephraim and Manasseh. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
