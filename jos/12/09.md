# Enaim ... Jarmuth ... Lachish ... Eglon ... Gezer

These are the names of cities. Translate "Jarmuth," "Lachish," and "Eglon" in the same way you did in [Joshua 10:3](../10/03.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
