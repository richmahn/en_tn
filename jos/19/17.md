## fourth casting ##

number four in a series (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## Jezreel ##

Translate this name the same way as in Joshua 15:56 and 17:16. Also found repeatedly in 1 & 2 Samuel, 1 & 2 Kings and Hosea. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Kesulloth, Shunem, Hapharaim, Shion, and Anaharath ##

names of cities given to Issachar's tribe (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Kesulloth (UDB) and Chesulloth (ULB) ##

are two ways to spell the same beginning sound.
