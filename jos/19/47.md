# Leshem

This is the name of a city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# This was the inheritance of the tribe of Dan

The land and cities that the tribe of Dan received are spoken of as if they were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "This was the land and the cities that the tribe of Dan received as an inheritance" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# it was given to their clans

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Joshua gave it to their clans" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
