# This was the inheritance of the tribe of Issachar

The land and cities that the tribe of Issachar received are spoken of as if they were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "This was the land and the cities that the tribe of Issachar received as an inheritance" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# it was given to their clans

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Joshua gave it to their clans" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
