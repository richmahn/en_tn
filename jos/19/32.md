## sixth casting ##

The number six in a series. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## casting of lots ##

A random method of choosing to remove the choice from the leader's will, often done with the idea that God will decide the outcome.

## Heleph, Zaanannim, Adaminekah, Jabneel, Lakkum, Aznothtabor, and Hukkok ##

Names of cities. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
