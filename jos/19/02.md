# General Information:

The writer lists cities that were in the land that the tribe of Simeon received as an inheritance. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# They had for their inheritance

The land and cities that the tribe of Simeon received are spoken of as if they were an inheritance that they received as a permanent possession. AT: "They had the following cities for their inheritance" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
