## inheritance to Israel ##

The word "Israel" here refers to the nation of Israel as a whole. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the land had rest from the wars ##

The "land" here "had rest" like a person. AT: "Canaan did not have wars for a while." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
