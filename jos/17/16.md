# Beth Shan ... Jezreel

These are the names of places. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# the house of Joseph

Here the word "house" refers to the descendants. AT: "the descendants of Joseph" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# you will clear it

"you will clear the forest of trees" or "you will cut down its trees"
