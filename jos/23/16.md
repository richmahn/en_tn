## translationNotes

* **He will do this** - Refers to the punishment threatened in the previous verse.
* **worship other gods and bow down to them** - Another way of describing worship. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **will be kindled** - "Kindled" is a metaphor for the beginning of Yahweh's anger, like a fire is "kindled" or started with "kindling" or small very easy to start burning like dried grass or small twigs. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
