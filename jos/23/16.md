## He will do this ##

Refers to the punishment threatened in the previous verse.

## worship other gods and bow down to them ##

Another way of describing worship. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## will be kindled ##

"Kindled" is a metaphor for the beginning of Yahweh's anger, like a fire is "kindled" or started with "kindling" or small very easy to start burning like dried grass or small twigs. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
