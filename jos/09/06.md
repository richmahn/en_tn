# men of Israel

This refers to the entire nation of Israel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# the Hivites

This is another name for the Gibeonites. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Perhaps you live near us. How can we make a covenant with you?

Joshua is emphasizing that the people of Israel have to follow the command of Yahweh above all else. AT: "If you do live near us, we cannot make a covenant with you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
