## translationNotes

* The Gibeonites continue to deceive Joshua and the nation of Israel.
* **Sihon** - This is the name of a man. The name of the defeated Amorite king.  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Og** - This is the name of a man. The name of the defeated king of Bashan.
* **the Jordan** - This is a shortened name for the Jordan River. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Heshbon** - This is the name of the royal city of the nation of Moab.
* **Ashtaroth** - This is the name of a city known for worshiping the goddess of the same name.
