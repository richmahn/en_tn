The Gibeonites continue to deceive Joshua and the nation of Israel.

## Sihon ##

This is the name of a man. The name of the defeated Amorite king.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Og ##

This is the name of a man. The name of the defeated king of Bashan.

## the Jordan ##

This is a shortened name for the Jordan River. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## Heshbon ##

This is the name of the royal city of the nation of Moab.

## Ashtaroth ##

This is the name of a city known for worshiping the goddess of the same name.
