## translationNotes

* Gibeonits continue to deceive the nation of Israel.
* **in your hand** - This phrase means "take with you". Here the word "hand" represents a whole person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **meet them; say to them** - The word "them refers to the people of Israel.
* **wineskins** - a leather bag or bottle made to contain and carry wine.
