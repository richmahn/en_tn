## translationNotes

* **Nun** - <b>Nun<b>- This is a male name; the father of Joshua. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **everything that had happened to them** - <b>everything that had happened to them<b>- "all that the men had experienced and seen."
* **us** - <b>us<b>- This word, "us," refers to Israel.
* **cross over** - "cross over" means to go to the opposite bank of the river. AT: "travel from this side to the opposite side of the Jordan." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
