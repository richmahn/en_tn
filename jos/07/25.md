## they burned them all, and they stoned them ##

Possible meanings are 1) the Israelites burned Achan's family to death and then covered them with stones (see UDB) or 2) the Israelites stoned Achan's family to death and then burned the dead bodies.

## Valley of Achor ##

The name means "Valley of Trouble," but it is best to translate this the way it sounds. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
