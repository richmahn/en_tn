## Achan, Carmi, Zimri, Zerah ##

These are the names of men. Translate Achan the same way in Joshua 7:10, 18-20, 24 and 22:20. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Yahweh's anger burned ##

"Anger" and "burned" indicates intensity, not that fire is present. AT:"Yahweh's anger burned like a fire" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
