## translationNotes

* **sent men** - The word "men" here means "soldiers." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **all the people** - The word "people" here refers to the army of Israel.
* **they are few in number** - This phrase refers to the people of Ai.
