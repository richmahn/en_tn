Yahweh tells Joshua why Israel is cursed.

## Why are you lying there on your face ##

AT: "Stand up! Stop lying there with your face in the dirt! (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## the reserved things ##

These are the things "marked for destruction" from [Joshua 6:17](../06/17.md). AT: "the cursed things" or "those things which God has cursed."
