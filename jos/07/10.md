## translationNotes

* Yahweh tells Joshua why Israel is cursed.
* **Why are you lying there on your face** - AT: "Stand up! Stop lying there with your face in the dirt! (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **the reserved things** - These are the things "marked for destruction" from [[en:bible:notes:jos:06:17]]. AT: "the cursed things" or "those things which God has cursed."
