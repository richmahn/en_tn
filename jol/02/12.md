## Return to me with all your heart ##

AT: "Turn away from your sins and be totally devoted to me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]]).

## Tear your heart and not only your garments ##

Tearing one's clothes is an outward act of shame or repentance. "Tear you heart" refers to having an inward attitude of repentance as well. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
