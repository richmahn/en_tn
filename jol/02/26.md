# General Information:

Yahweh continues the speech he began in [Joel 2:25](./24.md), promising good things for the people of Israel.

# praise the name of Yahweh

The name is a metonym for the person, specifically his reputation. AT: "praise Yahweh" or "praise Yahweh because everyone knows he is good" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# the name of Yahweh ... who has done wonders

Yahweh speaks of himself by name to show that he will certainly do as he has said. AT: "my name ... because I have done wonders" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-123person]])
