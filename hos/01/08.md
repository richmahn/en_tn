# Lo-Ruhamah

This name means "no mercy." The translator may choose to represent this meaning as the name. See how you translated this in [Hosea 1:6](./06.md). AT: "No Mercy" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Lo-Ammi

This name means "not my people." The translator may choose to represent this meaning as the name. AT: "Not My People" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
