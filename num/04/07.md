## translationNotes

* **On it they must put** - Here the word "it" refers to the "blue cloth."
* **bowls, and jars for pouring** - AT: "bowls and jars used to pour out drink offerings" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **They must cover them** - Here the word "them" refers to "the dishes, spoons, bowls, and jars."
* **scarlet cloth** - "red cloth"
