## On it they must put ##

Here the word "it" refers to the "blue cloth."

## bowls, and jars for pouring ##

AT: "bowls and jars used to pour out drink offerings" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## They must cover them ##

Here the word "them" refers to "the dishes, spoons, bowls, and jars."
