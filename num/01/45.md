# So all the men of Israel ... were counted in each of their families

The phrase "were counted" can be stated in active form. This long phrase is repeated multiple times in the census. See how you translated it in [Numbers 1:20](./20.md). Alternate translation: "So they counted all the men of Israel ... in each of their families" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
