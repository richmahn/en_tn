## translationNotes

* **took these men** - "gathered these men together"
* **who were recorded by name** - AT: "whose names they had recorded" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **Then each man...identified his ancestry. He had to name the clans and families descended from his ancestors** - The second sentence means basically the same thing as the first and is added for clarification. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **He had to name** - AT: "Each man had to say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
