# General Information:

Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do.

# the first month, on the fourteenth day of the month

"During the first month, on day 14 of the month." This refers to the first month of the Hebrew calendar. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-hebrewmonths]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-ordinal]])

# comes Yahweh's Passover

"you must celebrate Yahweh's Passover"
