## Must we bring water out of this rock for you? ##

Moses asks this question out of frustration to rebuke the people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Must we bring ##

Here the word "we" refers to Moses and Aaron and may include Yahweh, but does not include the people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])
