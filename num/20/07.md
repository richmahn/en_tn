# before their eyes

Here the people are represented by their "eyes" to emphasize what they see. AT: "while they watch you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# from before Yahweh

This represents Yahweh's tent. AT: "from Yahweh's tent" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
