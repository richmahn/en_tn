* **The people of Israel, the whole community** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **Aaron must be gathered to his people** - AT: "Aaron must die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
* **rebelled against my word** - AT: "refused to do what I said" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
