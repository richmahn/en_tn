# On the second day of the assembly

"On day 2 of the festival." Here the word "assembly" refers to the Festival of Weeks. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-ordinal]])

# twelve young bulls, two rams, and fourteen male lambs

"12 young bulls, 2 rams, and 14 male lambs" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
