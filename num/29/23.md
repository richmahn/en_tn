# General Information:

Yahweh continues telling Moses what the people must do during the festival in the seventh month.

# the fourth day of the assembly

"day 4 of the festival." Here the word "assembly" refers to the Festival of Weeks. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-ordinal]])

# fourteen male lambs

"14 male lambs" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
