## the commanders of thousands  ##

AT: "the men who commanded 1,000 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## the captains of hundreds  ##

AT: "the men who commanded 100 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## Have you let all the women live? ##

Moses uses a question to scold the commanders of the army. AT: "You should not have let all these women live." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
