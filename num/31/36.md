# General Information:

Moses is listing the plunder that goes to the soldiers and the tax that goes to Yahweh. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# The half that was kept for the soldiers

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "The soldiers' part of the sheep" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# 337,000 sheep

"three hundred and thirty-seven thousand sheep" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
