# seventy-two thousand oxen, sixty-one thousand donkeys, and thirty-two thousand women

"72,000 oxen, 61,000 donkeys, and 32,000 women" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# women who had not known a man by lying with him

"women who had no had sexual relations with a man." This refers to women who are virgins. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
