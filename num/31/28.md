# General Information:

"me" refers to Yahweh.

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Collect a tax from the soldiers' plunder and give it to me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# every five hundred

"every 500" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# from their half

"from the soldiers' half"

# to be presented to me

This can be stated in active form. AT: "which he will present to me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
