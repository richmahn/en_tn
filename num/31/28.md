# General Information:

Yahweh continues speaking to Moses.
"me" refers to Yahweh.

# Then levy a tax to be given to me from the soldiers who went out to battle

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "Collect a tax from the soldiers' plunder and give it to me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# every five hundred

"every 500" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
