## the commanders over thousands ##

AT: "the men who commanded 1,000 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## the commanders over hundreds ##

AT: "the men who commanded 100 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## Your servants have counted ##

The commanders refer to themselves as "your servants." This is a polite way to speak to someone with greater authority.
