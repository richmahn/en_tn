## Now ##

This word is used here to mark a break in the main teaching. Here Moses begins listing the amount of plunder and how much went to the solders, to the people, and to Yahweh.

## 675,000 sheep, 72,000 oxen, 61,000 donkeys, and 32,000 women ##
