## translationNotes

* Balaam finishes the first of his four prophecies.
* **out of Jacob will come** - Jacob was the ancestor of the Israelites. This refers to the whole people group. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **of their city** - This refers to the city of Ar where Balak met Balaam. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
