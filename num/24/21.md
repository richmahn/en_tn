## the Kenites ##

This is the name of a people group who descended from Kain. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## your nest is in the rocks ##

This means that they live in a secure place. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## Nevertheless Kain will be ruined when Assyria carries you away captive ##

AT: "Nevertheless the Assyrians will destroy the Kenites and take them as captives" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
