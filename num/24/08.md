Balaam continues the prophecy under the control of the Spirit of God.

## God brings him ##

"God brings the Israelites"

## He has strength like a wild ox ##

This emphasizes that the Israelites have great strength. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## He will eat up the nations ##

Balaam compares the Israelites to a wild animal. This means they will destroy their enemies. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
