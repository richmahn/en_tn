# The camp under the banner of Judah's descendants

This camp includes the three tribes under the division of Judah: Judah, Issachar, and Zebulun.

# went out first

"went out 1st." The phrase "went out" means that they packed up their camp and began moving. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-ordinal]])
