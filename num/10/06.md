## When you blow a loud signal ##

Here the word “you” is plural and refers to the priests. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## They must blow a loud signal for their journeys ##

Here the word "They" refers to the priests and the word "their" refers to the people of Israel.

## be a regulation for you ##

"be a rule for you." Here the word "you" is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])
