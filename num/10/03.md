## translationNotes

* **the leaders, the clan heads of Israel** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **When you blow a loud signal** - Here the word "you" is plural and refers to the priests. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
