## Should your brothers...Yahweh has given them? ##

Moses uses questions to emphasize that the people from Gad and Reuben need to help the other tribes take the land that Yahweh has promised them. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## discourage the hearts of the people of Israel from going ##

AT: "cause the people of Israel to lose confidence about going" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
