## every man who is armed ##

AT: "every man who is ready with his weapon"

## if the land is subdued before you ##

AT: "if they help you defeat the people living in the land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## then they will acquire their possessions among you in the land of Canaan ##

AT: "then the descendants of Gad and Reuben will receive land with you in Canaan and not be allowed to stay east of the Jordan River"
