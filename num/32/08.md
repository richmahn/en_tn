# General Information:

Moses continues speaking to the people of Reuben and Gad.

# Valley of Eshkol

This is the name of a place. See how you translated this in [Numbers 13:23](../13/23.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# They saw the land

This refers to seeing what was in the land. AT: "They saw the strong people and cities in the land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# discouraged the hearts of the people of Israel

Here the word "hearts" represents the people themselves and refers to the seat of their emotions. See how you translated this in [Numbers 32:7](./06.md). AT: "discouraged the people of Israel" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
