## translationNotes

* **following the directions given to them by Yahweh’s word, as Yahweh commanded** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **given to them by Yahweh’s word** - AT: "that Yahweh had given to them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
