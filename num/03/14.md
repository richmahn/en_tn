## following the directions given to them by Yahweh’s word, as Yahweh commanded ##

These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## given to them by Yahweh’s word ##

AT: "that Yahweh had given to them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
