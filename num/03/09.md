## You must give ##

Here the word "You" refers to Moses.

## They are wholly given ##

AT: "I have given them entirely" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## but any foreigner who comes near ##

AT: "but any foreigner who comes near the tabernacle" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## must be put to death ##

AT: "you must kill" or "must die" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
