## Arnon ##

This is the name of a river. See how you translated it in [Numbers 21:12](../21/12.md).

## Did I not send men to you to summon you ##

AT: "Surely I sent men to summon you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Why did you not come to me? ##

AT: "You should have come to me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Am I not able to honor you? ##

AT: "Surely you know that I am able to pay you money for coming to me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
