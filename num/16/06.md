## translationNotes

* **censers** - A censer is a container that people use to burn incense.
* **before Yahweh** - AT: "in Yahweh's presence" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **the one reserved for him** - AT: "the one whom Yahweh has reserved for himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **You have gone too far** - These are the same words that the men use to accuse Moses and Aaron in [[en:bible:notes:num:16:01]]. AT: "It is you who are trying to use more authority than you should" (UDB)
