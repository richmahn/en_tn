## censers ##

A censer is a container that people use to burn incense.

## before Yahweh ##

AT: "in Yahweh's presence" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## the one reserved for him ##

AT: "the one whom Yahweh has reserved for himself" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## You have gone too far ##

These are the same words that the men use to accuse Moses and Aaron in [Numbers 16:1](./01.md). AT: "It is you who are trying to use more authority than you should" (UDB)
