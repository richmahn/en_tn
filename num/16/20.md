## translationNotes

* **from among this community** - Here the word "community" refers to the 250 men whom Korah gathered at the tabernacle.
* **lay face down** - This was a posture of submission and indicates that Moses and Aaron were praying to Yahweh.
* **the God of the spirits of all humanity** - Here the word "spirits" refers either to breath or life-force. AT: "the God who gives life to all humanity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **if one man sins, must you be angry with all the community?** - AT: "please do not be angry with all the community because one man sins" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
