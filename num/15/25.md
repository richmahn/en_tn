# They will be forgiven

This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will forgive them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# made by fire

This can be stated in active form. AT: "that they made by fire" or "that they burned on the altar" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# all the community of the people of Israel will be forgiven

This can be stated in active form. AT: "I will forgive all the community of the people of Israel" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
