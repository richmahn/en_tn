## translationNotes

* **follow after your own heart and your own eyes** - Here the words "heart" and "eyes" refer to what a person wants and thinks is right. AT: "do whatever you desire or think is right" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **act like spiritual prostitutes** - AT: "act unfaithfully" or "worship false gods"
