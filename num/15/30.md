## That person must be cut off ##

AT: "You must cut that person off" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## His sin will be on him ##

Here the word "sin" refers to the consequences of sin. AT: "He will suffer the consequences for his sin" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
