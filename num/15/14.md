## translationNotes

* **As you are, so also must be the traveler** - AT: "You and the traveler are alike before Yahweh" or "The same law applies to both you and the traveler"
* **before Yahweh** - In order to obey Yahweh and please him.
* **law and decree** - These two words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
