# two-tenths of an ephah

An ephah is a unit of volume equal to about 22 liters. AT: "4 liters" or "four and a half liters" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])

# a third of a hin

A hin is a unit of volume equal to about 3.7 liters. AT: "one liter" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])

# It will produce a sweet aroma for Yahweh

"It will produce a smell that pleases Yahweh." The Lord's pleasure with the aroma represents his pleasure with the person who burns the offering. AT: "You will please Yahweh by offering it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
