## a tenth of an ephah ##

An ephah is a unit of volume equal to about 22 liters. AT: "about 2 liters" (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/other/biblicalvolume]])

## a fourth of a hin ##

A hin is a unit of volume equal to about 3.7 liters. AT: "about 1 liter" (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/other/biblicalvolume]])
