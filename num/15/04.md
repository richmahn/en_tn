# a burnt offering

This refers to the offerings spoken of in [Numbers 15:3](./04.md).

# a tenth of an ephah

An ephah is a unit of volume equal to about 22 liters. AT: "about 2 liters" or "two liters" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])

# one-fourth of a hin

A hin is a unit of volume equal to about 3.7 liters. AT: "about 1 liter" or "one liter" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])
