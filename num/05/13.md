## translationNotes

* **a spirit of jealousy might still inform the husband** - Here the word “spirit” refers to a person's attitude and emotions. AT: "the husband might feel jealous and become suspicious" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **a spirit of jealousy might falsely come on a man** - AT: "a man might feel jealous for no reason" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
