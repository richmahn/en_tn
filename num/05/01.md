## translationNotes

* **infectious skin disease** - This refers to leprosy which is a sickness that affects the skin and easily spreads to other people.
* **oozing sore** - This can refer to an open cut that is leaking bodily fluids.
* **you must send** - Here the word "you" is plural and refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
