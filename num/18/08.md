## the offerings raised up to me ##

To raise something up is to make a contribution. AT: "the offerings that people contribute to me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## that are not completely burned up ##

AT: "that you do not completely burn on the altar" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
