"by touching the body of a dead man"

## Why do you keep us from offering the sacrifice...among the people of Israel? ##

The men ask this question in order to complain that they are not allowed to participate in celebrating the Passover. AT: "It is not fair that you keep us away from offering the sacrifice...among the people of Israel." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
