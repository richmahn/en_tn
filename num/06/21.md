## he must keep the obligations of the vow he has taken, to keep the promise indicated by the law for the Nazirite ##

These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## the promise indicated by the law for the Nazirite ##

AT: "the promise that the law for the Nazirite indicates" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
