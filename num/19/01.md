## a statute, a law ##

These two words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## bring to you ##

Here the word "you" is singular and refers to Moses.

## flaw or blemish ##

These two words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
