## translationNotes

* **Spring up** - AT: "Fill up with water"
* **The well that our leaders dug, the well the people's nobles dug** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **with the scepter** - The scepter is a sign of authority. AT: "by their authority"
