## with the edge of the sword ##

AT: "with their swords" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## took their land ##

Here the word "their" refers to the Amorites. AT: "conquered their land"

## the border of the people of Ammon was fortified ##

AT: "the people of Ammon fortified their border" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## Sihon had taken all his land ##

Here the word "his" refers to the king of Moab.
