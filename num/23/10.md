## translationNotes

* **Who can count the dust of Jacob or number even only one fourth of Israel?** - AT: "No one can count the Israelites. No one could count even a fourth of them because there are so many" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **and let my life's end be like his** - Here "his" refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
