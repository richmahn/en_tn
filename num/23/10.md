## Who can count the dust of Jacob or number even only one fourth of Israel? ##

AT: "No one can count the Israelites. No one could count even a fourth of them because there are so many" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## and let my life's end be like his ##

Here "his" refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
