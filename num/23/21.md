## He has seen no hardship in Jacob or trouble in Israel ##

AT: "There is no hardship or trouble among the Israelites" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## and shouts for their king are among them ##

AT: "and they shout with joy because Yahweh is their king"

## With strength like that of a wild ox ##

This emphasizes Yahweh's great strength. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
