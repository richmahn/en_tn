# he heard his voice speaking to him

Here "his voice" refers to Yahweh. AT: "he heard Yahweh speaking to him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# from above the atonement lid ... from between the two cherubim

These two phrases describe the same location. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
