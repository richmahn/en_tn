## translationNotes

* **two carts and four oxen** - AT: "2 carts and 4 oxen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
* **because of what their work needed** - AT: "because this is what they needed to do their work"
* **four carts and eight oxen** - AT: "4 carts and 8 oxen" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
* **in the care of Ithamar son of Aaron the priest** - This means that Ithamar showed the descendants of Merari what to do as they worked.
