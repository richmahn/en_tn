# the avenger of blood

Here the word "blood" is a metonym for the murder. See how you translated these words in [Numbers 35:19](./19.md). AT: "the one who avenges the murder" or "the relative seeking vengeance" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
