## The blood avenger ##

AT: "The relative seeking vengeance"

## manhandles a victim ##

AT: "overpowers a victim" or "acts violently towards a victim"

## then the accused who struck him must surely be put to death ##

AT: "the relative must certainly execute the accused man" or "the accused man must die" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
