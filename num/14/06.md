# Nun ... Jephunneh

These are names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# who were some of those sent

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "who were some of those whom Moses sent" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# tore their clothes

Tearing one's clothes was a gesture indicating the person is very troubled and is mourning. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-symaction]])
