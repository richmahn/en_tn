## translationNotes

* **Why did Yahweh bring us to this land to die by the sword?** - The people ask this question in order to complain and accuse Yahweh of treating them unfairly. AT: "Yahweh should not have brought us to this land only to die by the sword." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **to die by the sword** - AT: "to die in battle" or "to have people kill us with swords" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Is it not better for us to return to Egypt?** - AT: "It is better for us to return to Egypt than to try to conquer Canaan." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
