# as you have spoken in my hearing

"as I have heard you say"

# Your dead bodies will fall

Their dead bodies falling represents them dying. AT: "You will die" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# you who were counted in the census

This can be stated in active form. AT: "you whom Moses counted in the census" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# from twenty years old and upward

"twenty years old and older"
