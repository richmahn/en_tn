## How long must I tolerate this evil community that criticizes me? ##

Yahweh asks this question because he has lost patience with the people. AT: "I have tolerated this evil community who criticizes me for long enough" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## I have heard the complaining of the people of Israel ##

AT: "I have heard the people of Israel complain" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
