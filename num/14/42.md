# Yahweh is not with you

Helping them is spoken of as being with them. Alternate translation: "Yahweh will not help you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# to prevent you from being defeated by your enemies

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "to prevent your enemies from defeating you" or "to give you victory over your enemies" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
