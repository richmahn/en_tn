## translationNotes

* **They will be consumed in this wilderness. Here they will die** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **They will be consumed in this wilderness** - AT: "This wilderness will cause them to die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
