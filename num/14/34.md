## They will be consumed in this wilderness. Here they will die ##

These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## They will be consumed in this wilderness ##

AT: "This wilderness will cause them to die" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
