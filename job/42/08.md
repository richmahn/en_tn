# seven bulls

"7 bulls" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# so that I may not deal with you after your folly

The abstract noun "folly" can be translated as an adjective. Alternate translation: "even though you have been very foolish, I will not punish you as you deserve" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
