# I know that you can do all things, that no purpose of yours can be stopped

You may want to repeat the words "I know." Alternate translation: "I know that you can do all things. I know that no purpose of yours can be stopped" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

# no purpose of yours can be stopped

This can be translated in active form. Alternate translation: "no one can stop any of your plans" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
