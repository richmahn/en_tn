## like an army through a wide hole in the city wall ##

Job compares his situation to a city under attack with a hole in its defensive wall. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## my honor is driven away as if by the wind ##

AT: "the wind has blown away my honor;" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## my prosperity passes away as a cloud ##

Job's belongings have disappeared like a cloud that suddenly appears and then vanishes from the sky. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
