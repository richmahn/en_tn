AT: "My enemies prepare to destroy me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicitinfo]])

## pile up against me their siege mounds ##

Job knows that his enemies are preparing to attack him, just as an army prepares to attack a wall of protection around a city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
