## covenant faithfulness ##

"love that never ends;"

## your help ##

"your care"

## you hid in your heart ##

"you kept this a secret" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## that if I sinned, you would notice it ##

"if I sinned, you would see it;"

## you would not acquit me of my iniquity ##

"you would not allow me to go unpunished." or "you would not overlook my sin."
