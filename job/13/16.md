## my acquittal ##

AT: "My release from guilt"

## God, listen carefully ##

Job begins directing his speech directly to God.

## listen carefully ... come to your hears ##

Both lines mean the same for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## come to your ears ##

"Ears" represent the hearing and listening by a person who here is God himself. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
