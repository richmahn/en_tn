# Connecting Statement:

Job finishes speaking to his friends and begins to address God directly.

# This will be the reason for my deliverance

The abstract noun "deliverance" can be expressed with the verb "deliver." Alternate translation: "This is the reason that God will deliver me" or "This is why God will save me from my troubles" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
