## translationNotes

* Job continues addressing his case directly to God himself.
* **See now** - This emphasizes what follows. "Please pay special attention"
* **I have set my defense in order** - This expression means "have thought how to explain myself and I am ready to show that I am innocent".
* **Who is the one who would argue against me in court?** - Job asks this question because he assumes everyone would know the answer. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **If you** - "You" here means God himself.
* **give up my life** - "allow myself to face death"
