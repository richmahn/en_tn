## Should not a multitude of words be answered ##

Zophar is asking a question in the negative to emphasize that Job's words must be challenged. AT "We must answer these words!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

## Should this man, so full of talk, be believed? ##

AT "Your many words alone do not mean you are innocent." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Should your boasting make others  remain silent? ##

<b>Should your boasting make others  remain silent?<b> AT "Just because you have spoken many words, this does not mean that others must keep silent." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## When you mock our teaching, will no one make you feel ashamed? ##

AT "You have mocked our teaching. Now we will make you feel ashamed!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
