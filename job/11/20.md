## the eyes of the wicked ##

Zophar compares one part (eyes) to the understanding a person has. AT: "the understanding of the wicked" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## a last gasp of life ##

AT: "their final breath of life" "stop trying and die" This phrase means to resign and give up, especially the "spirit" or breath of life. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
