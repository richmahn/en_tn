## translationNotes

* Bildad continues to describe the wicked person.
* **His roots will be dried up...branch be cut off** - This compares a wicked person to a sick tree that looses its roots and branches. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **His memory will perish...no name in the street** - These phrases are used together to emphasize the fact that nobody will remember him anymore. AT: "Everybody in the world will forget about him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
