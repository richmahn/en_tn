## translationNotes

* Bildad continues to describe the wicked person.
* **such are the homes of unrighteous people** - "that is what happens to the homes of unrighteous people." Bildad uses this phrase to conclude his comments on what happens to a wicked person.
* **the places of those who do not know God** - AT: "that is what happens to places that do not know God." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
