## translationNotes

* Bildad continues to describe the wicked person.
* **His wealth will turn into hunger** - AT: "Instead of being wealthy, he will be hungry"
* **and calamity will be ready at his side** - "and disaster will be ready by his side" or AT: "and he will suddenly experience disaster."
* **The parts of his body will be devoured** - "Disease will eat away at his skin" or "Disease will destroy his skin"
* **the firstborn of death will devour his parts** - The phrase "the firstborn of death" refers to diseases which kill people. AT: "a deadly disease will destroy the the different parts of his body." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
