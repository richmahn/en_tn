## But he is one of a kind ##

AT: "But no one can change him." This can also be translated as: "But God is one" or "But he alone is God."

## who can turn him back? ##

The implicit answer is: "no one." AT: "nobody can make him change his mind" or "no one can influence him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## What he desires, he does ##

AT: "What he wants is what he accomplishes" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## there are many them ##

AT: "he has many plans for me"
