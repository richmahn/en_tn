Job continues speaking.

## he knows the way that I take ##

AT: "God knows what I do" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## I will come out like gold ##

Job believes that the test will prove that he is as valuable as gold. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## My foot has held fast to his steps ##

AT: "I have followed closely behind him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## I have kept to his way ##

AT: "I have done what he told me to do" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## turned not aside ##

AT: "followed it precisely" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

## of his lips ##

AT: "that he spoke" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## I have treasured up in my heart ##

AT: "I constantly think of" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## the words of his mouth ##

AT: "what he said" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
