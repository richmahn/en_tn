## my complaint is bitter ##

AT: "my complaining is as bad ad a bitter taste" or "my complaint is very bad" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## my suffering is heavier than my groaning ##

Other translations may read: "my hand is heavy because of my groaning" or "his hand is heavy despite my groaning." The "heavy hand" is God's punishment that makes Job suffer. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
