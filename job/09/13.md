## How much less could I answer him, could I choose words to reason with him? ##

Job poses parallel questions to emphasize his reluctance to confront God.  AT: "What could I say to answer or even question him?" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
