## translationNotes

* Job continues describing situations in which he would deserve God's punishment, but he knows they are not true. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_hypo]])
* **indeed, I have not suffered my mouth to sin by asking for his life with a curse** - Job is describing how he truly treated those who hated him. AT: "actually, I did not let myself sin by cursing his life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
