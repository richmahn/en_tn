AT: "the moon moving across the sky" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

AT: "if I have secretly desired to worship them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## my mouth has kissed my hand ##

This is a sign of love and devotion.
