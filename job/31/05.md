Job continues speaking.

## If I have ##

In 31:5-40 Job describes different situations in which he would deserve God's punishment. But, he knows that they are not true and he is innocent. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hypo]])

## walked with falsehood, if my foot has hurried to deceit ##

AT: "done anything false or purposely deceived anyone"

## let me be weighed in an even balance ##

People used balances to weigh items and to determine their value.  AT: "Let God judge me honestly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
