## Eliphaz the Temanite, Bildad the Shuhite, and Zophar the Naamathite ##

Eliphaz, Bildad, and Zophar are men's names. Teman was a city in Edom. Shuhites are the decedents of Abraham and Keturah (see Genesis [Genesis 25:1](../../gen/25/01.md)). Naamah was a city in Canaan. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## set a time ##

AT: "agreed upon a time"

## to mourn with him and to comfort him ##

"to cry with" or "to sympathize with him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
