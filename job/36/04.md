## translationNotes

* **my words will not be false** - AT: "I will say what is true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **See** - The word “See” here adds emphasis to what follows. AT: “Indeed”
* **he is mighty in strength of understanding** - The phrase "mighty in strength" means "very strong." AT: "he is very strong in understanding" or "he understands everything very well" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
