## you are full of the judgment on the wicked ##

Possible meanings are 1) "God is punishing you as he would punish the wicked" (UDB) or 2) "you are obsessed with the judgment that the wicked deserve."

## judgment and justice have laid hold of you ##

AT: "God has brought you to judgment and given you justice" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## Do not let wealth attract you to deception ##

AT: "Do not let someone attract you to deception with wealth"

## do not let the great size of a bribe turn you aside from justice ##

AT: "do not let someone turn you aside from justice with a large bribe"
