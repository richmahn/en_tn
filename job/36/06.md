## translationNotes

* **does not withdraw his eyes from the righteous** - AT: "watches over those who are righteous" (UDB) or "protects the righteous" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **sets the righteous on thrones like kings** - Yahweh honors righteous people like people honor kings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **they are lifted up** - Possible meanings are 1) "he causes others to honor them" or 2) "he causes them to prosper" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
