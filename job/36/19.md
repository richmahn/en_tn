## Can your wealth benefit you...can all the force of your strength help you? ##

Elihu asks these questions to emphasize that money and power will not be able to help Job if he acts unjustly. AT: "Wealth cannot benefit you...all the force of your strength cannot help you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## all the force of your strength ##

"all of your great strength"

## when peoples are cut off in their place ##

Possible meanings are 1) "when people groups disappear from their place" or 2) "when people drag others away from their homes."

## you are being tested by suffering ##

AT: "God is testing you by making you suffer" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
