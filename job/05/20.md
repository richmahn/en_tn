The shift in pronoun from "he/him" to "you" which began in [Job 5:17](./17.md) continues through the end of Eliphaz's speech in [Job 5:26](./26.md).

## In famine he will rescue you ##

"In famine God will redeem you"

## rescue you ##

"ransom you" or "redeem you" or "save you from danger"

## destruction ##

"harm from enemies"

## you will not be afraid of wild beasts ##

"you will not be afraid of the wild beasts"

## wild beasts ##

animals that have not been tamed or trained
