Elihu continues speaking.

## Let us ##

Here "us" refers to Elihu, Job, and his three friends. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])

## has taken away my rights ##

"refused to give me justice"

## I am considered to be a liar ##

AT: "my friends think I am a liar." This could possibly mean: "God is not telling the truth about me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## My wound is incurable ##

AT: "I am sick and no one can heal me"
