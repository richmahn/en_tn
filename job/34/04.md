# General Information:

Elihu continues speaking.

# Let us

Here "us" refers to Elihu, Job, and his three friends. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])

# has taken away my rights

"refused to give me justice"

# I am considered to be a liar

This can be stated in active form. AT: "God considers me to be a liar" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# My wound is incurable

Here Job's sickness and suffering is spoken of as if it were a "wound." AT: "I am sick and no one can heal me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
