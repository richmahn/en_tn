## translationNotes

* Elihu continues speaking.
* **Let us** - Here "us" refers to Elihu, Job, and his three friends. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
* **has taken away my rights** - "refused to give me justice"
* **I am considered to be a liar** - AT: "my friends think I am a liar." This could possibly mean: "God is not telling the truth about me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **My wound is incurable** - AT: "I am sick and no one can heal me"
