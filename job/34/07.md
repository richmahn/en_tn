Elihu continues speaking.

## What man is like Job ##

Elihu uses a question to scold Job. AT: "There is no one else like Job" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## who drinks up mockery like water ##

Elihu is accusing Job of enjoying mocking other people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## who walks with wicked men ##

AT: "who behaves like wicked men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
