## Who can strip off his outer covering? ##

AT: "No one can strip off his outer clothing." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Who can penetrate his double armor? ##

AT: "No one can penetrate his very thick hide." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Who can open the doors of his face … ? ##

## close together as with a close seal. ##

This means the scales are very close to one another and tightly bound together.
