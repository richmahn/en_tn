## translationNotes

* Job continues speaking to his friends.
* **in bitterness of soul** - AT: "with anger and resentment" or "after living a sad life" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **They lie down alike in the dust** - AT: "They both die and people bury them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
* **the worms cover them both** - "and the worms in the dirt eat their dead bodies"
