Job continues speaking to his friends.

## in bitterness of soul ##

AT: "with anger and resentment" or "after living a sad life" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## They lie down alike in the dust ##

AT: "They both die and people bury them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])

## the worms cover them both ##

"and the worms in the dirt eat their dead bodies"
