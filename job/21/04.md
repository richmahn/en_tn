Job continues speaking to his friends.

## As for me, is my complaint to a person? Why should I not be impatient? ##

Job uses questions to emphasize that he thinks it is fair for him to complain to God. AT: "I am not complaining to a person. I have the right to be annoyed." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## and be astonished ##

AT: "and let what you see shock you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## lay your hand upon your mouth ##

AT: "sit there too amazed to speak"

## horror takes hold of my flesh ##

AT: "I shake with fear" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
