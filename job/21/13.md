Job continues speaking with his friends.

## they go down quietly to sheol ##

AT: "they die peacefully" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])

## your ways ##

This refers to how God wants people to behave.

## What is the Almighty, that we should worship him? What advantage would we get if we prayed to him? ##

The wicked people use these questions to mock God. AT: "We will not worship this Almighty God. He can do nothing for us" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
