## translationNotes

* **Hear diligently my speech** - "Listen carefully to what I am about to say"
* **let this be your consolations** - "let this be how you comfort me." Here "your" is plural and refers to Job's friends. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **Suffer me** - "Allow me"
* **mock on** - "you may continue mocking me"
