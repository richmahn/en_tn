## hidden from the eyes of all living things ##

AT: "Wisdom is not a thing that can be observed by man or beast. For example, a bird cannot watch it as it flies over the ground. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## destruction and death ##

Both "death" and "destruction" are represented as saying that they have only "heard" about wisdom. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])
