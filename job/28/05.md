## out of which bread comes ##

AT: "where food is produced," (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## it is turned up below as if by fire. ##

AT: "but below the earth it looks like everything has been heated by fire and crushed"

## it is turned ... its stones ... its dust ##

The word "it" refers to the "earth".

## sapphires ##

This word refers to a type of rare and expensive blue gem stone.
