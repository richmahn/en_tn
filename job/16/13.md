## pierces my kidneys...pours out my bile ##

These are parts of Job's body that he mentions to describe himself. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## smashes through my wall ##

Job's "wall" means his defenses in his speeches to protect himself. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## like a warrior ##

Job describes God as a soldier that attacks Job. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
