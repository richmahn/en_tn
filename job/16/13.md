## translationNotes

* **pierces my kidneys...pours out my bile** - These are parts of Job's body that he mentions to describe himself. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **smashes through my wall** - Job's "wall" means his defenses in his speeches to protect himself. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **like a warrior** - Job describes God as a soldier that attacks Job. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
