## scoff ##

"scorn" This word means to disrespect and to hate expressed in rudeness or crudeness.

## my eye pours out tears ##

Job describes how strongly he feels his sorrow. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])

## as a person pleads for his neighbor ##

Job describes how he wants that one in heaven to plead for him. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## this man ##

Job refers to himself.
