Zophar continues speaking to Job.

## His children ##

"The wicked person's children"

## his hands ##

Here "hands" refers to the wicked man's children. When he dies his children will to have to return for him everything he took from the poor. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## His bones are full of youthful strength ##

AT: "For a little while he may be young and strong"

## but it will lie down with him in the dust ##

AT: "but one day he will die" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
