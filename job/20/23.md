* Zophar continues speaking to Job.
* **to fill his belly** - "to eat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **God will rain it down on him** - God's punishment will fall upon the wicked man like rain falling on him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **liver** - a large and important human body part, which if shot through with an arrow, the wound would kill the person.
