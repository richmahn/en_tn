## translationNotes

* Zophar continues speaking to Job.
* **enjoy flowing streams of honey and butter** - The wicked person will not enjoy the good things that God gives his people, even good food. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **streams** - small rivers
* **he will not swallow it down** - AT: "he will not keep it"
* **neglected** - AT: "ignored"
