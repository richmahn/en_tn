## translationNotes

* **the bow of my strength is always new** - Job's former strength was like that of a new bow. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **dropped like water** - Job's words were like drops of water that refresh and strengthen others. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
