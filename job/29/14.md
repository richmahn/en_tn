## translationNotes

* **I put on righteousness** - Job's righteous actions were like a garment. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **I was eyes ... feet** - Job possessed a special understanding of those who were disabled.
* **I was a father to the needy** - Job cared for the poor people.
