## He stretches out the north ##

AT: "He stretches out the north side of the earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicitinfo]])

## binds up ##

To "bind up" means to gather something and tie it together.

## but the clouds are not torn under them. ##

The word "them" refers to the "waters". AT: "but the weight of the waters do not burst the clouds" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicitinfo]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
