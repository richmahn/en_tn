# See

"Look" or "Listen" or "Pay attention to what I am about to tell you"

# it cannot be built again

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "no one can rebuild it" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# if he imprisons someone, there can be no release

The abstract noun "release" can be expressed with the verb "free." Alternate translation: "if God shuts someone in, no one can free him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
