## Which animal among all these does not know ##

This is a figure of speech that does not expect an answer, but states the question to emphasize the point. It means, "Every animal knows that... " (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Yahweh, in whose hand is the life of every living thing ##

This figure of speech uses two similar statements to emphasize the idea. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
