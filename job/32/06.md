## you are very old ##

Here "you" is plural and refers to Job and his three friends. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## length of days should speak ##

Elihu describes "days" and "years" as a person who can speak. AT: "Those who are older should speak their wisdom first" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
