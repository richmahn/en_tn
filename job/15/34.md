## fire will consume their tents of bribery ##

AT: "the tents they bought with their bribes will be burned by fire"

## They conceive mischief and give birth to iniquity; their womb conceives deceit ##

The same thought is repeated three times to emphasize how much evil these people produce. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## They conceive mischief ##

AT: "Like a woman conceives a child, so they conceive mischief" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## give birth to iniquity ##

AT: "like a woman gives birth to a child, so they produce iniquity" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## their womb conceives deceit ##

AT: "they conceive deceit" or "like a woman conceives a child, so they conceive deceit" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
