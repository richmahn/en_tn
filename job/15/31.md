## his branch will not be green ##

AT: "he will look dead, just like the branch of a dead tree does not look green" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## He will drop his ... he will cast off his ... ##

These two lines give a similar image, which is repeated to emphasize that this will surely happen. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## He will drop his unripe grapes like a grapevine ##

AT: "Just like a grapevine drops its unripe grapes, so the wicked man will drop his strength" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## he will cast off his flowers like the olive tree ##

AT: "just like an olive tree casts off its flowers, so the wicked man will cast off his strength" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
