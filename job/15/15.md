## translationNotes

* **in his sight** - AT: "according to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **abominable and corrupt** - These two words basically mean the same thing and are used together to emphasize how bad humans are. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **who drinks iniquity like water** - This describes iniquity as if it were a liquid you can drink. AT: "who love iniquity as much as they love a cup of fresh water" or "who commit evil deeds as often as they drink water" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
