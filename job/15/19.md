## to whom alone the land was given ##

AT: "to whom alone God gave the land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## no stranger ever passed ##

AT: "no stranger ever corrupted their knowledge"

## twists in pain ##

This is either physical or emotional pain. AT: "suffers a lot." Other translations may read: "is overcome by fear"

## the number of years that are laid up ##

AT: "all the years that God has prepared" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
