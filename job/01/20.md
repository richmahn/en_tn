## translationNotes

* **tore his robe, shaved his head** - These are expressions of deep mourning.
* **I came naked out of my mother's womb, and I return there naked** - AT: "At my birth, I brought nothing into the world, and at my death I will return to the earth with nothing."
* **In all this matter** - "In spite of all that happened" (See: UDB)
