## translationNotes

* Job continues speaking.
* **the eye of the adulterer** - AT: "the adulterer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **for the twilight** - "for the sunset"
* **No eye will see me** - AT: "No one will see me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **For the morning is to all of them like thick darkness** - For the wicked, the light of the morning means the same thing as thick darkness means for normal people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **with the terrors of the thick darkness** - AT: "with the scary things that happen at night"
