## translationNotes

* This concludes Job's speech.
* **they will be brought low** - AT: "God will bring them low" or "God will destroy them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **they will be gathered up like all the others** - AT: "people will gather them up like they gathered up the other wicked people." Other translations may have: "they disappear like weeds wither and die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_textvariants]])
* **they will be cut off like the tops of the ears of grain** - AT: "God will cut them off like a farmer cuts off the top of a stalk of grain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **If it is not so, who can prove me to be a liar; who can make my speech worth nothing?** - The implicit answer is: "no one." AT: "This is true, no one can prove that I am a liar; no one can prove me wrong" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
