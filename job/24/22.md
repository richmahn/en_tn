## by his power ##

AT: "by using his power" or "because he is powerful" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## he rises up and does not strengthen them in life ##

AT: "God rises up and does not give wicked people the strenght to live"

## but his eyes are on their ways ##

AT: "but he is always watching what they do" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
