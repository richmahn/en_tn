# their faces

The "face" represents the whole person. AT: "them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# the hidden place

a euphemism for the place where people's spirits go when they die (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
