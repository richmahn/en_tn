## like a cedar ##

AT: "the way the branches of a cedar tree move" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## like tubes of brass ##

Here, his bones are compared to tubes made of bronze to show how tough this animal is. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## like bars of iron ##

This last comparisons describes the strength of this great animal. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
