## Will you condemn me so that you may claim that you are right? ##

AT: "You are condemning me so you can claim that you are innocent." (see: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## arm like God's ##

## can you thunder with a voice like him ##

AT: "You certainly do not have a powerful voice like God's, who causes thunder". (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]] [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
