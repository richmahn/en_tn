## house like a spider ##

A spider's web is easily destroyed because it is not strong. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## like a spider ##

AT: "like a spider builds its house" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

## like a hut ##

A hut is a temporary house that is also not very strong. AT: "like a temporary hut" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## he opens his eyes, and everything is gone. ##

AT: "he will open his eyes and all of his riches will be gone"
