## Is it at your orders...nest in high places? ##

The implicit answer to this question is "no." AT: "It is not at your orders...nest in high places." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Is it at your orders ##

AT: "Is it because you tell it to do so" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

## mounts up ##

AT: "flies up into the sky" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## a stronghold ##

The high cliffs are strongholds for eagles because the animals that would want to eat them cannot reach them.
