Yahweh continues to challenge Job.

## Have you given the horse...a locust? ##

Yahweh uses these questions to emphasize that he is the one who does these things and that Job is not. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## clothe his neck ##

The word "cloth" is a way of describing how Yahweh made the neck of the horse. AT: "make his neck." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## his flowing mane ##

the long hair that comes down from the neck of a horse and shakes when the horse moves

## a locust ##

a large kind of grasshopper that can jump very far and very quickly

## snorting ##

a very loud sound that horses make with their nose
