Yahweh continues to challenge Job.

## Who let...the salt land ##

Yahweh uses these questions to emphasize that he takes care of the wild donkeys and that Job does not. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

##  the wild donkey...the swift donkey ##

These are different names for the same kind of donkey.

## bonds ##

ropes, chains, or straps that hold an animal and keep it from running away

## whose home ##

Yahweh describes the donkey as though he were a person that had a house. AT: "I gave him the Arabah as a place to live." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the salt land ##

the land around the Salt Sea that has a lot of salt in it
