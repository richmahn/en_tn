# General Information:

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel.

# went far from me ... wandered away from me, going after their idols

The way a person lives is spoken of as walking on a path. AT: "stopped worshiping me ... stopped doing what I wanted them to do. Instead, they worshiped their idols" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
