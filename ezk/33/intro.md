# Ezekiel 33 General Notes #

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Repentance #####

God does not want sinners to die. He wants them to repent and live. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/sin]] and [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/repent]])
