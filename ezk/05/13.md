Yahweh continues to speak to the people of Israel and Judah.

## I will cause my fury toward them to rest ##

The word "fury" means violent anger, and here it refers to punishment. "I will stop punishing them." ([[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## I will be satisfied ##

"I will be satisfied that I have punished them enough"

## when I have completed my fury against them ##

"when I have finished punishing them"
