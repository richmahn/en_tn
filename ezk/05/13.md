## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues to speak to the people of Israel and Judah.
* **I will cause my fury toward them to rest** - The word "fury" means violent anger, and here it refers to punishment. "I will stop punishing them." ([[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **I will be satisfied** - "I will be satisfied that I have punished them enough"
* **when I have completed my fury against them** - "when I have finished punishing them"
