# the front of the lower gate to the front of the inner gate

The lower gate was on the wall of the outer courtyard, and the inner gate was on the wall of the inner courtyard. AT: "the front of the outer gate to the front of the inner gate"

# one hundred cubits

about 54 meters (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bdistance]])

# cubits

See how you translated these "long" cubits in [Ezekiel 40:05](./05.md).

# the same on the north side

"it was the same on the north side" or "on the north side, the distance from the outer gate to the inner gate was also one hundred cubits"
