## cubits ##

See how you translated these "long" cubits in [Ezekiel 40:5](./05.md).

## one and a half cubits ##

About 0.8 meter. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/other/biblicaldistance]])

## one cubit ##

about one-half meter

## Two-pronged hooks a span long were fastened in the portico all around ##

AT: "All around the portico, people had attached hooks that were a span long and had two prongs each" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## two-pronged hooks ##

something that has two long curved points, which people can hang things on

## a span long ##

about eight centimeters. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/other/biblicaldistance]]).
