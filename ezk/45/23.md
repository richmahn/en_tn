# General Information:

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the princes of Israel.

# food offering

This is also called a "grain offering."

# Seven bulls and seven unblemished rams

"Seven bulls and seven rams that are completely healthy"

# an ephah

You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "twenty-two liters" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])

# a hin

You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "four liters" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])

# each ephah

You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "each twenty-two liters" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bvolume]])
