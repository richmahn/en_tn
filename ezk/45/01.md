# General Information:

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel.

# twenty-five thousand cubits in length ... ten thousand cubits in width ... five hundred cubits ... fifty cubits in width

Each cubit was about 54 centimeters. See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 40:5](../40/05.md). AT: "25,000 cubits long ... 10,000 cubits wide ... 500 cubits ... 50 cubits wide" or "about 13.5 kilometers long ... about 5.4 kilometers wide ... about 270 meters ... about 27 meters wide" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bdistance]])

# all its area round about

"all of the area inside of the borders around it"
