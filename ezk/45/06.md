# General Information:

Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the house of Israel.

# five thousand cubits ... twenty-five thousand

Each long cubit was about 54 centimeters. See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 40:5](../40/05.md). Alternate translation: "5,000 cubits ... 25,000 cubits" or "about 2.7 kilometers ... about 13.5 kilometers" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bdistance]])

# reserved for the holy place

This can be translated in active form. Alternate translation: "that you gave for the holy place" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
