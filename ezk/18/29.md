## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues to teach the people of Israel why his way is fair.
* **How is my way not fair, house of Israel? ** - <b>How is my way not fair, house of Israel? <b>- AT: "My way is certainly fair, people of Israel." (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]]).
* **How is your way not fair? - ** - </b>  AT: "It is your ways that are not fair" (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **Each man among you according to his own way ** - <b>Each man among you according to his own way <b>- AT: "the actions of each man."
* **so that they will not** - "they" refers to all the transgressions of the people of Israel.
