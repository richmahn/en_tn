## What do you mean...‘Fathers eat sour grapes, and the children’s teeth made blunt’? ##

AT: "Children are punished by God because of their parents' wicked deeds." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]], [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## land of Israel ##

This refers to the people of Israel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
