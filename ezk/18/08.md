Yahweh continues to describe the actions of the righteous man.

## interest ##

This word refers to the money paid by a person to use borrowed money.

## injustice ##

the unfair treatment of all people under the laws of God and country.

## he turns his hand to justice ##

The phrase "turns his hand" describes the effort of working to establish justice (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
