# General Information:

Yahweh continues speaking to Ezekiel.

# David my servant

Yahweh said "David" to refer to a descendant of David. See how you translated "my servant David" in [Ezekiel 34:23](../34/23.md). AT: "A descendant of my servant David" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# one shepherd over them

This speaks of a king ruling a people as if he were a shepherd leading sheep. See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 34:23](../34/23.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# over them

"over the people of Israel"

# will walk according to my decrees

This speaks of acting or behaving in a certain way as if it were a person walking. AT: "will live as I have commanded" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
