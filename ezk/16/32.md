Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem and the people of Israel as his unfaithful wife.

## accept strangers ##

This refers to accepting someone as a sexual partner. AT: "accept strangers for sex" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## sleep with them ##

This is a polite way to refer to having sex. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])
