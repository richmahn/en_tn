Yahweh continues to refer to other nations as relatives of Jerusalem.

## you have shown that your sisters were better than you ##

Yahweh uses this phrase three times.  Repeating this phrase shows how important it is. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## Especially you, show your own shame ##

Yahweh uses this phrase two times. This is repeated to show how important it is that the people of Israel understand that their actions are wrong. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
