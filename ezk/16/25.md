Yahweh continues to speak about Jerusalem and the people of Israel as his unfaithful wife.

## head of every road  ##

The beginning of the road is referred to as its head. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## you opened up your legs ##

describes a woman's willingness to have sex. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
