## the word of Yahweh came to me and said ##

See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 3:16](../03/16.md).

## 'The days are prolonged, and every vision fails' ##

This was a saying of the people of Israel who did not believe that God would judge them.  AT: "Many days have passed without God's judgment, the prophet's warnings are false" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## Thus says the Lord Yahweh ##

See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 5:5](../05/05.md).

## The days draw near  ##

This phrase refers to the days in which Israel will be judged. AT: "The days of judgment draw near" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## every vision will speak ##

The visions God gives to the prophets will come true. AT: "every prophecy will happen" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])
