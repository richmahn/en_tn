## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to Pharaoh.
* **they were scattered** - AT: "I scattered them." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **will restore the fortunes of Egypt** - "will give Egypt what it had lost"
* **the region of Pathros** - the southern part of Egypt between the Delta and the land of Cush. This is also called Upper Egypt. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
