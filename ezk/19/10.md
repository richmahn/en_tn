## mother ##

Yahweh describes the nation of Israel as though it was the mother of the princes of Israel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## in your blood ##

It is implied that this is the blood of grapes. AT: "in the blood of your grapes" or "in your wine." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
