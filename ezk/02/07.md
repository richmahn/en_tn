# General Information:

God continues to speak to Ezekiel.

# are most rebellious

"are very rebellious" or "totally rebel against me"

# house

This is a metonym for the family that lives in the house, in this case the Israelites, the descendants of Jacob over many years. See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 2:5](./04.md). AT: "people group" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
