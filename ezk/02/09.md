## translationNotes

* **a written scroll** - <b>a written scroll</b> "a scroll that had writing on it"
* **He spread it out** - The word "He" refers to the Spirit, who opens the scroll.
* **words were written on both sides of the scroll** - "writing was on the front and back of the scroll"
* **mourning** - great sorrow and sadness such as what people feel when someone dies
