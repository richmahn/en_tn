## translationNotes

* **lift up your eyes** - "look" or "turn your head and look" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **do you see what they are doing** - God uses this question to bring Ezekiel's attention to what the people were doing. Alternate translation: "do you understand what the people here are doing."
