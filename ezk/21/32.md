Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Ammon.

## You will become fuel for the fire ##

"The fire will burn you up"

## You ##

The word "your" is feminine singular and refers to the nation of Ammon. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-gendernotations]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-yousingular]])
