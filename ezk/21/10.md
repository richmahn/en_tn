Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message to the people of Jerusalem and of the land of Israel.

## Should we rejoice in my son's scepter ##

AT: "The people of Judah will not celebrate about their king's scepter." (UDB) (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Should we ##

The word "we" refers to Yahweh and the people of Israel.

## in my son's scepter ##

This phrase means "in the power of the king of Judah." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
