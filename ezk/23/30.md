acting like a prostitute, lusting after nations** - The reason this phrase says Oholibah acts like a prostitute with the nations is because the men she has had sex with are symbols of the wealth and power of these nations. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## I will put her cup ##

The word "cup" refers to Oholibah's punishment.  It represents the thing she has received. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
