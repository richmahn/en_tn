# General Information:

Yahweh continues his metaphor in which he speaks of the cities of Jerusalem and Samaria as if they were two prostitutes. This metaphor continues through [Ezekiel 23:34](../23/34.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# Behold!

"Listen!" The word "behold" here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.

# I will bring them against you from every side

"I will cause them to attack you from all directions"
