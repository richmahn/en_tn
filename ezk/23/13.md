# unclean

A person who God considers spiritually unacceptable or defiled is spoken of as if the person were physically unclean. See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 23:7](./07.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# It was the same for both sisters

This refers to how they had both made themselves unclean. AT: "Both sisters became unclean through their acts of prostitution" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
