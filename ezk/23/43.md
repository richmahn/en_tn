## the one worn out by acts of adultery ##

Ezekiel refers to Oholah and Ohobilah with a description.

## penetrated her as they would any prostitute ##

"went into her as they would any prostitute" This phrase describes when a man has sex with a woman. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])

##  as they would any prostitute ##

AT: "as they would penetrate any prostitute"

## have blood on their hands ##

This phrase uses evidence of violence to say they are guilty of violence.
