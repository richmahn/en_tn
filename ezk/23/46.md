## raise up a company ##

The phrase "raise up" refers to increase in the number of people. AT: "gather a large group of people"

## give them over ##

Yahweh gives up the responsibility to take care of them and allows them to suffer for their choices.

## to be terrorized and plundered ##

AT: "for the company to terrorize and plunder them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
