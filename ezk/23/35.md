## translationNotes

* **and thrown me away behind your back** - Yahweh is saying Oholibah does not care about him.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **you will lift high and reveal** - Both of these phrases describe one action.  AT: "you will put on display" or "you will make it clear for everyone to see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
