## Will it prosper?...will it grow? ##

AT: "It will not grow."  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Won't he pull up its roots and pluck out its fruit so all of its growth of leaves will wither away? ##

AT: "He will pull up its roots and pluck out its fruit so all of its growth of leaves will wither away."  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## behold ##

The word "behold" here adds emphasis to what follows. ("indeed", "look" or "listen" or "pay attention to what I am about to tell you."

## Will it not wither when the eastern wind touches it? ##

AT: "It will wither when the eastern wind touches it." (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]]).
