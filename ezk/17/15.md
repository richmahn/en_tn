Yahweh speaks about the king of Jerusalem rebelling against the king of Babylon.

## Will he succeed? -  ##

</b> - AT: "He will certainly not succeed" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Will the one doing these things escape? ##

AT: "The one doing these things will not escape!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## If he violates the covenant, will he escape? ##

AT: "If he violates the covenant, he will certainly not escape!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]]) "Violate" means to do what was agreed on.
