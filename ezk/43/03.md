## translationNotes

* **And it was like** - The word "it" refers to the vision of the glory of God coming from the east.
* **when he had come to destroy the city** - Possible meanings are 1) "when the God of Israel had come to destroy the city" or 2) "when I had come to see the God of Israel destroy the city."
