## Gog ##

This is the name of a leader or king who ruled in the land of Magog. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## the land of Magog ##

It is implied that this is the land that Gog rules over. AT: "who rules over the land of Magog." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## Magog ##

the name of an ancient nation that probably lived in the land that is now Turkey. Magog might be the ancient nation of Lydia.
