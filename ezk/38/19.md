Yahweh continues giving Ezekiel his message about Gog.

## in the ardor of my anger ##

"with passionate anger" or "with jealous anger"

## with the fire of my rage ##

This phrase means "with intense anger." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## The mountains will be thrown down ##

"The earthquake will make mountains fall down." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]]
