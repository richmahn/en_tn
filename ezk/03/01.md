Ezekiel continues to tell about the vision that he saw.

## He said to me ##

The word "He" refers to the Spirit of God. (See: [Ezekiel 2:1](../02/01.md)

## Son of man ##

See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 2:1](../02/01.md).

## what you have found ##

This refers to the scroll that God was giving him. (See: [Ezekiel 2:9](../02/09.md))

## the house of Israel ##

This refers to "the descendants of Israel" who became "the kingdom of Israel." Alternate translation "the people of Israel."

## feed your stomach and fill your belly with this scroll ##

These two phrases mean the same thing.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## It was as sweet as honey ##

"It tasted sweet like honey"
