## this city is the pot, and we are the meat ##

The wall around the city protects the people as a pot protects the food in it. AT: "This city is like a pot that will protect us as a pot protects meat." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## the pot ##

Possible meanings are 1) a pot for storing meat or 2) a pot for cooking meat.
