## translationNotes

* Yahweh continues to talk to the prophet Ezekiel.
* **house of Israel** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:03:01]].
* **as you are saying** - God was referring to what the people were saying in 11:3. AT: "It will be as you are saying" or "You are saying those things"
* **The people you have killed...are the meat, and this city is the pot** - AT: "The people you have killed ... are like the meat in the pot, and this city is the pot. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
