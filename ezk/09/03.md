# from the cherubim where it had been

Possible meanings are 1) "from above the four winged creatures" ([Ezekiel 1:5](../01/05.md)) or 2) from between the two cherubim in the most holy place in the temple. Try to translate this literally. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# threshold of the house

The "house" refers to God's temple.

See how you translated this in [Ezekiel 9:2](./02.md).
