## he spoke to the others within my hearing ##

The word "others" refers to the guards. (See: [Ezekiel 9:1](./01.md))

## Do not let your eyes have compassion ##

"Do not have compassion on the people you see."

## do not spare ##

"do not refrain from killing"

## mark on his head ##

These were the people who groaned about the abominations happening in Jerusalem.  See how you translated "mark" in [Ezekiel 9:3](./03.md).

## Begin at my sanctuary ##

"Begin to kill the ones who do not have the mark at my sanctuary"  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
