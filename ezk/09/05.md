## translationNotes

* **he spoke to the others within my hearing** - The word "others" refers to the guards. (See: [[en:bible:notes:ezk:09:01]])
* **Do not let your eyes have compassion** - "Do not have compassion on the people you see."
* **do not spare** - "do not refrain from killing"
* **mark on his head** - These were the people who groaned about the abominations happening in Jerusalem.  See how you translated "mark" in [[en:bible:notes:ezk:09:03]].
* **Begin at my sanctuary** - "Begin to kill the ones who do not have the mark at my sanctuary"  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
