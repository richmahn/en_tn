# General Information:

Yahweh continues to speak to Pharaoh and to compare him to a monster that lives in the water.

# your worm-filled corpse

The worms help the dead body to rot. AT: "your rotting body" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# the stream beds will be filled with your blood

This can be put into active form. AT: "I will fill the streams with your blood" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
