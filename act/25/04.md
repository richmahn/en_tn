## that Paul was a prisoner in Caesarea ##

<b>that Paul was a prisoner in Caesarea<b>- This is an indirect speech quote. Alternated translation: "Paul is a prisoner in Caesarea, and I myself will soon return there." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-quotations]])
