## translationNotes

* **into the temple** - "into the temple area" or "to the temple." They did not go into the inner building which only the serving priests were allowed.
* **the ninth hour** - "three o'clock in the afternoon" (UDB). (See: [[en:obe:other:biblicaltime]])
* **alms** - "Alms" refers to money that people give to the poor. The man was poor and was asking for money for himself.
