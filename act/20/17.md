## From Miletus ##

See how you translated Miletus in Acts [Acts 20:15](./15.md).

## set foot in Asia  ##

<b>set foot in Asia <b>- "entered this region of Asia"

## lowliness of mind ##

"humility" or "humbleness"

## with tears ##

I sometimes cried as I served the Lord.

## keep back ##

"hold back" or "withhold"

## going from house to house ##

<b>going from house to house<b>- That is, he taught people in various private homes.

## repentance toward God ##

"turn from their sins toward God"
