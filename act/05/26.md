## translationNotes

* **council** - This was the most important Jewish Council, which was also known as the Sanhedrin.
* **Bring this man's blood upon us** - "hold us responsible for the death of this man" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **charged you** - Here the word "you" is plural. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **you have filled** - <b>you have filled</b> The word "you" here is plural. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
