## council ##

This was the most important Jewish Council, which was also known as the Sanhedrin.

## Bring this man's blood upon us ##

"hold us responsible for the death of this man" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## charged you ##

Here the word "you" is plural. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## you have filled ##

<b>you have filled</b> The word "you" here is plural. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])
