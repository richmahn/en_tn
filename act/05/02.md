# laid it at the apostles' feet

This means that they presented to money to the apostles. See how you translated this in [Acts 4:35](../04/35.md). AT: "presented it to the apostles" or "gave it to the apostles" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
