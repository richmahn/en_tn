## give repentance to Israel, and forgiveness of sins ##

<b>give repentance to Israel, and forgiveness of sins<b>-This could also be translated as, "give Israel the opportunity to turn from their sin and receive forgiveness for their sins"

## So is the Holy Spirit ##

<b>So is the Holy Spirit<b>-The Holy Spirit is spoken of as a person who can testify to validate the miraculous work of Jesus
