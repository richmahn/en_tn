## translationNotes

* **Right away he took ** - "Right away the chief captain"
* **ran down** - From the fortress, there are stairs going down into the court.
* **the chief captain** - The military officer or leader of about 600 soldiers.
* **laid hold on Paul** - "took hold of Paul" or "arrested Paul"
