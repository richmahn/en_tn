# The following night

This means the night after the day Paul went before the council. AT: "That night"

# bear witness in Rome

The words "about me" are understood. AT: "bear witness about me in Rome" or "testify about me in Rome" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
