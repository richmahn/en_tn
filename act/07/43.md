Stephen continues his defense before the Jewish council that he began in [Acts 7:1](./01.md). Stephen here continues a quotation of Amos [Amos 5:25](../../amo/05/25.md) that he started in [Acts 7:41](./41.md).

## tabernacle of Molech ##

The tabernacle or tent that housed the false god Molech.

## the star of the god Rephan ##

the star that is identified to the false god Rephan.

## Images that you made ##

They made statues or images of the gods Molech and Rephan for the purpose to worship them.

## I will carry you away beyond Babylon ##

"I will remove you to Babylon"
