## he forced Paul out of our hands ##

"The soldiers of Lusias forced Paul out of our custody." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]]) "Forced" has the sense of using violent or strong force.

## When you question Paul ##

"When you interrogate Paul" or "question as in a court trial"

## we accuse him of ##

"accuse Paul of doing" or "accuse Paul of being guilty of doing"
