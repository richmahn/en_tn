## translationNotes

* **Paul would give money to him** - "Paul would give money to Felix." Felix was hoping Paul would offer him a bribe to set him free.
* ** so he often sent for him and spoke with him** - "so Felix often sent for Paul and spoke with Paul"
* ** Porcius Festus** - The new roman Governor who replaced Felix. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
