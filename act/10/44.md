## translationNotes

* **fell on all of those who were listening-  ** - **"all" likely refers to all the non-Jews at the house who were believing Peter. Favoring literal rather than hyperbole.
* **the gift** - "the free gift"
* **the Holy Spirit was poured out** - God poured out the Holy Spirit. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
