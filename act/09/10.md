## Now there was ##

This begins a new part of the story with the introduction of a new character.

## disciple ##

Anyone who believes in Jesus and obeys his teachings is called a disciple of Jesus.

## Ananias ##

A disciple of Jesus who obeyed God by going to Saul, and healed Saul by laying his hands on Saul.

## house of Judas ##

Judas was owner of a house in Damascus where Ananias stayed. Though there are several Judases mentioned in the New Testament, it is likely this is his only appearance.
