## translationNotes

* **do not be silent** - "do not stop speaking about the gospel"
* **for I am** - "I" refers to the Lord, who is speaking to Paul.
* **with you** - "you" refers to Paul, to whom the Lord is speaking in a vision.
* **I have many people in this city** - "There are many people in this city who have put their faith in Me"
