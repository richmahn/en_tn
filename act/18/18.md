## sailed for Syria with Priscilla and Aquila  ##

Paul got on a ship that sailed out for Syria. Priscilla and Aquila went along with him.

## the seaport, Cenchreae ##

Cenchreae was a seaport that was part of the greater Corinth city area. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Nazarite vow ##

This was a promise or oath to do something for God outlined in the Law of Moses. It allowed a person outside the tribe of Levi to serve God.
