## After these things ##

"After these events took place in Athens"

## There he found  ##

Possible meanings are 1) "There Paul happened to find by chance" or 2) "There Paul found after seeking with intent."

## Pontus by race ##

A province on the southern coast of the Black Sea.

## had recently come ##

This is probably sometime in the past year.

## Claudius had commanded ##

The current Roman emperor. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## commanded all the Jews ##

"ordered" or "instructed"
