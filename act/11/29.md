## as each one was able  ##

<b>as each one was able <b>- The richer people sent more; the poorer people sent less.

## They did this; they sent money ##

<b>They did this; they sent money</b> "the believers in Antioch donated money and they sent the money..."

## by the hand of Barnabas and Saul  ##

<b>by the hand of Barnabas and Saul <b>- This is an expression for "Barnabas and Saul personally brought the money to the church elders in Jerusalem" (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
