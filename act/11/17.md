(Peter finishes his speech in v17)

## God gave to them ##

"Them" from the context of the story are Cornelius and his Gentile contacts. However, Peter does not call them Gentiles in his account to the Jewish believers at Jerusalem.

## the same gift ##

Peter is referring to the gift of the Holy Spirit.

## who was I, that I could oppose God? ##

I could not oppose God. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## when they heard these things  ##

"they", the circumcision group who were criticizing Peter

## given repentance for life  ##

"given repentance leading to life"
