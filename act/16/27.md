## he, himself, he ##

These pronouns refer to the jailer throughout these verses.

## was about to kill himself ##

"was ready to kill himself". The jailer preferred to commit suicide rather than suffer the consequences of letting the prisoners escape.

## we are all here ##

The word "we" is inclusive and refers to Paul, Silas and all of the other prisoners. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])
