## translationNotes

* **we made** - The word "we" throughout these verses refers to Paul and his companions. Luke, the author of Acts, was one of Paul's companions at this time. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_inclusive]])
* ** Samothrace...Neapolis** - Costal cities near Phillipi. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **a Roman colony** - A place that Rome conquered and occupied at that time, particularly with soldiers.
