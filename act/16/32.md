Paul and Silas continue their involvement with the Jailer and his household.

## in his house ##

"in his home". The Jailer brought Paul and Silas to his home.

## he and all his household were baptized ##

This sentence can be turned from the passive to the active form: "Paul and Silas baptized the jailer and all members of his household." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## he, his ##

These pronouns refer to the jailer.

## because they had all believed ##

"because all members of his household had believed."
