## translationNotes

* **Phrygia and Galatia** - These are provinces in Asia. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Mysia... Bithynia** - These are two more provinces in Asia.
* **they had been forbidden by the Holy Spirit** - "the Holy Spirit had forbidden them" or "the Holy Spirit did not permit them" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **the Spirit of Jesus** - "the Holy Spirit"
