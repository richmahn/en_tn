# General Information:

Here the word "we" refers to Peter and John but not to those whom they are addressing. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])

# Whether it is right in the sight of God

Here the phrase "in the sight of God" refers to God's opinion. AT: "Whether God thinks it is right" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
