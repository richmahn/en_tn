## translationNotes

* **amazed and perplexed ** - <b>amazed and perplexed <b>- The people did not know what to think about what was happening (UDB). It can be translated as, "surprised and confused."
* **What does this mean?** - Some took the event seriously.
* **But others mocked** - "But others insulted" or "put down"
* **They are full of new wine** - This can be translated as "They are drunk." Some chose to not believe the miracle, but to mock the apostles.
* **new wine** - a stronger drink than normal wine
