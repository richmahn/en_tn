## amazed and perplexed  ##

The people did not know what to think about what was happening (UDB). It can be translated as, "surprised and confused."

## What does this mean? ##

Some people were amazed at what was happening. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## But others mocked ##

"But others insulted" or "put down"

## They are full of new wine ##

This can be translated as "They are drunk." Some chose to not believe the miracle, but to mock the apostles.

## new wine ##

a stronger drink than normal wine
