## godly men ##

people who wanted to honor and or worship God

## every nation under heaven ##

"every nation in the world"

## when this sound was heard ##

This refers to the sound that was like a strong wind. This can be translated with an active verb: "when they heard this sound." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## the multitude ##

"the large crowd of people"

## Galileans ##

This can be translated as "from Galilee."
