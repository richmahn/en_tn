## when Peter came to himself ##

"When Peter became fully awake and alert" or "When Peter became aware that what had happened was real"

## delivered me out of the hand of Herod ##

"…the hand of Herod" is an expression for "rescued me from the harm Herod had planned for me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the expectation of the people of the Jews ##

"what the Jewish leaders thought would happen to me"

## realized this ##

"became aware of this truth"

## mother of John whose surname was Mark ##

"…who was also called Mark"
