# Connecting Statement:

James begins to speak to the apostles and elders. (See: [Acts 15:6](./05.md))

# General Information:

Here the word "they" refers to Paul and Barnabas. (See: [Acts 15:12](./12.md))

# Brothers

"Fellow believers"

# in order to take from them a people

"so that he might choose from among them a people"

# for his name

"for God's name." Here "name" refers to God. AT: "for himself" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
