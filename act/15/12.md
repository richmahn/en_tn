# General Information:

Here the word "them" refers to Paul and Barnabas.

# All the multitude

"Everyone" or "The whole group" (See: [Acts 15:6](./05.md))

# God had worked

"God had done" or "God had caused"

# a people for his name

"Name" is a metonymy for "God's own purposes." AT: "a people for God's own purposes" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
