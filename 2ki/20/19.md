## For he thought ##

"Because Hezekiah thought"

## Will there not be peace and stability in my days? ##

"There will be peace and stability in my days." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]]) Hezekiah asks the question to show that he has nothing to worry about. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## pool ##

## conduit ##

A tunnel for carrying the water

## are they not written in The Book of the Events of the Kings of Judah?  ##

"yes, they are written in The Book of the Kings of Judah." The writer asks the question to emphasize that they are written. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
