# call to mind

This is a common way of speaking, an idiom, urging Yahweh to remember. Alternate translation: "remember" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# walked

This is an idiom. Alternate translation: "lived my life" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# good in your sight

Here sight represents judgment or evaluation. Alternate translation: "in your judgment" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
