## translationNotes

* **Shall the shadow go forward ten steps, or go back ten steps?"** - AT: "Do you want Yahweh to make the shadow caused by the sunlight on the stairway to go forward ten steps or to go back ten steps?" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]]).  Steps could also be translated "degrees".
