## translationNotes

* **So Isaiah said to Hezekiah** - AT: "So, because Isaiah knew Hezekiah had been foolish to show the men all his valuable things, Isaiah said to him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]])
* **Look, the days are about to come when** - AT: "Listen to me, someday there will come a time when";  "Look" is used to draw attention to what Isaiah is about to tell Hezekiah.
* **the days** - This refers to an undefined span of time. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
