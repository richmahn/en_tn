## translationNotes

* **Yahweh had not said he would blot out the name of Israel from under heaven** - Here "the name of Israel" represents all of Israel and its inhabitants. AT: "not destroy Israel completely," (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **he saved them by the hand of Jeroboam** - "he enabled King Jeroboam to rescue them" (UDB)
