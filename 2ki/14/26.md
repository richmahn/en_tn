## Yahweh had not said he would blot out the name of Israel from under heaven ##

Here "the name of Israel" represents all of Israel and its inhabitants. AT: "not destroy Israel completely," (UDB) (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## he saved them by the hand of Jeroboam ##

"he enabled King Jeroboam to rescue them" (UDB)
