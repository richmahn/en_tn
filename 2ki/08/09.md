# carried by forty camels

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "which forty camels carried"

# forty camels

"40 camels" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# Your son Ben Hadad king of Aram

Ben Hadad was not actually Elisha's son, but Hazael called him that to show a close relationship between them. Alternate translation: "Ben Hadad, the king of Aram, who is like a son to you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
