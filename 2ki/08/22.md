King Jehoram of Judah dies and his son Ahaziah becomes king.

## So Edom has been in rebellion from the power of Judah to this present day ##

AT: "So the people of Edom have been independent from the rule of Judah from that day on." or "Edom became a separate country from Judah from that time on." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

AT: "Jehoram died and was buried with his ancestors" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])

## Then Ahaziah his son became king in his place ##

"Then Ahaziah, Jehoram's son, became king after Jehoram died."
