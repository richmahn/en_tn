# General Information:

Jehoram becomes king of Judah.

# In the fifth year of Joram son of Ahab, king of Israel

This describes the time that Jehoram began to reign by stating how long Joram, the current king of Israel, had reigned. AT: "In the fifth year that Jorman son of Ahab was king of Israel" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# fifth year

"5th year" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-ordinal]])

# Jehoram began to reign

Jehoram, son of Jehoshaphat, became the king of Judah.

# thirty-two years old

"32 years old" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
