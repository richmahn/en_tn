## Call this Shunammite ##

Shunammite refers to the woman from Shunem that Elisha was staying with.

## You have gone to all this trouble to care for us. What can be done for you? ##

AT: "You have shown much kindness and concern for us. What can we do for you?"

## I live among my own people ##

AT: "I live surrounded by my family, and because they take care of me, I have no needs." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicitinfo]])
