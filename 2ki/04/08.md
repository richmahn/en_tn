# Shunem

This is the name of a city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# she urged him to eat food with her

This means that she asked him to stop and have a meal at her house. Alternate translation: "she asked him to come to her house to have a meal" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
