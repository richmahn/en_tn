## Was not my spirit with you when the man turned his chariot to meet you? ##

AT: "You should have realized that my spirit could see you when Naaman stopped his chariot and talked to you?"  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Is this a time to accept money and clothes, olive groves and vineyards, sheep and oxen, and male servants and female servants? ##

AT: "This is not the time to accept... and female servants." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## the leprosy of Naaman will be on you and your descendants ##

AT: "the same disease Naaman had, you and your future family members will have."

## as white as snow ##

Leprosy makes skin white, like the color of snow. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
