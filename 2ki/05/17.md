## let there be given to your servant ##

"let me have"; (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## two mule loads of earth ##

"as much soil from Israel as two mules can carry, so that I can build an altar to Yahweh." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicitinfo]])

## when my king ##

This is referring to the king of Aram, the king that Namaan works for.

## Go in peace ##

AT: "Leave without fear"
