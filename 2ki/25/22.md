## Nebuzaradan, Gedaliah, Ahikam, Shaphan, Ishmael, Nethaniah, Johanan, Kareah, Seraiah, Tanhumeth, Jaazaniah ##

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Netophathite ##

This means that Tanhumeth is from the family or clan of Netophah. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Maacathite ##

This means that Jaazaniah's father was from a place called Maacah. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
