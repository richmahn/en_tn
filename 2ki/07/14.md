## "Go and see" ##

AT: "Go and see what has happened to the Aramean army." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicitinfo]])

## They went after them to the Jordan ##

AT: "They followed the path the Aramean army took all the way to the Jordan River,"

## all the road was full of clothes and equipment ##

AT: "along the road, there was much discarded clothing and equipment" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])

## that the Arameans had cast away in their hurry ##

"that the Arameans had thrown away in their rush to escape."
