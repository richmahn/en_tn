## translationNotes

* **Now** - AT: "At that same time"
* **Why should we sit here until we die?** - AT: "Certainly we should not sit here until we die" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **let us go to the army of the Arameans** - AT: "let us go to the place where the army of the Arameans has their camp."
* **If they keep us alive, we will live, and if they kill us, we will only die** - AT: "If the Arameans give us food, we will live, and if they kill us, we were only going to die here anyway." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]])
