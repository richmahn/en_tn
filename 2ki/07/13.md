# the horses that remain, which are left in the city

Many of the horses belonging to the Israelites had died because of the famine. The meaning of this can be made clear. Alternate translation: "the horses in the city that are still alive" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# see

The implicit information about what he wanted to see may be expressed clearly. Alternate translation: "see if what these lepers have said is true" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
