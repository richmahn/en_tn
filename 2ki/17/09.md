# General Information:

The narrative continues to summarize Yahweh's judgment on Israel.

# on every high hill and under every green tree

These are exaggerations to show that worship of false gods was widespread. AT: "on high hills and under green trees everywhere" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])
