# General Information:

Rabshakeh continues telling the message from the king of Assyria to King Hezekiah's men.

# is not he the one whose high places ... Jerusalem'?

This question assumes the listeners know the answer and is used for emphasis. AT: "You need to remember that he is the one whose high places ... Jerusalem'!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
