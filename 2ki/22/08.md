# Hilkiah

This is the name of a man. See how you translated this in [2 Kings 18:18](../18/16.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# the book of the law

Most likely these laws were written on a scroll. A scroll was a type of book made of one long, rolled-up sheet of papyrus or leather.

# given it into the hand of the workmen

Here "hand" represents the workmen as a whole. AT: "given it to the workmen" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
