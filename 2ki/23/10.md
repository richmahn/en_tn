## translationNotes

* **Topheth ... Ben Hinnom** - place names (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **pass his son or his daughter through the fire to Molech** - "put his son or his daughter in the fire and burn them as an offering to Molech"
* **He took away** - "King Josiah had his officials take away"
* **the horses** - Possible meanings are 1) real horses or 2) statues of horses.
* **Nathan Melech** - a man's name (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
