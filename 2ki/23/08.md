## translationNotes

* **Geba ... Beersheba** - names of places (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **city governor named Joshua** - "city ruler named Joshua" or "city leader named Joshua." This is a different Joshua from the Joshua in the Old Testament Book of Joshua.
* **brother priests** - "fellow priests" or AT: "the priests who also served at the temple"
