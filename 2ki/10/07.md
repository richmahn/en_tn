# seventy in number ... seventy persons

"70 in number ... 70 persons" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# sent them to Jehu

This means that they sent people to deliver the baskets to Jehu. Alternate translation: "sent people to take them to Jehu" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
