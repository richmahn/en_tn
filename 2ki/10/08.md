## translationNotes

* **of the king's sons** - "of Ahab's descendants" (UDB)
* **Jehu went out and stood** - AT: "Jehu went to the city gate and stood before the people"
* **You are innocent** - AT: "You did not kill king Joram" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicitinfo]])
* **See, I plotted against my master and killed him** - "See" is to bring attention to what Jehu is about to say. AT: "Hear my words, I was the one who planned to harm king Joram and killed him,"
* **but who killed all these?** - AT: "but it was Yahweh who commanded the deaths of all Ahab's descendants." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
