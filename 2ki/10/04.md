## translationNotes

* **But they were terrified** - "Then they were very afraid"
* **the two kings** - "two kings, Joram and Ahaziah"
* **could not stand before Jehu** - "could not resist him" (UDB)
* **So how can we stand?** - "how can we resist him?" (UDB) or "we cannot resist him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **Then the man who was in charge of the palace, and the man who was over the city, and the elders also, and they who raised the children** - AT: "Then the rulers of Jezreel, the elders and those who raised Ahab's children" (See: [[:en:bible:notes:2ki:10:01]])
* **good in your eyes** - "You do whatever you think is the best" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
