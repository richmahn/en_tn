## in executing ##

"in carrying out" or "in accomplishing"

## what was right in my eyes ##

"what I considered right" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## all that was in my heart ##

"all that I wanted you to do,"

## sit on the throne ##

"be the kings" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## to the fourth generation ##

son, and grandson, and great-grandson, and great-great-grandson (UDB)

## Jehu took no care to walk in the Law of Yahweh, the God of Israel, with all his heart ##

AT: "But Jehu was not careful to follow the Law of Yahweh, the God of Israel, in everything he did."

## He did not turn away from the sins of Jerobaom, by which he made Israel sin ##

AT: "Jehu did not stop from sinning in the same ways as Jerobaom, which caused the people of Israel to sin."
