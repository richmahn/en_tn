## Nehushta, Elnathan ##

Nehushta is the name of a woman. Elnathan is the name of a man.(See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## He did what was evil in the sight of Yahweh ##

"Jehoiachin did what Yahweh said was evil" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## he did all that his father had done ##

"just as his father had done"
