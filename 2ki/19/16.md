## translationNotes

* King Hezekiah continues praying to Yahweh after receiving the letter from King Sennacherib of Assyria.
* **turn your ear, Yahweh, and listen** - AT: "Please listen to what I am going to say Yahweh." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **open your eyes, Yahweh, and see** - AT: "Please look and see what is happening Yahweh,"
* **They have put their gods into the fire ** - The Assyrian kings have burned up the other nations' gods.
* **Assyrians have destroyed them** - The Assyrians destroyed both the nations and the nation's gods.
