## translationNotes

* King Hezekiah continues praying to Yahweh after receiving the letter from King Sennacherib of Assyria.
* **Yahweh our God** - Hezekiah acknowledges Yahweh as the God, Israel's God, and his God.
* **I implore you** - AT: "I beg of you,"
* **from his power** - "from the power of the King of Assyria" (UDB) or AT: "from the armies of the King of Assyria."
