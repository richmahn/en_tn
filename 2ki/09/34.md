# See now to

The phrase "see now to" means to give your attention to whatever is specified. Alternate translation: "Now go to" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# for she is a king's daughter

Since Jezebel was the daughter of a king, it was necessary to bury her properly. Alternate translation: "because she is a king's daughter and therefore should be buried properly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
