## Now Joram ##

"Now Joram and his army" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## the wounds that the Arameans had given him ##

AT: "wounds that Joram got during the battle with the Aramean army,"

## Jehu said to the servants of Joram ##

This refers to the officers who were with Joram at Ramoth Gilead.

## If this is your opinion ##

AT: "If you truly want me to be your king,"

## then let no one escape and go out of the city, in order to go tell this news in Jezreel ##

AT: "then do not let anyone leave this city for the purpose of going to warn king Joram and his army in Jezreel." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicitinfo]])

## Now Ahaziah king of Judah had come down to see Joram ##

King Ahaziah was also in Jezreel at this time, visiting Joram after he was injured in battle.
