## translationNotes

* **Uzziah, king of Judah** - Uzziah is the same person as "Azariah." Some other English translations may use that name here.
* **Zechariah the son of Jeroboam became king over Israel in Samaria and he reigned for six months** - AT: "Zechariah the son of Jeroboam was king of Israel for 6 months"
* **evil in the sight of Yahweh** - “evil according to Yahweh” or “evil before Yahweh” or "Yahweh condemns what he did and declares it to be evil" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **who caused Israel to sin** - "Jeroboam did many bad things, and he caused the people of Israel to sin with him."
* **Zechariah son the Jeroboam** - 756 BC; he is also referred to as Joash.
* **Jeroboam the son of Nebat** - 901 BC
