## Uzziah, king of Judah ##

Uzziah is the same person as "Azariah." Some other English translations may use that name here.

## Zechariah the son of Jeroboam became king over Israel in Samaria and he reigned for six months ##

AT: "Zechariah the son of Jeroboam was king of Israel for 6 months"

## evil in the sight of Yahweh ##

“evil according to Yahweh” or “evil before Yahweh” or "Yahweh condemns what he did and declares it to be evil" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## who caused Israel to sin ##

"Jeroboam did many bad things, and he caused the people of Israel to sin with him."
