## Pekah, Remaliah, Argob, and Arieh ##

(See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]]) Some versions regard Argob and Arieh as men who were killed along with the king.

## conspired ##

To conspire is to make a secret plan to do harm to someone or something, in this case, King Pekahiah.

## fifty men ##

"50 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## citadel of the king's palace ##

"fortified place in the king's palace" (UDB)
