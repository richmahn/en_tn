## If Yahweh does not help you, how can I help you? ##

AT: "If Yaheweh is not helping you, then I cannot help you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Is there anything coming from the threshing floor or winepress? ##

AT: "There is no food to harvest or any grapes to make wine to drink." See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
