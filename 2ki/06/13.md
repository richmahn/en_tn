# I may send men and capture him

The king plans to send the men to capture Elisha for him. The king does not plan to capture him himself. Alternate translation: "I may send men to capture him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# See

This word is used here to draw the king's attention to what is said next. Alternate translation: "Listen"

# he is in Dothan

"Elisha is in Dothan"

# Dothan

This is the name of a city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
