## stretch over Jerusalem the measuring line used against Samaria ##

AT: "judge Jerusalem using the same measure I used when I judged Samaria"

## plumb line ##

a tool made of a heavy weight and a thin rope used to show if a wall is straight

## I will throw off ##

AT: "I will abandon" (UDB) or "I will reject"

## hand them over into the hand of their enemies ##

"let their enemies defeat them and take over their land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
