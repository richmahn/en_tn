## translationNotes

* **harpist** - A harpist is someone who plays the harp.
* **hand of Yahweh** - Here "​hand"​ is used to represent power. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **full of trenches** - A trench is a long ditch that workers dig in the ground, where water can collect.
