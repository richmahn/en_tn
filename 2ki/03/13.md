## What have I to do with you?  ##

<b>What have I to do with you? <b>- AT:  "I have nothing to do with you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## As Yahweh of hosts lives ##

"As I know that Yahweh of hosts lives"

## before whom I stand ##

AT: "and I am ready to serve him"

## I honor the presence of Jehoshaphat ##

Elijah respects the man, King Jehoshaphat, not just that he is present.

## I would not pay any attention to you, or even look at you ##

<b>I would not pay any attention to you, or even look at you<b>- Elisha is making a strong statement, making it clear that he did not want to be involved. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
