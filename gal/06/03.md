# For

"Because." This indicates that the words that follow are why the Galatians should 1) "carry one other's burdens" ([Galatians 6:2](./01.md)) or 2) be careful that they themselves are not tempted ([Galatians 6:1](./01.md)) or 3) "not become conceited" ([Galatians 5:26](../05/01.md)).

# he is something

"he is someone important" or "he is better than others"

# he is nothing

"he is not important" or "he is not better than others"

# Each one should

"Each person must"

# each one will carry his own load

"each person will be judged by his own work only" or "each person will be responsible for his own work only"

# each one will

"each person will"
