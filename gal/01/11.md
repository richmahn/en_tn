## translationNotes

* **I did not receive it … nor was I taught it** - "I did not hear the gospel … nor was I taught the gospel"
* **it was by revelation of Jesus Christ to me** - This can mean either "Jesus Christ himself revealed the gospel to me" or "God made me know the gospel when he showed me who Jesus Christ was"
* **mere humans** - "human origin"
