## he called me through his grace ##

Possible meanings are "God called me to serve Him because He is gracious" or 2) "he called me by means of his grace."

## to reveal his Son in me ##

Possible meanings are 1) "to allow me to know his (God's) son" (see UDB) or 2) "to show the world through me that Jesus is God's son."

## proclaim him ##

"proclaim God's Son" or "preach the good news about God's Son"

## consult with flesh and blood ##

"ask people to help me understand the message"

## go up ##

"travel"
