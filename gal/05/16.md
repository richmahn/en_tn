## translationNotes

* **you** - plural
* **walk by the Spirit ** - <b>walk by the Spirit <b>- Walking is a metaphor for living. This can be translated "conduct your life in the power of the Holy Spirit" or "live your life in dependence on the Spirit." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **you will not fulfill the lust of the flesh ** - <b>you will not fulfill the lust of the flesh <b>- "you will not fulfill the desire of your human nature to commit sin"
* **not under the law** - "not obligated to obey the law of Moses"
