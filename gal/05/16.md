## you ##

plural

## walk by the Spirit  ##

Walking is a metaphor for living. This can be translated "conduct your life in the power of the Holy Spirit" or "live your life in dependence on the Spirit." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## you will not fulfill the lust of the flesh  ##

"you will not fulfill the desire of your human nature to commit sin"

## not under the law ##

"not obligated to obey the law of Moses"
