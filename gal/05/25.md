## If we live by the Spirit ##

"If we are alive by the Spirit" or "Since God's Spirit caused us to be alive"

## walk ##

This word refers to an army marching side by side and is a metaphor for practicing in community what Jesus taught (UDB; see: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]]).
