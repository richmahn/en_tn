## Her princes are roaring lions in her midst ##

AT: "Jerusalem's leaders are violent."  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## Her judges are evening wolves who leave nothing to be gnawed upon in the morning ##

AT: "Her judges are evil people who take things that belong to others."  This is also a metaphor.

## Her prophets are insolent and treasonous men ##

AT: "Her prophets are disobedient and disloyal men."
