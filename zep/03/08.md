## Yahweh's declaration ##

This means what Yahweh has solemnly said.

## to plunder ##

This means to take the property of others often by conquering them in war.

## to gather the nations, to assemble the kingdoms ##

<b>to gather the nations, to assemble the kingdoms</b> and <b>to pour out upon them my anger, all my fierce wrath</b> See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]] (See v. 6-7).

## fire of my anger ##

God's anger is very powerful, like a fire is very powerful.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
