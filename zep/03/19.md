## translationNotes

* **the lame** - This refers to people who cannot walk.
* **At that time I will bring you together, and at that time I will  assemble you together** - The idea is repeated to emphasize that it will certainly happen.  This is a doublet.
* **when I restore your fortunes** - <b>when I restore your fortunes<b>- AT: "When I cause you to be strong and successful again"
* **before your eyes** - "you will see" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
