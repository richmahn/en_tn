## he will break out like fire ##

"he will become like a fire that breaks out suddenly and destroys everything" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## turn justice into a bitter thing and throw righteousness down to the ground ##

call evil things good and treat good things as unimportant

## turn justice into a bitter thing ##

or "twist justice" or "do what is not just but say it is just" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## throw righteousness down to the ground ##

or "treat righteousness as though it were as unimportant as dirt"  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
