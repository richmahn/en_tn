## Break them ##

Possible meanings: 1) break the pieces of the temple (see UDB) or 2) break the tops of the pillars.

## Though they dig into Sheol, there my hand will take them. Though they climb up to heaven, there I will bring them down ##

AT: "No matter where they try to go, I will be there to catch them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])
