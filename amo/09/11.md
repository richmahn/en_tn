## tent ... breaches ... ruins ##

After Yahweh destroys the kingdom of Israel, it will be like a tent whose wooden poles have collapsed, a wall that has been broken down in some places, and a house that has been torn down. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## breaches ##

parts of a wall that have fallen down

## ruins ##

what is left when a building is destroyed
