## they will never again be uprooted from the land ##

AT: "no one will ever again uproot them from the land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]]) "they will live forever in the land like a plant that keeps its roots in the ground" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## uproot ##

pull a plant and its roots out of the ground
