## look ##

AT: “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you.”

## the big house will be smashed to pieces, and the little house to bits ##

Possible meanings: 1) Yahweh will command others to destroy every house or 2) Yahweh himself will destroy every house himself simply by giving a command.

## big house ... and ... little house ##

all houses (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])

## pieces ... and ... bits ##

You can use the same word for both if you need to. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
