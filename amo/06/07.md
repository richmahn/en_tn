## Declaration of the Lord Yahweh ##

Translate this as you did in [Amos 3:13](../03/13.md).

## I hate his fortresses ##

"I hate the people of Israel because they trust in their fortresses, not in me, to protect them"

## fortresses ##

walls built around cities to protect them from attackers (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
