## For the wrath ##

AT: "God reveals his anger" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

AT: "toward"

## all ungodliness and unrighteousness of people ##

AT: "they hide the true information about God"

## that which is known about God is visible to them ##

AT: "they can know about God because of what they can see" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
