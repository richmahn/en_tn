## translationNotes

* **Therefore** - "Because of this"
* **God gave them over to** - "God allowed them to indulge in" (see also "God allowed,"[[:en:bible:notes:rom:01:26]] and [[:en:bible:notes:rom:01:28]]).
* **them…their…themselves…they** - the "mankind" of [[:en:bible:notes:rom:01:18]]
* **the lusts of their hearts for uncleanness** - "the morally impure things they longed for."
* **for their bodies to be dishonored among themselves** - They committed sexually immoral and degrading acts. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
* **instead of** - Possible meanings are 1) "instead of" or "rather than" or 2) "along with."
