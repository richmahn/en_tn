## translationNotes

* **we have died with Christ** - Although Christ died physically, here "died" refers to believers dying spiritually to the power of sin. Alternate translation: "we have spiritually died with Christ"
* **We know that Christ has been raised from the dead** - This can be translated with an active verb: "God brought Christ back to life after he died." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **Death no longer rules over him** - Here "death" is described as a king or ruler that has power over people. This can be translated as: "He can never die again." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
