## translationNotes

* **the inner man** - the part of a person that remains after the body dies (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **a different principle in my body parts, warring against that new principle in my mind, and taking me captive** - "I am able only to do what my old nature tells me to do, not to live the new way the Spirit shows me"
* **a different principle in my body parts** - the old nature, the way people are when they are born
* **that new principle** - the new spiritually alive nature
* **the principle of sin which is in my body parts** - "my sinful nature," the sinful nature I was born with
