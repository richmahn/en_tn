## sin regained life ##

This can mean 1) "I realized that I was sinning" (UDB) or 2) "I strongly desired to sin" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## The commandment, which was to bring life, I found to be death ##

Paul did not literally die. AT: "God gave me the commandment so I would live, but it killed me instead" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
