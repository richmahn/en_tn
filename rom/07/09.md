## translationNotes

* **sin regained life** - This can mean 1) "I realized that I was sinning (UDB) or 2) "I strongly desired to sin." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **The commandment, which was to bring life, I found to be death** - Paul did not literally die. Alternate translation: "God gave me the commandment so I would live, but it killed me instead." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
