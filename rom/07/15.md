## translationNotes

* **For that which I do, I do not really understand** - AT: "I am not sure why I do some of the things that I do" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
* **For** - AT: "I do not understand why I do what I do because"
* **what I hate, I do** - AT:  "The things that I know are not good are the things that I do"
* **But** - "However"
* **I agree with the Law** - AT:  "I know God's law is good"
