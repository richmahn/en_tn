# will not his uncircumcision be considered as circumcision?

This is the first of two questions that Paul asks here to emphasize that circumcision is not what makes one right before God. You can translate this questions as a statement in an active form. Alternate translation: "God will consider him as circumcised. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
