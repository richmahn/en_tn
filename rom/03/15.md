## Their...their...They...their ##

the "Jews and Greeks" of [Romans 3:9](./09.md)

## Their feet are swift to pour out blood ##

AT: "They are in a hurry to harm and kill people" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## Destruction and suffering are in their paths ##

AT: "Everyone lives in such a way that they intentionally try to destroy others and cause them to suffer" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## a way of peace ##

A "way" is a "road" or "path." AT: "how to live at peace with others" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## There is no fear of God before their eyes ##

AT: "Everyone refuses to give God the respect he deserves" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
