## translationNotes

* Paul again takes up his imaginary argument with a Jewish person, answering the questions such a person might have.
* **Then what advantage has the Jew? And what is the benefit of circumcision?** - AT: "Then Jewish people have no benefit from God's covenant, even though God promised they would!" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **It is great** - AT: "There is much advantage"
* **First** - AT: "First in order of time" or "Most certainly" (see UDB) or "Most importantly"
