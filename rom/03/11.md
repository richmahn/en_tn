## none who understands ##

"None really understands God's truth"

## none who seeks after God ##

"none who sincerely tries to have a righteous relationship with God"

## turned away ##

"rejected God and his righteous will for them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## have become useless ##

"have become worthless as far as God's will for them is concerned"
