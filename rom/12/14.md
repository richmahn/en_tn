## Be of the same mind toward one another  ##

AT: "Agree with one another" or "Live in unity with each other" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## Do not think in proud ways ##

AT: "Do not think that you are more important than others"

## accept lowly people ##

AT: "welcome people who do not seem important"

## Do not be wise in your own thoughts ##

AT: "Do not think of yourselves as having more wisdom than everyone else"
