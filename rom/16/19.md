## translationNotes

* **For your example of obedience reaches everyone** - AT: "For when you obey Jesus everyone hears about it"
* **The God of peace will soon crush Satan under your feet** - The phrase "crush under your feet" refers to complete victory over an enemy. AT: "Soon God will give you peace and complete victory over Satan" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **innocent to that which is evil** - AT: "not mixed up in evil"
