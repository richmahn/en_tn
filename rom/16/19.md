## For your example of obedience reaches everyone ##

AT: "For when you obey Jesus everyone hears about it"

## The God of peace will soon crush Satan under your feet ##

The phrase "crush under your feet" refers to complete victory over an enemy. AT: "Soon God will give you peace and complete victory over Satan" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## innocent to that which is evil ##

AT: "not mixed up in evil"
