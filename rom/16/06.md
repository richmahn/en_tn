Paul is greeting many of the believers in Rome by name. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])

## Mary ##

a woman's name

## Junias ##

This could be either 1) Junia, a woman's name (see UDB) or 2) Junias, a man's name.

## Andronicus...Ampliatus ##

men's names

## my beloved in the Lord ##

"my dear friend and fellow believer"
