## Gaius...Erastus...Quartus ##

These are men's names. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## the host ##

This means that the believers met in his house for worship.

## the treasurer ##

This is a person who takes care of money for a group.

## 16:24 ##

This verse was omitted because some of the older, more reliable, ancient texts did not include the verse. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-textvariants]])
