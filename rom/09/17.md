## translationNotes

* **For the scripture says** - Here the scripture is personified as God talking to Pharaoh. Alternate translation: "The scripture records that God said" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **I...my** - God is referring to himself.
* **you** - singular
* **and that my name might be proclaimed in all the earth** - "and that people might proclaim my name in all the earth" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **and whom he wishes, he makes stubborn.** - God makes stubborn whom God wishes to make stubborn.
