## For the scripture says ##

Here the scripture is personified as God talking to Pharaoh. Alternate translation: "The scripture records that God said" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## I...my ##

God is referring to himself.

## you ##

singular

## and that my name might be proclaimed in all the earth ##

"and that people might proclaim my name in all the earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## and whom he wishes, he makes stubborn. ##

God makes stubborn whom God wishes to make stubborn.
