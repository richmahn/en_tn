## translationNotes

* **the children of the flesh** - This refers to people who are the physical descendants of Abraham.
* **children of God** - This refers to people who are the spiritual descendants who have faith in Jesus.
* **children of the promise** - This refers to people who will inherit the promise.
* **a son will be given to Sarah** - AT: "I will give Sara a son" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
