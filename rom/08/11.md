# If the Spirit ... lives in you

Paul assumes that the Holy Spirit lives in his readers. AT: "Since the Spirit ... lives in you"

# of him who raised

"of God, who raised"

# raised Jesus

Here to raise is an idiom for causing someone who has died to become alive again. AT: "caused Jesus to live again" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# mortal bodies

"physical bodies" or "bodies, which will die someday"
