## translationNotes

* **Because of this** - AT: "Because the government punishes evildoers"
* **you....Pay to everyone** - Paul is addressing the believers here. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_you]])
* **For** - AT: "This is why you should pay taxes:"
* **attend to** - AT: "administer" or "work on"
* **toll** - This refers to customs duty.
