"Jesus Christ came to help the Jews" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## as it is written ##

"as it is written in the Scriptures" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

"and Christ has been made the servant of the Gentiles" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
