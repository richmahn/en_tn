## translationNotes

* **For I say** - "I"refers to Paul.
* **Christ has been made a servant of the circumcision** - "Jesus Christ came to help the Jews" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **that he might confirm the promises given to the fathers** - "that God might confirm the promises he gave the ancestors of the Jews" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **as it is written** - "as it is written in the Scriptures" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **and for the Gentiles** - "and Christ has been made the servant of the Gentiles" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
