## translationNotes

* **the gift that was given me by God** - This gift is his appointment as an apostle despite his persecution of believers prior to his conversion. AT: "the gift God gave me"
* **the offering of the Gentiles might become acceptable** - AT: "God might be pleased with the Gentiles when they obey him" (see UDB) (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
