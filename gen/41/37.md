# This advice was good in the eyes of [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) and in the eyes of all his servants

The eyes represent seeing, and seeing represents thoughts or judgment. AT: "[Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) and his servants thought this was a good plan" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# his servants

This means [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh)'s officials.
