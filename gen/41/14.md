# [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) sent and called for

It is understood that [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) sent servants. AT: "[Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) sent his servants to get Joseph" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

# out of the dungeon

"out of the jail" or "out of the prison"

# He shaved himself

It was common practice to shave both the facial and head hair when preparing to go before [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh).

# came in to [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh)

Here "came" can be stated as "went." AT: "went before [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh)" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-go]])
