Joseph continues to counsel Pharaoh

## Let them gather ##

"allow the overseers to gather"

## store up ##

"store" or "save"

## Let them guard it ##

"Let the overseers guard the grain"

## the food will be a supply ##

"the food will be a stockpile"

## will not be devastated by the famine ##

The people and animals will not starve to death as a result of the coming harvest shortages.
