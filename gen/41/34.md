# let them take a fifth of the crops of [Egypt](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/egypt)

The word "fifth" is a fraction. AT: "let them divide the crops of [Egypt](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/egypt) into five equal parts, then take one of those parts" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-fraction]])
