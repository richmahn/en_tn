# behold, I stood

[Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) uses the word "behold" to make Joseph pay attention to surprising information.

# bank of the Nile

This is the higher ground along the edge of the Nile River. See how you translated a similar phrase in [Genesis 41:3](./01.md). AT: "beside the Nile"
