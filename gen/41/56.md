# The famine was over all the face of the whole land

The word "face" refers to the surface of the land. AT: "The famine had spread throughout the land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# Joseph opened all the storehouses and sold to the [Egypt](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/egypt)ians

Here "Joseph" stands for Joseph's servants. AT: "Joseph had his servants open all the storehouses and sell grain to the [Egypt](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/egypt)ians" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
