# [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) summoned Abram

"[Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) called Abram" or "[Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) ordered Abram to come to him"

# What is this that you have done to me?

[Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) used this rhetorical question to show how angry he was about what Abram had done to him. It can also be stated as an exclamation. AT: "You have done a terrible thing to me!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
