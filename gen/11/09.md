# its name was called [Babel](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/babel), because there Yahweh confused

The name "[Babel](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/babel)" sounds like the word that means "confused." Translators may want to add a footnote about this.

# confused the language of the whole earth

It means that Yahweh caused the people all over the earth to no longer speak the same language. AT: "mixed up the language of the whole earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
