# Mamre

This was another name for the city of [Hebron](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/hebron). It may have been named after Mamre, the friend of [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) who lived there. See how you translated this in [Genesis 13:18](../13/16.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
