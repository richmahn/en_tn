## translationNotes

* **Anah...Dishon...Ezer...Dishan** - See how you translated these names in [[en:bible:notes:gen:36:20]]. In 36:25 "Dishon" refers to the son of Anah. In 36:26 "Dishon" refers to the son of Seir.
* **Oholibamah** - This is the name of a woman. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Hemdan, Eshban, Ithran, and Cheran...Bilhan, Zaavan, and Akan...Uz, and Aran** - These are names of men.
