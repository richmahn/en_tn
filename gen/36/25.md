## Anah...Dishon...Ezer...Dishan ##

See how you translated these names in [Genesis 36:20](./20.md). In 36:25 "Dishon" refers to the son of Anah. In 36:26 "Dishon" refers to the son of Seir.

## Oholibamah ##

This is the name of a woman. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Hemdan, Eshban, Ithran, and Cheran...Bilhan, Zaavan, and Akan...Uz, and Aran ##

These are names of men.
