# Adah ... Oholibamah

These are names of [Esau](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/esau)'s wives. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Elon the [Hittite](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/hittite)

"Elon the descendant of Heth" or "Elon a descendant of Heth." This is the name of a man. See how you translate this in [Genesis 26:34](../26/34.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Hivite

This refers to a larger group of people. See how you translated it in [Genesis 10:17](../10/15.md).
