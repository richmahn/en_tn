# Nahath, Zerah, Shammah, Mizzah

These are the names of Reuel's sons. See how you translated these names in [Genesis 36:13](./13.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# in the land of [Edom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/edom)

This means that they lived in the land of [Edom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/edom). AT: "who lived in the land of [Edom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/edom)" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
