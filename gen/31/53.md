# May the God of [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham), and the god of [Nahor](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/nahor), the gods of their father, judge between us

[Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) is Jacob's grandfather. [Nahor](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/nahor) is Laban's grandfather. The father of [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) and [Nahor](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/nahor) is Terah. Not all of them worshipped Yahweh.

# the Fear of his father [Isaac](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/isaac)

Here the word "Fear" refers to Yahweh, who [Isaac](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/isaac) deeply respected and showed that respect by obeying him.
