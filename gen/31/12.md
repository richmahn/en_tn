The angel of the Lord continues to talk to Jacob. (See: [Genesis 31:10](./10.md))

## Lift up your eyes ##

This means "Look up" (UDB). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## are striped, speckled, and spotted ##

"have stripes and spots"

## where you vowed a vow to me ##

"where you made a vow to me"

## the land of your birth ##

"the land where you were born"
