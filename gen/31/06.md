# You know that it is with all my strength that I have served your father

The word "you" here refers to both [Rachel](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/rachel) and [Leah](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/leah). It also adds emphasis. AT: "You yourselves know that I have served your father with all my strength" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])
