## He said to him ##

"Jacob said to Laban"

## What is my offense? What is my sin, that you have hotly pursued after me? ##

"What have I done wrong that you should pursue me like this?"

## What have you found of all your household goods? ##

"What have you found that belongs to you?"

## Set them here before our relatives ##

"Lay anything you have found in front of our relatives." Here the word "our" refers to Jacob's relatives and includes Laban's relatives. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])

## they may judge between us two ##

the "two of us" being Jacob and Laban. Alternate Translation: "they may judge between the two of us."
