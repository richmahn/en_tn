# Yahweh went on his way

Here "went on his way" is an idiom that means he left, or he continued his journey. Yahweh appeared to [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) in human form as a traveler. AT: "Yahweh departed" or "Yahweh continued on his journey" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
