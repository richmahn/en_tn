## Nod ##

## had marital relations ##

“had sex with.” Your language may have a way of saying this politely. Some languages say “knew” or “slept with.” (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]]). See how you translated this in [Genesis 4:1](./01.md).
