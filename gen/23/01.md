## translationNotes

* **Sarah lived a hundred and twenty-seven years.** - "Sarah lived 127 years." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
* **These were the years of the life of Sarah.** - Some translations do not include this sentence.
* **Kiriatharba** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Abraham mourned and wept for Sarah** - This can be translated as "Abraham was very sad and cried because Sarah died."
