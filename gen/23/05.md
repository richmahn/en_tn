## translationNotes

* **sons of Heth** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:23:03]].
* **a prince of God** - This probably means "a powerful man" (UDB) or "a mighty leader."
* **your dead** - This can be translated as "your loved one" or "your wife" or "your wife's body."
* **the choicest of our tombs** - This can be translated as "the best of our burial places"
* **refuse you his tomb** - "withhold his tomb from you" or "refuse to give to you his tomb"
