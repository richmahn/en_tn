## translationNotes

* **rose up and went from his dead wife** - This can be translated as "got up and left the body"
* **the sons of Heth** - "the descendants of Heth" (UDB). It can also be translated as "the Hittites."
* **among you** - This can be translated as "here" or "in your country."
* **Please grant me a property** - This can be translated as "Sell me some land" or "Allow me to buy a piece of land."
* **my dead** - This means "the dead body of a person that I love." It can be translated here as "my wife's body" or "my wife" or "my loved one."
