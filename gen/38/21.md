## Adullamite ##

This is Hirah, the Adullamite, who was Judah's friend. See how you translated this in [Genesis 38:1](./01.md).

## temple prostitute who was at Enaim by the road? ##

"the prostitute who serves in the temple? I met here at Enaim by the road." See how you translated "Enaim" in [Genesis 38:12](./12.md).
