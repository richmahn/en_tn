## behold ##

The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.

## Perez ##

Translators may add a footnote that says: "The name Perez means 'breaking out.'" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Zerah ##

Translators may add a footnote that says: "The name Zerah means 'scarlet or bright red.'"
