# It came about after these things

"After these events." The phrase "these things" refers to the events of Genesis 22:1-19. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-newevent]])

# [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) was told

This can be stated in active form. AT: "someone told [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham)" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
