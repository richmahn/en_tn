## saw the place afar off ##

"saw far away the place that God had spoken of"

## young men ##

"servants" (UDB)

## We will worship ##

The word "we" refers only to Abraham and Isaac. (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])

## come again to you ##

"return to you."

## put it on Isaac his son ##

"had Isaac, his son, carry it"

## He took in his own hand ##

"Abraham himself carried"

## the fire ##

"something for starting a fire" (UDB). It could have been a pan containing burning coals or a torch or lamp.

## they went both of them together ##

"they left together" or "the two of them went together"
