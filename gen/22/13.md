## translationNotes

* **behold** - The word “behold” here alerts us to pay attention to the surprising information that follows.
* **went and took the ram** - "Abraham went over to the ram and took it."
* **Yahweh will provide** - This can be translated as "Yahweh will give what is needed."
* **to this day** - This can also be translated as "even now."
* **it will be provided** - This can also be translated with an active clause: "he will provide." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
