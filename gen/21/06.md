# God has made me laugh

[Sarah](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/sarah) was laughing because she was surprised and happy. This can be made explicit. AT: "God has caused me to laugh joyfully" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# every one who hears

What people would hear can be stated explicitly. AT: "everyone who hears about what God has done for me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
