# [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) took sheep and oxen and gave them to [Abimelech](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abimelech)

This is a sign of friendship and that [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) agrees to make covenant with [Abimelech](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abimelech). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-symaction]])
