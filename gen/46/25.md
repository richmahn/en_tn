# Bilhah

This is the name of [Rachel](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/rachel)'s female servant. See how you translated this name in [Genesis 29:29](../29/28.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# seven in all

This refers to the 7 children and grandchildren related to Bilhah. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
