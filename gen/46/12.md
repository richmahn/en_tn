## All the souls of his sons and his daughters were thirty-three in number ##

The word "soul" refers to the person. Here "sons" and "daughters" refer to his sons, daughters, and grandchildren. Alternate translation: "Altogether he had 33 sons and daughters." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
