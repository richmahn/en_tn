# Asenath

a woman's name. See how you translated this in [Genesis 41:45](../41/44.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Potiphera

a man's name. See how you translated this in [Genesis 41:45](../41/44.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# priest of On

On is a city, also called Heliopolis, which was "the City of the Sun" and the center of worship of the sun god Ra. See how you translated this in [Genesis 41:45](../41/44.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Bela, Beker, Ashbel, Gera, Naaman, Ehi, Rosh, Muppim, Huppim, and Ard

These are names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# fourteen in all

This refers to the 14 sons and grandsons related to Rachel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
