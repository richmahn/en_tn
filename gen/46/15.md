# Dinah

This is the name of [Leah](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/leah)'s daughter. See how you translated this name in [Genesis 30:21](../30/19.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# His sons and his daughters numbered thirty-three

Here "sons" and "daughters" refer to [Jacob](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/jacob)'s sons, daughters, and grandchildren related to [Leah](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/leah). AT: "Altogether he had 33 sons, daughters, and grandchildren" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
