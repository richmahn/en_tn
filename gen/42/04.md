# [Benjamin](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/benjamin), Joseph's brother, [Jacob](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/jacob) did not send

[Benjamin](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/benjamin) and Joseph had the same father and mother. [Jacob](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/jacob) did not want to risk sending Rachel's last son. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
