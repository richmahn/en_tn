## You are spies ##

Spies are people who secretly try to get information about a country to help another country.

## You came to see the undefended parts of the land ##

"You came to find out where we are not guarding our land"

## Your servants ##

The brothers refer to themselves as "your servants." This is a formal way of speaking to someone with greater authority.
