# By this you will be tested

This can be stated in active form. AT: "This is how I will test you" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# By the life of [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh)

This phrase indicates a solemn oath. AT: "I swear by the life of [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh)"
