# they also took [Lot](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/lot), Abram's brother's son, who was living in [Sodom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/sodom), along with all his possessions

The phrases "Abram's brother's son" and "who was living in [Sodom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/sodom)" remind the reader of things that were written earlier about [Lot](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/lot). AT: "they also took [Lot](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/lot), along with all his possessions. [Lot](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/lot) was Abram's brother's son and was living in [Sodom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/sodom) at that time" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-distinguish]])
