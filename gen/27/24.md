# He said

Isaac asks this question before blessing his son. AT: "But first Isaac asked" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-events]])

# eat of your game

The word "game" refers to a wild animal that people hunt and kill. See how "game" was translated in [Genesis 27:7](./05.md).
