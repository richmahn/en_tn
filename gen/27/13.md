## My son, let any curse fall on me ##

"Let your curse be on me, my son"

## obey my voice ##

Rebekah said "my voice" to refer to what she was saying. This could be translated as "obey what I tell you" or "obey me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
