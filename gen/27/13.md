## translationNotes

* **My son, let any curse fall upon me** - "Let your curse be on me, my son"
* **obey my voice** - Rebekah said "my voice" to refer to what she was saying. This could be translated as "obey what I tell you" or "obey me." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **bring them to me** - "bring me the young goats"
* **made delicious food, just like his father loved** - See how a similar sentence was translated in [[en:bible:notes:gen:27:03]].
