## Now ##

This word is used here to mark a new part of the story.

## they will be listed under the names of their brothers in their inheritance ##

This means the rest of Joseph's children would inherit land as part of the tribes of Ephraim and Manasseh. Alternate translation: "As for their inheritance, you will list them under the names of their brothers.
