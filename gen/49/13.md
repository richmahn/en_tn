## Zebulun will live ##

This refers to the descendants of Zebulun. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## He will be a harbor ##

<b>He will be a harbor</b> The sea towns that Zebulun will inhabit or build will provide shelter for ships. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## harbor ##

A part of the sea that is next to land and is a safe place for ships.
