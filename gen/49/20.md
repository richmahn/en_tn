# [Asher](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/asher)'s food ... and he

Here "[Asher](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/asher)" stands for his descendants. AT: "[Asher](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/asher)'s descendants' food ... and they" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# food will be rich

Here "rich" is a way of saying "delicious." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
