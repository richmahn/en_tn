## Their words pleased Hamor and his son Shechem ##

"Hamor and his son Shechem agreed with what Jacob's sons said"

## to do the thing ##

"to become circumcised"

## Jacob's daughter ##

"Jacob's daughter Dinah"

## and because he was the most honored person in all his father's household ##

This can be translated as a new sentence. Also the full meaning can be made explicit: "And Shechem knew all the men in his father's household would agree with him because he was the most honored among them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
