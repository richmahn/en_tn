# Should [Shechem](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/shechem) have dealt with our sister as with a prostitute?

[Simeon](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/simeon) and Levi use a question to emphasize that [Shechem](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/shechem) did what was wrong and deserved to die. AT: "[Shechem](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/shechem) should not have treated our sister as if she were a prostitute!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
