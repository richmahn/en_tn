# So the gifts went on ahead of him

Here "gifts" stands for the servants taking the gifts. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# He himself stayed

Here "himself" emphasizes that [Jacob](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/jacob) did not go with the servants. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rpronouns]])
