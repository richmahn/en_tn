# Oh that [Ishmael](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/ishmael) might live before you

"Please let [Ishmael](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/ishmael) inherit the covenant that you have made with me" or "Perhaps [Ishmael](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/ishmael) could receive your covenant blessing." [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham) suggested something that he believed really could happen.
