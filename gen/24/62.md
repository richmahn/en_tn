# Now

This word marks a change in the story. It was telling about the servant finding a wife, and now it will tell about [Isaac](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/isaac).

# Beer Lahai Roi

This is the name of a water well in the [Negev](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/negev). See how you translated it in [Genesis 16:14](../16/13.md).
