# Now

This word is used here to mark a break in the main story line. Here the author tells background information about [Rebekah](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/rebekah). The author introduces her brother, [Laban](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/laban), to the story. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-background]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-participants]])
