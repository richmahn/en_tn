# the oldest ... the youngest

The word "brother" is understood. Alternate translation: "the oldest brother ... the youngest brother" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

# youngest, and the cup was found in Benjamin's sack

This can be translated as a new sentence and in active form. Alternate translation: "youngest. The steward found the cup in Benjamin's sack" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
