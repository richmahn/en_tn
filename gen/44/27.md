Judah continues his story to Joseph

## said to us ##

"us" does not include Joseph. (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])

## you know ##

"you"refers to Judah.

## And if you take this one also from me  ##

"you" refers to Judah, "this one" refers to Benjamin, and "me" refers to Jacob.
