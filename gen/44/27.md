## translationNotes

* Judah continues his story to Joseph
* **said to us** - "us" does not include Joseph. (See [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_exclusive]])
* **you know** - "you"refers to Judah.
* **And if you take this one also from me ** - "you" refers to Judah, "this one" refers to Benjamin, and "me" refers to Jacob.
* **you will bring down my gray hair with sorrow to Sheol** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:gen:42:37]].
