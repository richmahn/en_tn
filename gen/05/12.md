# General Information:

The records in Genesis 5:6-27 have the same format. Translate them according to the notes in [Genesis 5:6-8](./06.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# seventy years

"70 years" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# 840 years

"eight hundred and forty years" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# 910 years

"nine hundred and ten years" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
