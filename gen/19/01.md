# The two angels

Genesis 18 says that two men went to [Sodom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/sodom). Here we learn that they are really angels. (See: [Genesis 18:22](../18/22.md))

# the gate of [Sodom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/sodom)

"the entrance to the city of [Sodom](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/sodom)." The city had a wall around it, and people had to go through a gate to get into it. This was a very important place in a city. Important people often spent time there.

# bowed down with his face to the ground

He put his knees on the ground then touched his forehead and nose to the ground.
