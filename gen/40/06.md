## translationNotes

* **Joseph came to them** - "Joseph came to the cupbearer and the baker"
* **Behold, they were sad** - The word “behold” here shows that Joseph was surprised by what he saw. Alternate translation: "He was surprised to see that they were sad."
* **Do not interpretations belong to God?** - Joseph uses a question for emphasis. Alternate translation: "God is able to reveal the meaning of dreams." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **Tell me, please.** - "Tell me the dreams, please."
