## the chief cupbearer and the chief baker ##

See how this was translated in [Genesis 40:1](./01.md).

## It came about on the third day that ##

“Afterward, on the third day,”

## He made a feast ##

"He had a feast"

## He paid special attention to the chief cupbearer and the chief baker, more than to all his other servants.  ##

<b>He paid special attention to the chief cupbearer and the chief baker, more than to all his other servants. <b>- "He lifted up the head of the chief cupbearer and the head of the chief baker in the midst of his servants."

## He restored the chief cupbearer to his responsibility ##

"He allowed the chief cupbearer to serve him again"

## and he put the cup into Pharaoh’s hand again  ##

<b>and he put the cup into Pharaoh’s hand again <b>- "so that he put the cup into Pharaoh's hand"
