# Mention me to [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) and bring me out of this prison

Joseph means for the cupbearer to tell [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) about him so that [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) will release him from prison. AT: "Help me get out of this prison by telling [Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) about me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
