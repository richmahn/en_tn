# [Jacob](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/jacob) slept with [Rachel](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/rachel)

This is a polite way to say that they had marital relations. AT: "[Jacob](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/jacob) married [Rachel](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/rachel)" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]])

# he loved [Rachel](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/rachel)

This refers to the romantic love between a man and a woman.
