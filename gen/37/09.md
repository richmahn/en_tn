## His father rebuked him. He said to him ##

"Israel scolded him, saying"

## What is this dream that you have dreamed? Will your mother and I and your brothers actually come to bow down to the ground to you? ##

Israel uses questions to correct Joseph. Alternate translation: "This dream you had is not real. Your mother, brothers, and I will not bow down before you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
