# the Kenites, the Kenizzites, the Kadmonites, the Hittites, the Perizzites, the Rephaites, the Amorites, the Canaanites, the Girgashites, and the Jebusites

These are the names of groups of people who lived in that land. God would allow [Abraham](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/abraham)'s descendants to conquer these people and take their land. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
