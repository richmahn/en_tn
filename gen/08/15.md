## Come out ##

This can be translated as "Go out."

## every living creature of all flesh ##

"every kind of living creature."

## abound ##

This could be translated as "swarm."

## be fruitful and multiply ##

This can be translated "reproduce greatly" or "have many offspring". This is the same command God gave to the first people in [Genesis 1:28](../01/28.md).

## be fruitful ##

"have offspring" or "produce young"

## multiply ##

"increase greatly in number" or "become many"
