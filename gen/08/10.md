## translationNotes

* **Behold** - The word "behold" here tells us to pay attention to the important information that comes next.
* **freshly plucked** - "freshly picked" or "newly picked"
* **He waited another seven days** - "He waited a second seven days"
* **she did not return again to him** - If people would not understand, you could make state the reason explicitly: "She did not return again to him because she found a place to land." (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
