## The man ##

This can also be translated as the name Adam.

## called his wife's name Eve ##

Adam "gave his wife the name Eve" or "named his wife Eve" (UDB).

## Eve ##

Translators may write a footnote saying, "The name Eve sounds like the Hebrew word that means 'living.'"

## all living things ##

This could be translated as "all the living." This refers to people.

## garments of skins ##

This could be translated "clothing made out of animal skins."
