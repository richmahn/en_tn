## translationNotes

* **fresh poplar...almond...plane tree** - These are all trees with white wood. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_unknown]])
* **and peeled white streaks in them, and made the white inner wood appear that was in the sticks** - "and peeled off pieces of bark so that the white wood underneath would show"
* **the watering-trough** - a long open container for holding water for animals to drink
* **conceived** - "mated" or "bred"
