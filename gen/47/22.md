# the priests were given an allowance

An "allowance" is an amount of money or food that someone regularly gives to another person. This can be stated in active form. AT: "[Pharaoh](rc://en/tw/dict/bible/names/pharaoh) gave the priests a certain amount of food each day" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
