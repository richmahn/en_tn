## When all the money of the lands of Egypt and  Canaan was spent ##

Alternate translation: "When the people of Egypt and Canaan used up all their money." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## of the lands of Egypt and Canaan ##

"from the land of Egypt and from the land of Canaan"

## Why should we die in your presence because our money is gone? ##

The people used a question to emphasize how desperate they are to buy food. Alternate translation: "Please, do not let us die because we have used up all of our money." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## fed them with ##

"provided them with" or "gave them" (UDB)
