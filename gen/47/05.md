* **Settle your father and your brothers in the best region, the land of Goshen** - "Settle your father and your brothers in the best part of the land, which is the land of Goshen."
* **If you know any capable men among them** - "If you know of any men among them who have great skill taking care of animals." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
