## but I will establish my covenant with you ##

This covenant was a promise that God made to Noah. God would fulfill the covenant. He did not require Noah to do anything as part of the covenant.

## with you ##

The word "you" is singular. God was making his covenant with Noah. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## Of every living creature of all flesh, two of every kind you must bring into the ark ##

"You must bring into the ark two of every kind of very living creature"

## of all flesh ##

"of every kind of animal"
