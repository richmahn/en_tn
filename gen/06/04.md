## giants ##

very large people

## This happened when ##

This refers to the presence of the giants. It can be translated as "This happened because."

## sons of God ##

Translate this as you did in [Genesis 6:1](./01.md).

## These were the mighty men of old ##

"These giants were the mighty men who lived long ago" or "These children were the powerful fighters who lived long ago."

## men of renown ##

"famous men"
