## your eyes see, and the eyes of my brother Benjamin ##

The word "eyes" refers to the brothers and Benjamin. Alternate translation: "All of you and Benjamin see that it is me, Joseph, your brother." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## that it is my mouth that speaks to you ##

Joseph uses the word "mouth" to refer to himself. Alternate translation: "that I, Joseph, am speaking to you."

## about all my power ##

"how I am greatly honored"

## my father down here ##

"my father here to me"
