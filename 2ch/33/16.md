# He rebuilt

Manasseh did not do the building and construction himself, rather, he commanded his workers to do it. Alternate translation: "He commanded them to rebuild" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# he commanded Judah

Here "Judah" refers to the people who live there. Alternate translation: "he commanded the people of Judah" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
