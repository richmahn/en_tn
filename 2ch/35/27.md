# and his deeds ... are written in the book

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "and men have written of all his deeds ... in the book" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# his deeds, from beginning to end,

This refers to all of the significant things that he did from the beginning of his reign as king to the end of his life. Alternate translation: "all of his deeds" or "everything he did from the beginning of his reign to when he died" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])

# the book of the kings of Judah and Israel

This is a book that no longer exists.
