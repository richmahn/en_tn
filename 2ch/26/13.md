# 2,600 ... 307,500

"two thousand six hundred ... three hundred seven thousand five hundred" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# Under their hand was an army

Here "their hand" represents their authority. Alternate translation: "Under their authority was an army" or "They commanded an army" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
