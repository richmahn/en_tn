# ships

These are ships capable of traveling on large bodies of water.

# Tarshish

This is the name of a city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# They built the ships

Jehoshaphat and Ahaziah did not build the ships, rather, their workers built them. Alternate translation: "Their workers built the ships" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
