# Kohathites and Korahites

These are the names of people groups descending from Kohath and Korah. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# with a very loud voice

This speaks of all the people praising Yahweh as if they together had one voice. Alternate translation: "with very loud voices" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
