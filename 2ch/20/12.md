# will you not judge them?

This rhetorical question is used as a request. This question can be written as a statement. AT: "please judge them." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

# our eyes are on you

Here the people are represented by their "eyes" to emphasize that they are directing their attention to God. AT: "we are looking to you for help" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
