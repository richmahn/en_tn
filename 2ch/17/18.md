# Eliada ... Jehozabad

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# 200,000 ... 180,000

"two hundred thousand ... one hundred and eighty thousand." Both of these numbers refer to men. AT: "200,000 men ... 180,000 men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
