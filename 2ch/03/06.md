# He decorated ... He also overlaid ... he carved

The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. AT: "Solomon's workers decorated ... They also overlaid ... they carved" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# precious stones

Stones that were beautiful, highly valued, and used for decorations.

# Parvaim

This is probably the name of a region. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# beams

A beam is a long heavy piece of wood used to support a building.
