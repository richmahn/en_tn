# thank offerings

These were offerings of thanksgiving.

# the house of Yahweh

The word "house" represents the temple. AT: "the temple of Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# who had a willing heart

Here the word "heart" represents the person who was willing. AT: "who were willing" or "who desired" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
