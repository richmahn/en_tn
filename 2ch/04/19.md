# Solomon made

The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. Alternate translation: "Solomon's workers made" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# all the furnishings

This refers to all of the bowls and tools that were used in the house of God.

# the tables on which the bread of the presence was to be placed

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "the tables on which the priests were to place the bread of the presence" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# the bread of the presence

This is a reference to the 12 loaves of bread that were placed in front of the altar. See how you translated this in [2 Chronicles 2:4](../02/04.md).
