# the house of God ... the house of Yahweh

The word "house" represents the temple. AT: "the temple of God ... the temple of Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# They burned down

"They" refers to the Babylonian soldiers.
