# They have emptied out

This is an idiom. Alternate translation: "They have gathered all" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# into the hand of the supervisors and to the workmen

The supervisors and workmen are represented by their "hand" to emphasize their possession. Alternate translation: "to the supervisors and the workmen" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
