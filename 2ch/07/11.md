# Solomon finished the house of Yahweh and his own house

The readers should understand that Solomon probably commanded other people to do the actual work. Alternate translation: "the workers whom Solomon commanded finished the house of Yahweh and Solomon's own house" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# Everything that came into Solomon's heart to make

The heart is spoken of as if it were a container, and desires are spoken of as if they came into the heart. Alternate translation: "Everything that Solomon desired to make" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# he successfully carried out

This is an idiom. Alternate translation: "he successfully finished" or "he caused to be done successfully (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
