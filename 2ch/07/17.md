# As for you

Here "you" refers to King Solomon.

# if you walk before me as David your father walked

Here walking is a metaphor for living. Walking before God represents living in obedience to him. Alternate translation: "If you obey me as David your father did" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# David your father

Solomon was one of David's sons.

# keeping my statutes and my decrees

"obeying my statutes and my decrees"
