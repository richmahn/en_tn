# Hezekiah who also stopped up ... and who brought them straight down

The readers should understand that Hezekiah probably commanded other people to do the actual work. Alternate translation: "Hezekiah who ordered his workers to stop up ... and to build a tunnel so that the water would flow down" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# waters of Gihon

This is the name of a stream near Jerusalem. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
