## against me they hope for my hurt ##

AT: "they hope that I will be hurt" or "they hope that very bad things will happen to me" (UDB)

## An evil disease... holds tightly to him ##

AT: He has an evil disease that will not leave him"  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])
