# The cords of the wicked have ensnared me

In this metaphor, wicked or evil people tried to cause the writer to sin like a hunter seeking to catch an animal with a trap. AT: "My enemies have tried to catch me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
