# Let the proud be put to shame

This can be stated in active form. AT: "Put the proud to shame" or "Cause those who are proud to be ashamed" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# the proud

This nominal adjective can be stated as an adjective. AT: "proud people" or "those who are proud" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-nominaladj]])
