## SAMEKH: ##

120 begins with this letter.

## who have a double mind ##

These people are not sincere. They are not wholly committed to following God. AT: "who cannot make up their mind about following God" or "who are insincere" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## my hiding place ##

This means God is like a place where we can hide from our enemies, where we are safe and protected. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## my shield ##

AT: "my protector"

## your word ##

AT: "your instruction"
