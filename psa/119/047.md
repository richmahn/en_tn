## translationNotes

* **I delight in your commandments** - Possible meanings are 1) "I find delight in studying your commandments" or 2) I am delighted that I have the opportunity to study your commandments".
* **lift up my hands to** - to honor, cherish or respect God's commandments. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
