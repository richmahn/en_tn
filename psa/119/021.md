## who are cursed ##

Possible meanings are that 1) God will curse them, or 2) they deserve God's punishment. AT: "who deserve your punishment"

## who wander from your commandments ##

AT: "who go away from the path of your commandments" or "who disobey your commandments" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## Spare me from ##

"Keep me from" or "Rescue me from"

## and humiliation ##

"and feeling ashamed and foolish"
