## BETH ##

This is the second letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 9-16 begins with this letter.

## How can a young person keep his path pure? ##

This question is used to introduce a new value to the word of God. AT: "This is how a young person can keep his path pure" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## I seek you ##

Here this means "I try to serve you."

## stray from your commandments ##

AT: “disobey your commandments.” (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
