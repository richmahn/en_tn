# VAV

This is the name of the sixth letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 41-48 begins with this letter.

# give me your unfailing love

This is a request. AT: "please give me your unfailing love" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# your salvation

You may make clear the understood information. AT: "give me your salvation" or "save me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

# a reply

"an answer" or "a response"
