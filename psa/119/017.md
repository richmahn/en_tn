## GIMEL ##

This is the third letter of the Hebrew alphabet. In the Hebrew language, each line of verses 17-24 begins with this letter.

## your servant ##

The writer called himself "your servant" to show his humility.

## open my eyes ##

This means "help me to understand." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## in your law ##

AT: "in your instructions" or "in your commands"
