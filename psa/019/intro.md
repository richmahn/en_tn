# Psalm 019 General Notes #

#### Type of psalm ####

Psalm 19 is a praise psalm, praising God for his creation and for his law.

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### God's law #####
God's law keeps people from sinning. There are two distinct themes in this psalm. Verses 1-6 are God's glory in the heavens and the second is praising God for his law. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/lawofmoses]], [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/glory]] and [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/heaven]])
