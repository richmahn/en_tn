## translationNotes

* **Yahweh, rescue the king** - Possible interpretations are 1) the people ask God to protect the king 2) the king continues to speak about himself in the third person (UDB).
* **the king; help us when we call** - Some translations understand the Hebrew differently. AT: "King, help us when we call you"
