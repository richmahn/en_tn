## translationNotes

* **examines** - AT: "watches carefully"
* **do violence** - AT: "hurt others."
* **He rains burning coals and brimstone upon the wicked: a scorching wind will be their portion from his cup!** - AT: "He punishes the wicked; there will be no relief for them! (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **brimstone** - This is another name for sulphur.
* **see his face** - AT: "be in his presence forever."
* **Selah** - AT: "Think about these things."
