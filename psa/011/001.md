## A psalm of David ##

“This is a psalm that David wrote.”

## I take refuge in Yahweh ##

A "refuge" is a kind of shelter. Here it represents the protection that Yahweh can provided. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## how will you say to me, "Flee like a bird to the mountain?" ##

AT: "So do not ask me to run away!" ([[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## For see! The wicked prepare their bows. They make ready their arrows on the strings to shoot in the darkness at the upright in heart ##

AT: "Look! the wicked are preparing to attack upright people."
