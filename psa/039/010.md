## Stop wounding me ##

AT: "O my God, stop your blows upon me"

## I am overwhelmed ##

AT: "I am defeated completely"

## like a moth ##

The writer compares the strength of people to cloth that the worm of a moth eats. AT: "like moths eat away at clothing" (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## people are nothing but vapor ##

The writer compares people to mist or fumes that disappear quickly. AT: "Our lives are completely fragile" (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
