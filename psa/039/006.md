## Surely every man walks about like a shadow ##

AT: "Our life disappears like a shadow does." (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## Now, Lord, for what am I waiting? ##

AT: "Lord, now I am waiting for you to act," The writer asks this question to emphasize that Yahweh is his only hope. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
