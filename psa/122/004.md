# the name of Yahweh

Here "name" refers to Yahweh himself. AT: "Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# the house of David

Here "house" refers to the descendants of David. AT: "the descendants of David" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
