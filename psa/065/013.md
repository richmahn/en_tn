## pastures ##

a large field where animals feed on the grass

## flocks ##

group of animals, such as sheep and goats

## they shout for joy, and they sing ##

The pastures, hills and valleys are so bountiful, that they seem to shout and sing for joy. AT: "they are like joyful singing people" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## they shout ##

The word "they" refers to the pastures and valleys.
