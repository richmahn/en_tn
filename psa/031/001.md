## For the chief musician ##

"This is for the director of music to use in worship."

##  A psalm of David ##

"This is a psalm that David wrote."

## never let me be humiliated ##

AT: "do not let others humiliate me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## rock of refuge ##

AT: "like a huge rock on which I can be safe" (UDB) (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## be my rock of refuge, a stronghold to save me ##

The phrase "be my rock of refuge" is a request for protection. A second similar request is provided for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
