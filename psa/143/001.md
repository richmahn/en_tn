## A psalm of David ##

"This is a psalm that David wrote."

## answer me ##

AT: "please do what I ask you to do" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## Do not enter into judgment ##

AT: "Please do not judge" or "I beg you not to judge" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## in your sight no one is righteous ##

AT: "you do not think anyone is righteous" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
