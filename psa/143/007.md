## my spirit faints ##

"I am weak" or "I am not confident" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## Do not hide your face from me ##

This is a request. AT: "I beg you not to hide from me" or "Please, do not hide from me"

## Show me ##

AT: "Reveal to me" or "explain to me"

## for I lift up my soul ##

AT: "I pray to you" or "I ask of you"
