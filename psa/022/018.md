# my garments

# Do not be far away

This can be stated in positive form. AT: "Be very close" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

# my strength

Here "strength" represents Yahweh who gives him strength. AT: "you who give me strength" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
