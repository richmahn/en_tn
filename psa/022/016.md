## translationNotes

* **dogs** - This word refers to his enemies or evil people. AT: "my enemies are like dogs that" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **a company of evildoers** - "a group of evildoers" or "a gang of evildoers"
* **encircled** - "surrounded"
* **pierced** - stabbed through something with a sharp object
* **I can count all my bones** - AT: "I can see all of my bones" or "I can feel each of my bones"
* **look and stare at me** - These two words mean the same thing and are repeated to emphasize how uncomfortable he is with people looking at him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
