## Oh, send out your light ... let them lead me  ##

The words your light and your truth have the same meaning. AT: "Please give me your truths to guide my life" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## holy hill ##

The Temple in Jerusalem is referred to by the hill it is built on. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## exceeding joy ##

AT: "very great joy"
