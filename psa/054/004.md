## who upholds me ##

AT: "who provides what I need" "he keeps me safe"(See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## in your faithfulness, destroy them! ##

Here David changes from talking about God to talking to God. AT: "God, destroy them because you are faithful to me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-apostrophe]])
