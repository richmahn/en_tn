## translationNotes

* **abundant** - "great" or "a large amount of"
* **joy resides in your presence** - The writer speaks of "joy" as if it were a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **in your right hand** - The words "right hand" indicate being in God's special presence. AT: "when I am near you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
