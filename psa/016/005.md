## translationNotes

* **my cup** - The word "cup" represents the one who gives blessings. AT: "the one who blesses me." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **Measuring lines have been laid for me** - AT: "The place you have provided for me is pleasant."
* **You hold on to my destiny** - AT: "You determine my future."
