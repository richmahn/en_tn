## he who comes in the name of Yahweh ##

AT: "the one who comes in the power of the Lord" or "the one who does things to bring glory to God" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## bind the sacrifice with cords ##

AT: "tie the sacrifice with rope"

## horns of the altar ##

The horns were part of the top of the altar.
