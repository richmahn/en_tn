## Taste and see that Yahweh is good ##

Yahweh's goodness cannot be literally tasted and seen. AT: "Try and experience that Yahweh is good" (see: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## there is no lack for those who fear him ##

AT: "those who fear him will always have what they need" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])
