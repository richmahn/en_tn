## The eyes of Yahweh are on the righteous ##

AT: "Yahweh watches over the righteous" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## his ears are directed toward their cry ##

AT: "he directs his attention toward their cry" or "he pays attention to their cry" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## in order to wipe out their memory from the earth ##

AT: "so that when they are dead, people will forget them completely" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## and Yahweh hears ##

AT: "and Yahweh pays attention to them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
