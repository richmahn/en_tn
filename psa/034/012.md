## What man desires life and desires to live many days and have a good life? ##

The implicit answer to this question is "every man." AT: "Every man desires life and desires to live many days and have a good life" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Then keep from speaking evil ##

AT: "Therefore, keep from speaking evil"

## keep your lips from speaking lies ##

AT: "keep yourself from speaking lies" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
