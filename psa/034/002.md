## translationNotes

* **Praise Yahweh with me** - The verb "praise" is a command to a group. AT: "Everyone should praise Yahweh with me"
* **let us lift up** - "let us exalt" AT: "let us describe as great"
* **his name** - AT: "his character" or "him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
