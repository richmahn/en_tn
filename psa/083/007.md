# Gebal, Ammon, Amalek ... Philistia

These all represent the people of each area or tribe. AT: "the people of Gebal, the Ammonites, the Amalekites ... the Philistines" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# Gebal

This is the name of a region south of the Dead Sea. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
