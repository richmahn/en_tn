# General Information:

Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-poetry]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

# the name of Yahweh

Here the word "name" represents Yahweh, himself. AT: "Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
