## translationNotes

* **Protect me like the apple of your eye** - "Protect me as you would something most valuable and precious;" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **hide me under the shadow of your wings** - "Keep me safe as a mother bird protects her babies by gathering them under her wing" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **their mouths speak with pride** - AT: "they are always boasting." "Their mouths" represents the people who speak. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
