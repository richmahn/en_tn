## A prayer of David ##

"This is a prayer that David wrote."

## Give ear to my prayer from lips without deceit ##

"Listen to my prayer which does not come from lying lips" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## Let my vindication come from your presence ##

AT "In your presence I will be declared innocent"

## let your eyes see what is right! ##

"you know what is true and right!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
