## For my days pass away like smoke ##

Here "my days" refers to my life and the idea of "smoke" is something that quickly disappears. AT: "My life passes away quickly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## my bones burn like fire ##

AT: "my body feels like it is burning" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## my heart is crushed ##

"my heart is broken"
