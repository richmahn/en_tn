## translationNotes

* **May the gold of Sheba be given to him** - AT: "May they give him the gold of Sheba" or "May he receive the gold of Sheba" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **all day long** - AT: "continually" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **in the earth** - "in the land"
* **their crops wave in the breeze** - AT: "their crops move back and forth in the calm wind"
* **crops** - plants that people grow for food
* **like the trees of Lebanon** - AT: "just like the trees in Lebanon move back and forth in the wind" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **like grass on the earth** - AT: "just like the grass on the earth flourishes" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
