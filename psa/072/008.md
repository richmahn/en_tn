## May he have dominion ##

AT: "May the king have dominion"

## from sea to sea, and from the River to the ends of the earth ##

Both of these expressions refer to the whole earth. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])

## the River ##

AT: "the Euphrates"

## lick the dust ##

This is a symbol of extreme humiliation.

## render ##

"return" AT: "give"

## offer gifts ##

"bring near gifts" AT: "give gifts"

## Seba ##

This is the name of a country. It is not the same country as Sheba. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
