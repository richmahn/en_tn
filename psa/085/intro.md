# Psalm 085 General Notes #

#### Type of psalm ####

Psalm 85 is a worship psalm.

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Blessings #####
God has given so many blessings. If the people stop their sinning, then the land will be filled by the glory of God and his blessings. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/bless]], [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/sin]] and [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/glory]])

##### Group Prayer #####
Note how this psalm uses the plural to show that it is a group praying.
