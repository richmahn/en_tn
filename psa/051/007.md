## translationNotes

* **Purify me with hyssop, and I will be clean; wash me, and I will be whiter than snow** - These phrases have similar meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "Cleanse me from my sins, and I will be clean; wash me, and I will be whiter than snow" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **whiter than snow** - AT: "very, very white"
* **hyssop** - a plant used in ceremonial sprinkling
* **joy and gladness** - These two words have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* ** the bones that you have broken may rejoice** - Here the author is referring to the pain in his inner being. AT: "for you have caused me great pain in my inner being. Let me rejoice again!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
