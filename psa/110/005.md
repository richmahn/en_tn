## The Lord is at your right hand ##

When the lord goes to battle, Yahweh stands at his right hand in order to help him. AT: "The Lord helps you in battle" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## The Lord ##

The word "Lord" refers to Yahweh.

## your right hand ##

David is speaking to the lord, whom Yahweh has made king.

## He will kill ##

The word "He" refers to the Yahweh who does these things through the king.

## the kings ##

"the enemy kings" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## on the day of his anger ##

AT: "on the day of judgment when his patience turns to anger" (See: Implicit/Explicit)
