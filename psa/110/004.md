# You are

Yahweh is speaking to the lord.

# will not change

This means that he will not change what he has said. AT: "will not change his mind" or "will not change what he has said" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# after the manner of Melchizedek

The missing words may be added. AT: "after the manner that Melchizedek was priest" or "in the same way that Melchizedek was priest" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
