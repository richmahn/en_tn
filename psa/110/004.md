* **You are** - Yahweh is speaking to the lord.
* **and will not change** - "and will not change his mind" or "and will not regret his oath"
* **after the manner of Melchizedek** - AT: "in the same way that Melchizedek was priest." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
