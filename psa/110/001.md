## A psalm of David ##

“This is a psalm that David wrote.”

## Sit at my right hand ##

AT: "Sit at the place of honor which I have for you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## make your enemies your footstool  ##

AT: "put your enemies under your power." (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
