## He makes the winds his messengers ##

It is also possible to translate this "He makes his messengers like the wind" or "He makes his angels swift" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## flames of fire his servant ##

It is also possible to translate this "he makes his servants to be like flames of fire" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## He laid the foundations of the earth ##

AT: "He created the whole earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
