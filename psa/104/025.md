## deep and wide ##

"it is deep and wide" This means that the sea is great in both directions, depth and width. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])

## both small and great ##

This refers to all creatures. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])
