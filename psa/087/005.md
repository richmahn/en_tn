## these were born in her ##

AT: "these had their beginning in Zion"

## the Most High himself ##

The writer uses a reflexive pronoun to emphasize that it is the Most High who is doing this. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rpronouns]])

## Yahweh notes ##

"Yahweh counts" or "Yahweh pays special attention"
