## translationNotes

* See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]
* **A psalm of David** - “This is a psalm that David wrote.”
* **without wavering** - The idea of "wavering" here represents doubting. AT: "without doubting"
* **Examine me** - "Test me" or "Try me"
* **test the purity of my inner parts and my heart** - AT: "Test my inner parts and my heart to see if they are pure" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
* **my inner parts and my heart** - These body parts represent David's thoughts and attitude. Some languages may use different body parts of these.
