## Yahweh, how long will the wicked, how long will the wicked rejoice? ##

By repeating these phrases and asking a question, the writer shows his strong emotions. AT: "Yahweh, the wicked people always seem to win" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## They pour out arrogant and defiant speeches, and they all boast ##

AT: "The wicked people say arrogant things and boast about doing evil" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
