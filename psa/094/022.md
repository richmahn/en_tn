## Yahweh has been my high tower ##

Here "tower" represents protection. AT: "Yahweh has protected me from my enemies" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## God has been the rock of my refuge ##

Here "rock" describes God as strong and firm, and "refuge" refers to safety. AT: "God has kept me safe by his power" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
