## subdues ##

to control by force.

## under us...under our feet ##

Authority is described as having a position over others. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## with a shout...the sound of a trumpet ##

These phrases use actions associated with celebration to describe the people's celebration. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
