## translationNotes

* **his holy throne** - This throne is present and not invisible.
* **The princes of the peoples** - "The princes of all the nations"
* **to the people of the God of Abraham** - "with the people belonging to the God of Abraham"
* **shields of the earth belong to Yahweh** - The word "shields" refers to the protection of an army. AT: "armies of the earth are controlled by Yahweh" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
