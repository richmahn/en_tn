## By you I have been supported from the womb ##

AT:  “You have supported me from the womb” or "You have taken care of me ever since I came out of my mother's womb" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## you are he who ##

AT: "you"

## I am an example to many people ##

This may also be translated, "I have become a wonder to many people".
