## God, do not forsake me, ##

AT: "God, please do not forsake me"

## your power to everyone who is to come ##

AT: "and as I declare your power to everyone who is to come" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

## everyone who is to come ##

This refers to the people who he will meet later on, but older people and children. AT: "the generation to come" or "everyone in the future"
