## translationNotes

* **Peor** - This is the name of a mountain. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **to the dead** - "The dead" refers to the idols and gods that the Israelites were worshiping. AT: "to gods who are dead" or "to lifeless gods" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **a plague broke out** - "a plague spread"
* **provoked him to anger** - AT: "angered him"
