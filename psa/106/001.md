## Who can recount the mighty acts of Yahweh ... ? ##

The author asks this question to praise God and does not expect an answer. AT: "No one can recount the mighty acts of Yahweh ..." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## praiseworthy deeds ##

"deeds that are worthy of praise"
