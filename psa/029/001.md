## A psalm of David ##

“This is a psalm that David wrote.”

## sons of the mighty ##

AT: "mighty men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## that his name deserves ##

The phrase "his name" refers to Yahweh or his reputation. AT: "as he deserves" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]]}

## that is suitable ##

"that is right"
