## He makes Lebanon skip like a calf ##

AT: He makes the land of Lebanon shake like a calf skipping. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## skip ##

jump lightly back and forth

## and Sirion like a young ox ##

AT: "and he makes Sirion jump like a young ox" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

## Sirion ##

This is a mountain in Lebanon. It is also called Mount Hermon. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
