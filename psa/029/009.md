## translationNotes

* **to go into labor** - "to be ready to give birth"
* **strips the forests bare** - "removes everything from the forest" or "uncovers the forest" AT: "strips off the leaves"
* **Yahweh sits as king** - This means that he rules. AT: "Yahweh rules" or "Yahweh is king"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
