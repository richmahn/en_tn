## myrrh, aloes, and cassia -  ##

**  These are substances collected from plants to make perfume. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])

## ivory palaces ##

Ivory is a hard white substance that forms animal's tusks. This phrase describes a palace decorated with items made of ivory.

## honorable women  ##

These women are wives of the king who receive his approval.

## Ophir ##

This is the name of a city. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
