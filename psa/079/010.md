## the blood of your servants that was shed ##

The shedding of blood means to wound and even kill. AT: "the killing of your servants" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## before our eyes  ##

AT: "in our sight" or "while we were present"

## keep the children of death alive ##

AT: "keep alive those who are condemned to die" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
