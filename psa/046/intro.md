# Psalm 046 General Notes #

#### Type of psalm ####

Psalm 46 is a psalm of praise to God.

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

God is all-powerful, controls nature, and protects his people. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/peopleofgod]])
