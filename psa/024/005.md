## He will receive a blessing ##

The word "he" refers to the ones with pure hearts mentioned in previous verse.

## and righteousness from the God of his salvation ##

AT: "and God will deal righteously with him and save him." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

## those who seek him ##

AT: "those who approach God"

## those who seek the face of the God of Jacob ##

AT: "those who worship the God of Jacob"
