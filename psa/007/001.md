## A musical composition of David ##

“This is a song that David wrote”

## concerning the words of Cush the Benjamite ##

AT: "about what Cush, a man from the tribe of Benjamin, said."

## rip me apart like a lion, tearing me in pieces with no one else able to bring me to safety ##

AT: "violently kill me with no one else to save me." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
