# in his presence

"where he is"

# Splendor and majesty are in his presence

The psalmist speaks as if splendor and majesty are people who can stand before a king. AT: "Everyone knows of his splendor and majesty" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

# Strength and beauty are in his sanctuary

The words "strength" and "beauty" are metonyms for the ark of the covenant decrees, which is found in the sanctuary. AT: "It is his sanctuary that contains the ark of the covenant decrees" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
