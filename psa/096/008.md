# Give to Yahweh the glory that his name deserves

The abstract noun "glory" can be stated as a verb or adjective. See how these words are translated in [Psalms 29:2](../029/002.md). Alternate translation: "Honor Yahweh just as his name deserves" or "Proclaim that Yahweh is glorious just as his name deserves" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

# his name deserves

Here "name" refers to the person of God. Alternate translation: "he deserves" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# his courts

the temple courtyard where the priests sacrificed animals to Yahweh
