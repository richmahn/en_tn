## translationNotes

* **let the heavens ... the earth... the sea ... the fields ... the trees** - All of these things are said to have emotions like people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **He is coming to judge the earth. He will judge the world ... and the peoples ... ** - The three phrase have similar meanings; the last two phases are used to strengthen the first. ( See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **and the peoples with his faithfulness** - AT: " and he will judge the peoples with his faithfulness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
