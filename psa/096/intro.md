# Psalm 096 General Notes #

#### Type of psalm ####

Psalm 96 is one of six worship psalms (Psalms 95-100).

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Yahweh, the king #####
Yahweh is king over all the earth and is worthy of all praise.
