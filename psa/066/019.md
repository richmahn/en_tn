## But God has truly heard; he has paid attention ... ##

These two phrase have similar meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "But God has truly heard me and paid attention to the voice of my prayer." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

##  the voice of my prayer ##

Here the author's prayer is given the quality of having a voice. AT: "my prayer" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## or his covenant faithfulness from me ##

AT: "or turned his covenant faithfulness away from me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
