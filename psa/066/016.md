## he was praised with my tongue ##

## seen sin ##

"loved iniquity" or "held iniquity" or "found wicked, sinful things"

## will not hear ##

AT: "will not hear me call out to him" or "he will not hear my prayer" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
