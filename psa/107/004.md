## translationNotes

* **They wandered** - "Some people wandered"
* **on a desert road** - "on a road that was in the desert"
* **in which to live** - "that they could live in"
* **they called out to Yahweh** - It is implied that they are praying to Yahweh so that he will help them. AT: "they prayed to Yahweh for help" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **distress ** - "difficulties" or "afflictions"
