## He settles the hungry there ##

"Yahweh makes people who are hungry live there"

## there ##

in the land where he turned the desert into a lake and the dry land into springs

##  to plant fields in, to plant vineyards ##

AT: "and plant crops and fruit"

## and to bring in an abundant harvest ##

"and they grow produce that they can harvest"

## so they are very numerous ##

"so that there are very many people"

## He does not let their cattle decrease in number ##

"He will not make their animals few in number." AT: "He gives them very many animals." (See: [[[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]]]])
