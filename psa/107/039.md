## They ##

The word "They" refers to the people who were hungry but Yahweh blessed them.

## were diminished ##

"became fewer in number"

## and brought low by painful distress and suffering ##

AT: "because their leaders oppressed and made them suffer." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## pours contempt on ##

AT: "shows contempt for." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## the leaders ##

"the nobility" or "the upper class"

## where there are no roads ##

"where people never go."
