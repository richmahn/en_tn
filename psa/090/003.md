## translationNotes

* **You return man to dust** - AT: "You return man to dust when he dies" (See:[[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **"Return, you descendants** - AT: "Return to dust, you descendants"
* **a thousand years** - "1000 years" (See:[[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
* **For a thousand years in your sight are as yesterday when it is past, and as a watch in the night.** - AT: "You see a thousand years the same as yesterday when it has passed by, or as a few short hours in the night." or "Even a thousand years is not a long time to you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
