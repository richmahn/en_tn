## translationNotes

* **we are consumed** - The word "we" here refers to God's people
* **and in your wrath we are terrified** - AT: "and when you are angry we are greatly afraid"
* **You have set our iniquities before you, our hidden sins in the light of your presence** - AT: "You keep remembering even our secret sins."
