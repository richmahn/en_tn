# the way ... his way

How God wants a person to behave is spoken of as if it were a way or path on which a person travels. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# the humble

This nominal adjective can be stated as an adjective. AT: "humble people" or "those who are humble" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-nominaladj]])
