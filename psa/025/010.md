## translationNotes

* **All the paths of Yahweh are made from covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness** - The phrase "paths of Yahweh" refers to the way he lives and behaves. AT: Yahweh is always faithful because of his covenant and he is always trustworthy. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **for your name's sake** - The phrase “your name” here refers to Yahweh's reputation. AT: “for your reputation” or “so that people will honor you”
* **pardon my sin for it is great** - AT: "Please pardon me, for I have sinned much" or "I have sinned terribly; please forgive me"
