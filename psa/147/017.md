## He dispenses  ##

AT: "He sends"

## hail like crumbs ##

AT: "hail easily" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## hail  ##

small pieces of ice that fall from the sky like rain

## who can withstand the cold he sends? ##

AT: "no one can live in the cold he sends." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## He sends out his command and melts them ##

"He commands the ice to melt"
