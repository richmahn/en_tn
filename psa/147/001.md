# General Information:

Parallelism is common in Hebrew poetry. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-poetry]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

# it is pleasant

The object may be supplied from the previous phrase. AT: "it is pleasant to sing praises" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

# praise is suitable

"praise is fitting" or "praise is appropriate"
