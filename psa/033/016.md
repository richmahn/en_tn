## translationNotes

* **No king is saved by a vast army** - "There is no king who is saved by a great army" AT: "Kings are not saved by their great strength"
* **A horse is false security** - The horse stands for the strongest part of the army. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
