## See ##

This word functions to show that a new theme begins in the Psalm. AT: "Look” or “Listen” or “Pay attention to what I am about to tell you”

## Yahweh's eye ##

AT: "Yahweh's attention" or "Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## who count on ##

"who wait for" or "who hope for"

## to deliver their lives from death ##

AT: "to keep them from dying" or "to protect them from danger" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
