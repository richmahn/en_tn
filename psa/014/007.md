* **Oh, that the salvation of Isreal would come from Zion!** - David is saying he longs for help from God in Jerusalem. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Jacob** - Here "Jacob" refers to the people of Isreal, the descendents of Jacob.
