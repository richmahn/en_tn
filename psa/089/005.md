## translationNotes

* **your truthfulness is praised in the assembly of the holy ones.** - AT: "The assembly of the holy ones praises your truthfulness." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **For who in the skies can be compared to Yahweh? Who among the sons of the gods is like Yahweh?** - The writer is saying that there is no one in heaven or on earth like Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
