## your truthfulness is praised in the assembly of the holy ones. ##

AT: "The assembly of the holy ones praises your truthfulness." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## For who in the skies can be compared to Yahweh? Who among the sons of the gods is like Yahweh? ##

The writer is saying that there is no one in heaven or on earth like Yahweh. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
