## the council of the holy ones ##

AT: "the gathering of heavenly beings"

## who is strong like you, Yahweh? ##

The writer asks the question to emphasize there is no one like Yahweh. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Your truthfulness surrounds you. ##

AT: "Your faithfulness surrounds you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])
