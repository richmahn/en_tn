## made him rule over the works ... put all things under his feet ##

These two clauses express similar meanings. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## the works of your hands ##

This means everything that God created. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## put all things under his feet ##

This means that God gave his people authority over all creation.
