# how magnificent is your name in all the earth

With this exclamation, David shows his joy and awe about how great God is. AT: "your name is wonderfully magnificent in all the earth" or "people in all the earth know how magnificent you are" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclamations]])

# your name

God's "name" represents him or his reputation. AT: "your reputation" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# magnificent

"excellent" or "great"
