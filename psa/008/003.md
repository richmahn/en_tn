## translationNotes

* **your heavens, which your fingers have made** - AT: "the heavens which you have made," (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **Of what importance is the human race that you notice them, or mankind that you pay attention to them?** - These remarks have been expressed in the form of a question to add emphasis. AT: "It is amazing that you think about people and are concerned about them!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **crowned them with glory and honor** - The words "glory and honor" are similar in meaning. AT: "caused them to be like kings." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
