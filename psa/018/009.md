## translationNotes

* **He opened** - The word "He" refers to Yahweh.
* **thick darkness was under his feet** - Though Yahweh does not actually have feet, human characteristics are given to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **wings of the wind** - The wind does not actually have wings, but it treats the wind like an angel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
