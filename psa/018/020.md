## my hands were clean ##

AT: "all my actions were right" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## I have kept the ways of Yahweh ##

"I have paid attention to the ways of Yahweh" AT: "I have obeyed Yahweh's laws" (UDB) (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## have not wickedly turned away from my God ##

"have not become wicked by turning away from my God"
