## Yahweh is my rock  ##

The word "rock" is a picture of a safe place. God is not an actual rock. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## my rock, my fortress  ##

These two phrases contain similar ideas. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## my shield, the horn of my salvation, and my stronghold ##

A similar idea is repeated three ways for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
