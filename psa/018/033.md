## makes my feet swift ##

"makes my feet very fast" AT: "makes me run very fast" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## like a deer and places me on the mountains ##

The deer is especially quick and stable in the mountains. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
