## translationNotes

* **have lifted up their voice; the oceans' waves crash and roar** - AT: "have made a mighty noise because their waves crash and roar" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **roar** - make a long, loud sound.
* **Above the crashing of many waves, the mighty breakers of the sea, ** - AT: "But Yahweh you are greater than the roar of the oceans or the crash of their mighty breakers." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **breakers** - large waves coming to land
