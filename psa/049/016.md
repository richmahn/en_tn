## translationNotes

* **he will take nothing away** - "he will not keep anything"
* **his power will not descend with him** - The word "descend" refers to when a person dies. AT: "his power will not go with him when he dies" or "he will not keep his power when he dies" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_euphemism]])
