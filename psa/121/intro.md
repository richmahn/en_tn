# Psalm 121 General Notes #

#### Type of psalm ####

Psalm 121 is a worship psalm.

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Yahweh's protection #####
God always watches over those who believe in him and protects them from harm. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/believe]])
