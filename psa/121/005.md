## Yahweh is the shade at your right hand. ##

AT: "God protects you from things that would hurt you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## The sun will not harm you by day, nor the moon by night. ##

AT: "God protects you during the day and the night." or "God protects you from the elements during all the time." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])
