## translationNotes

* **Yahweh is the shade at your right hand.** - AT: "God protects you from things that would hurt you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **The sun will not harm you by day, nor the moon by night.** - AT: "God protects you during the day and the night." or "God protects you from the elements during all the time." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_merism]])
