# arise

Deciding to do something and beginning to do it is spoken of as getting up. AT: "do something" or "take action" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

# all the nations

The word "nations" here represents the people of the nations that do not honor God. AT: "the people of all the nations" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
