## They ##

The word "They" refers to God's terrifying acts of anger.

## They surround me like water all the day long ##

AT: "All day long they threaten to destroy me like a flood" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## they have all encircled me ##

AT: "They have surrounded me like enemy soldiers" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## every friend and acquaintance ##

AT: "every person I love, or even know slightly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-hyperbole]])
