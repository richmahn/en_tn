## translationNotes

* **the king** - David is speaking about himself. AT: "I, the king of Israel" UDB
* **swears by him ... proud of him** - The word "him" refers to "God".
* **but the mouth of those who speak lies will be stopped up** - AT: "but God will silence the mouths of liars" (See:  [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **the mouths of those who speak lies** - AT: "liars" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
