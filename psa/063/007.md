## in the shadow of your wings I rejoice ##

This compares God to a mother bird that protects her baby bird under her wing. AT: "I rejoice because you protect me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## I cling to you ##

"I need you" or "I depend on you"

## your right hand supports me ##

Here, the right hand is used as a symbol of strength. AT: "you support me" or "you uphold me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
