## translationNotes

* **hit me** - AT: "rebuke me" or "correct me" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **may my head not refuse to accept it** - AT: "may I accept it" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublenegatives]])
* **Their leaders will be thrown down** - Possible meanings are 1) "Someone will throw their leaders down" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]] and UDB) or 2) "Their leaders will throw them down."
* **cliffs** - ground that goes straight down a long way
* **our bones have been scattered** - Possible meanings are 1) "people have thrown our bones around in different directions" or 2) as a result of the fall from the cliffs (141:6) "our bodies are broken and our bones lie in disorder"
