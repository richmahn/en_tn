# will hear them

"will hear my enemies" or "will hear what my enemies say." Some versions say "will hear me."

# humiliate them

How God will humiliate them can be stated clearly. AT: "will defeat and humiliate them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
