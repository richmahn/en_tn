## translationNotes

* **them** - "my enemies" or "David's enemies"
* **for wickedness is where they live, right among them** - These two phrases mean basically the same thing and are used together for emphasis. AT: "for they live in wickedness, and wickedness is among them"(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
