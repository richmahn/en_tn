# I said

Possible meanings are 1) the psalmist was speaking to himself or 2) the psalmist was speaking to Yahweh.

# the changing of the right hand of the Most High toward us

The "right hand" is a metonym for power, authority or strength. AT: "the Most High no longer uses his power to help us" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
