## translationNotes

* **rejoice and be glad** - These two phrases mean the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **the Lord thinks about me** - AT: "the Lord cares for me"
* **You are my help** - "You are the one who helps me"
* **you come to my rescue** - "you come to save me"
