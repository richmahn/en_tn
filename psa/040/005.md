## translationNotes

* **your thoughts...cannot be numbered...more than could be counted** - (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **your thoughts which are about us cannot be numbered** - AT: "no one can count all the things you think about us"  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **You have no delight in sacrifice or offering** - AT: "Sacrifices and offerings are not what make you happy"
* **you have opened my ears** - AT: "you have caused me to hear and understand your commands" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **you have not required burnt offerings or sin offerings** - AT: "Burnt offerings and sin offerings are not what you require of us"
