## A psalm of David ##

“This is a psalm that David wrote.”

## whom should I fear? ##

This question emphasizes that there is no one that David should fear. AT: "There is no one that I should be afraid of." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## whom should I dread? ##

This question emphasizes that there is no one that David should be afraid of". AT: "There is no one that I should dread" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
