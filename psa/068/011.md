## translationNotes

* David continues the story of the journey of the Israelites through the desert. In this part of the story, the Israelites are victorious in war over their enemies.
* **those who announced them...army** - Many people heard and obeyed the Lord's commands. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **Kings of armies flee, they flee** - The enemies of God's people were defeated. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **plunder** - things that are taken from the defeated army and brought to the victorious army's home.
* **doves covered with silver...gold** - This means that some of the plunder is very valuable because it is covered with precious metals. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* ** When some of you people stayed among the sheepfolds, why did you do this?** - AT: "Those who stayed among the sheepfolds should not have stayed; they should have gone to the battle." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
