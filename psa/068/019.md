## will strike through the heads of his enemies ##

This means that God will kill them. AT: 'will strike the head of his enemies, killing them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## through the hairy scalps ##

It seems to have been the custom for soldiers not to cut their hair during the time of war. AT: "he will strike the hairy scalps of those people who" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

## of those who walk in offenses against him ##

AT: "of those who habitually offend him"
