# twenty thousand, thousands upon thousands

This is probably not meant to be an exact number, but to indicate a large number. AT: "many thousands" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

# ascended

To "ascend" is to to move up, to go toward the sky.
