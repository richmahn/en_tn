## Your God, Israel, has decreed your strength ##

Some versions understand the Hebrew text differently: "Summon (or use) your strength, God."

## to us ##

The word "us" refers to the people David is speaking to and to himself. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])

## Reveal your power to us from your temple at Jerusalem ##

AT: "As you are present in your temple in Jerusalem, send out your power to help us."
