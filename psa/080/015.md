# This is the root that your right hand planted

The right hand represents the Yahweh's power and control. Alternate translation: "This is the root that you, Yahweh, planted" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# shoot

the part of a new plant that is just starting to grow above the ground. See how you translated this in [Psalms 80:11](./011.md).
