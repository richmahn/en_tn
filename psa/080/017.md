## your hand ##

This refers to Yahweh's power and control. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## the man of your right hand ##

This refers to the nation of Israel which Yahweh has chosen as his people. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## right hand ##

The side of a person that is favored and gives honor to the one chosen to stand next to that person. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
