# Psalm 097 General Notes #

#### Type of psalm ####

Psalm 97 is one of six worship psalms (Psalms 95-100)

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Yahweh's authority #####
All people who worship idols are disgraced because Yahweh rules over all the world.
