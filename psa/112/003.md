The person who sings this song continues to describe a person who reveres Yahweh.

## Light shines in the darkness for the godly person ##

"When bad things happen, righteous people have hope and a way to escape." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## lends money ##

The man lends money in order to help people who are having a hard time and not in order to take advantage of other people.

## who conducts his affairs with honesty ##

"he is just when taking care of his own business"
