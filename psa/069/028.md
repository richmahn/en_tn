## Let them be blotted out ##

AT: "Blot them out" or "Wipe their names out" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## and not be written down ##

AT: " and let them not be written down" or "and do not write their names down" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

## let your salvation, God, set me up on high. ##

AT: "God, by the salvation you give me, set me up on high"
