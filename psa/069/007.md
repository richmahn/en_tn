## For your sake ##

"On your behalf" or "For you"

## I have borne rebuke ##

AT: "I have received rebuke" or "People have rebuked me"

## shame has covered my face ##

AT: "I feel the heat in my face from my shame" or "I have felt very ashamed"

## an alien ... ##

AT: "I have become an alien ..." or "I have become like a foreigner ..."

## the zeal of your house has eaten me up ##

AT: "the zeal I have for your house consumes me" or "I am filled up by the zeal I have for your house" (See:  [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])
