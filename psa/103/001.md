## A psalm of David ##

"This is a psalm that David wrote."

## With all my life, I give praise to Yahweh; and with all that is within me, I  give praise to his holy name. ##

AT: "I should give praise to Yahweh, and to his glorious name, with my whole life" (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## and all that is within me ##

"my complete physical and non-physical being" (See: Synecdoche)
