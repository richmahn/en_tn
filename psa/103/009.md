# He will not always discipline; he is not always angry

These two phrases have similar meanings. The second one strengthens the thought in the first. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

# He does not deal with us ... or repay us

These two phrases are parallel and emphasize that God does not punish us as we deserve. AT: "He does not repay us with the punishment we deserve for our sins" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
