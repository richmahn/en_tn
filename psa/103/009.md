## translationNotes

* **He will not always discipline; he is not always angry.** - These two phrases have similar meanings. The second one strengthens the thought in the first.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **He does not deal with us ... or repay us ...** - These two phrases are parallel and emphasize that God does not punish us as we deserve.(See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
