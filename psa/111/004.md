## gracious and merciful ##

These two words have basically the same meaning and are used together for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## call to mind ##

AT: "remember" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## his powerful works ##

AT: "his power" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## inheritance of the nations ##

This is a reference to the land of other nations. This land would have normally been inherited.
