# the beginning of wisdom

The word "wisdom" can be translated as "wise." AT: "the first step to becoming wise" or "the most important thing to become wise" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

# those who carry out his instructions

"those who obey his instructions"

# His praise endures forever

The word "praise" can be translated as a verb. AT: "People will praise him forever" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
