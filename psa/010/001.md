## Why, Yahweh, do you stand far off? Why do you hide yourself in times of trouble? ##

AT: "Yahweh, it seems that you are very far away!" AT: "Yahweh, It seems as though you hide from me whenever I am in trouble." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## schemes ##

"plans"

## his deepest desires ##

AT: "the evil things that he wants to do." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## greedy ##

This is a when someone desires more things selfishly.
