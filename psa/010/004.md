## the wicked man ##

In this phrase, "the wicked man" is representing many wicked people. (See UDB)

## he does not care at all about him ##

AT: "he has no concern for him." or "they have no concern for him."

## He is secure at all times ##

He is not really secure, but he seems to be secure to others. (See UDB)

## snorts ##

This means to make an offensive sound with the nose and mouth.
