## God has forgotten ##

AT: "God does not care;"

## he covers his face ##

AT: "God refuses to see what is happening;" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## lift up your hand ##

Here "your hand" represents God's power to punish. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
