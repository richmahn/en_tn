## translationNotes

* **He says** - AT: "The wicked people say"
* **his heart** - AT: "their hearts,"
* **God has forgotten** - AT: "God does not care;"
* **he covers his face** - AT: "God refuses to see what is happening;" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **lift up your hand** - Here "your hand" represents God's power to punish. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
