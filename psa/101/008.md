# Morning by morning

"Every day"

# the wicked

This refers to wicked people. Alternate translation: "the wicked people" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-nominaladj]])

# from the city of Yahweh

David is referring to the city that he is in as "the city of Yahweh." This can be made clear. Alternate translation: "from this city, which is Yahweh's city" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
