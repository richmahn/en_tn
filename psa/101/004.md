## translationNotes

* **a proud demeanor and an arrogant attitude** - These two phrases have the same meaning and are used to emphasize the concept of pride. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **demeanor** - "attitude" or "personality"
* **look to the faithful** - AT: "look to God's faithful people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **walk in the way of integrity** - AT: "live lives full of integrity" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
