## a proud demeanor and an arrogant attitude ##

These two phrases have the same meaning and are used to emphasize the concept of pride. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## demeanor ##

"attitude" or "personality"

## look to the faithful ##

AT: "look to God's faithful people" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## walk in the way of integrity ##

AT: "live lives full of integrity" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
