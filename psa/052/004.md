## translationNotes

* **that devour others** - Here "words" are being compared to an animal devouring people. AT: "that cause others great pain" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **take you up…pluck you…root you** - All three of these phrases are different ways of saying "to remove". (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **you deceitful tongue** - This refers to the person the author is speaking to. AT: "you speaker of deceit" or "you who speaks deceitful words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
