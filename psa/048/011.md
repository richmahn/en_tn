## the daughters of Judah ##

Smaller cities in Judah are referred to as daughters because they developed within the nation. The smaller cities refer to the people who live in those cities. AT: "the smaller cities of Judah" or "the people who live in the smaller cities in Judah" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
