## translationNotes

* **May the person be blessed, whoever pays you back** - AT: "may God bless the one who pays you back" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **pays you back for what you have done to us** - "does to you what you did to us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_apostrophe]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **May the person be blessed** - AT: "God will certainly bless him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **dashes your little ones against the rock** - "smashes your babies' heads against rocks"
