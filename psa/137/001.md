## by the rivers ##

AT: "Next to one of the rivers near Babylon"

## we sat ... we thought ... we hung ##

The writer does not include the readers. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-exclusive]])

## On the poplars there ##

"Poplars" do not grow in "Zion." "On the trees in Babylon" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
