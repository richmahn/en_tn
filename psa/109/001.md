## For the wicked and deceitful ##

The words "wicked" and "deceitful" have similar meaning and are used together for emphasis. AT: "For wicked and deceitful men" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]] and  [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
