## translationNotes

* **May his curses...cover himself** - People wore their clothes everyday. AT: "Let his curses be close to him every day like the garment he wears." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **like the belt he always wears** - AT: "and let his curses always be on him like the belt he always wears." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_ellipsis]])
