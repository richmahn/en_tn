## May his curses...cover himself ##

People wore their clothes everyday. AT: "Let his curses be close to him every day like the garment he wears." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## like the belt he always wears ##

AT: "and let his curses always be on him like the belt he always wears." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])
