## the creditor ##

someone who lends money to another person but expects that the person will pay the money back

## plunder ##

## May his children be cut off; may their name be blotted out ... ##

These two phrases have similar meanings. AT: "May his children be destroyed; may he have no descendants in the next generation." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
