## with silver and gold ##

AT: "with silver and gold in their possession" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## none of his tribes stumbled on the way ##

AT: "no one was unable to make the journey" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## Egypt was glad ##

AT: The people of Egypt were glad (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## He spread a cloud for a covering ##

AT: "He placed a cloud in the sky to protect them from the sun and heat"
