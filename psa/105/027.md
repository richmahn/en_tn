# They performed his signs among the Egyptians ... his wonders in the land of Ham

These two phrases have basically the same meaning. Alternate translation: "Moses and Aaron performed God's miracles in Egypt among the descendants of Ham" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

# his wonders in the land of Ham

The words "they performed" are understood fromt the previous phrase. They may be repeated here. Alternate translation: "and they performed his wonders in the land of Ham" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-ellipsis]])

# the land of Ham

Ham was an ancestor of the people of Egypt. Alternate translation: "the land of Ham's descendants" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
