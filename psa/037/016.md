## Better is the little that the righteous has ##

AT: "It is better to be righteous, even if it means that you are poor..."

## than the abundance of many wicked people. ##

AT: "...than to be wicked with great wealth." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
