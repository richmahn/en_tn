## his day is coming ##

AT: "the day is coming when Yahweh will judge and punish the wicked people"

## gnashes his teeth against him ##

This picture shows the hatred wicked people feel for righteous people so their teeth clench together. "Teeth" represents the person. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
