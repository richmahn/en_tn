## Yahweh's enemies will be like the glory of the pastures ##

The writer compares Yahweh's enemies and the flowers blooming in the fields. (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## and disappear in the smoke ##

The writer compares the fields that are burned off after the harvest with the wicked. AT:"his enemies will be defeated and cease to exist." (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
