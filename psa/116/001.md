## translationNotes

* See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_poetry]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]]
* **I love** - The word "I" refers to the person who wrote this psalm. We do not know who this person was.
* **my voice** - AT: "what I say." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **will call on him** - "call to him" or "pray to him"
