## translationNotes

* The person who made this song continues to speak.
* **and fair** - "and righteous" or "and does what is right"
* **our God** - The word "our" refers to the person who made the song and the rest of the people of Israel.
* **the naive** - "people who are ignorant" or "those who are inexperienced"
* **I was brought low** - AT: "I was in danger" or "I was helpless." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
