## translationNotes

* This begins a new theme: Asaph is proclaiming God's mighty deeds in the history of his people.
* **You divided the sea by your strength** - The word "You" here represents Yahweh. This sentence shows Yahweh's great power. It may refer to creation of the world or to when the Israelites crossed the Reed Sea.
* **the sea** - AT: "great waters."
