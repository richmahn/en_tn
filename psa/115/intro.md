# Psalm 115 General Notes #

#### Type of psalm ####

Psalm 115 is a psalm of praise. 

#### Special concepts in this chapter ####

##### Yahweh's power #####
God is in heaven and is able to do whatever he wants. Idols can not do anything. Therefore, people and priests should trust God for their protection. (See: [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/heaven]], [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/priest]] and [[rc://en/tw/dict/bible/kt/trust]])
