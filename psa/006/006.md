## I drench my bed with tears ... I wash my couch away with my tears ##

Both of these clauses express the same meaning. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## I drench my bed with tears ##

"I wet my bed with my tears;"

## My eyes grow dim from grief ##

AT: "I am grieving so much that I cannot see well;" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
