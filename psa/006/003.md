## Return ##

AT: "Have mercy" or "Come back for me"

## in death there is no remembrance of you ... In sheol who will give you thanks? ##

Both of these clauses express similar meanings. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## In sheol, who will give you thanks? ##

The speaker puts this remark in the form of a question to add emphasis. AT: "The dead cannot praise you!" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
