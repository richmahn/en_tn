# insight and understanding

The words "insight" and "understanding" mean the same thing and emphasize that Gabriel will help Daniel to understand the message completely. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

# the order was given

This can be stated in active form. AT: "God gave the order" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# consider this word

"think about this message"

# the revelation

This refers back to the prophesy of Jeremiah in [Daniel 9:2](./01.md).
