## He gave me understanding and said to me ##

The repetition is to emphasize that Gabriel is the reason Daniel will understand the vision. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## insight and understanding ##

The two words "insight" and "understanding" mean the same thing and are repeated for emphasis.

## the order was given ##

AT: "God gave the order" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## consider this word ##

"think about this message". The word "word" is the same the word as "order" earlier in this verse. Translate the same way if possible.

## the revelation ##

This refers back to the prophesy of Jeremiah in [Daniel 9:1](./01.md).
