## who had been made king over the realm of the Babylonians ##

AT: "who became king over the realm of the Babylonians" or "who conquered the Babylonians" (UDB). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## over the realm ##

"over the country" or "over the kingdom"

## there would be seventy years until Jerusalem's abandonment would end ##

"from the time Jerusalem was destroyed, it would be abandoned for 70 years" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
