## One who looked like a human being ##

This refers to the one who had just spoken to Daniel. AT: "This one who looked like a human"

## the vision has turned my anguish on me ##

"the vision has made my anguish make me very weak" or "because I saw this vision, my fear makes me weak"

## anguish ##

severe emotional suffering

## there is no breath left in me ##

AT: "I cannot breathe" or "it's very hard to breathe" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]]).
