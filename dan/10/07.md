## So I was left alone and saw ##

AT: “No one was with me, and I saw” (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## my radiant appearance was fearfully changed ##

AT: "I was so afraid that my normal appearance changed and I did not look like myself."  (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]]).

## I fell on my face in deep sleep ##

Daniel was so afraid by what he saw that he fainted and fell forward so that his face was toward the ground.   (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
