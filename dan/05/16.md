# you will be clothed with purple and have a gold chain placed around your neck

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "I will give you purple clothes and a gold neck chain" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# clothed with purple

Purple cloth was rare and reserved for royal officials. Alternate translation: "dressed in royal clothing" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# the third highest ruler

"the number three ruler" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-ordinal]])
