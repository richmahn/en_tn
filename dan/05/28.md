# 'Peres,' 'your kingdom

"'Peres' means 'your kingdom."

# Peres

This is the singular form of "Pharsin" in 5:25.

# your kingdom is divided and is given to the Medes and Persians

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "God has divided your kingdom and given it to the Medes and Persians" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
