## Belshazzar ##

This is the son of Nebuchadnezzar who became king after his father. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## for a thousand ##

"for 1,000" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## he drank wine in front of ##

"he drank wine in the presence of"

## the containers made of gold or silver ##

AT: "the containers that the Israelites had made of gold or sliver" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## Nebuchadnezzar his father had taken ##

AT: "the army of Nebuchadnezzar his father had taken" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
