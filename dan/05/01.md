* **Belshazzar** - This is the son of Nebuchadnezzar who became king after his father. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **for a thousand** - "for 1,000" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:translate_numbers]])
