# until the Ancient of Days came, and justice was given

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "until the Ancient of Days came and brought justice" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# Ancient of Days

This is a title for God that emphasizes that he is eternal. See how you translated this title in [Daniel 7:9](../07/09.md).

# the holy people received the kingdom

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "God gave his kingdom to his holy people" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
