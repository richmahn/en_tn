## a dream and visions ##

The words “dream” and “visions” both refer to the same dream that is described in this chapter. AT: “visions while he was dreaming”. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## the four winds of heaven ##

"winds from everywhere" or "strong winds from all four directions" (UDB)

## stirring up ##
