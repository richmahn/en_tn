## translationNotes

* **His forces will rise up** - AT: "His army will appear" or "His army will come." The word "His" refers to the king of the North. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **the abomination that causes complete desolation** - AT: "the disgusting thing that makes the temple unclean" or "the disgusting thing that causes God to abandon the temple"
* **seduce them** - "pull them to himself" or "turn them from God"
* **who know their God** - AT: "who are faithful to God"
* **will be strong and take action** - "will be firm and resist them"
