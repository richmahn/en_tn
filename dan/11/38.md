## Instead of them ##

The word "them" refers to the gods mentioned in [Daniel 11:36](./36.md)

## the god of fortresses ##

"the god that controls fortresses." He probably believes that this false god will either protect his fortresses or help him to attack other people's fortresses.

## he will divide up the land for a price ##

Possible meanings are 1) "he will give the land to his followers as a reward" or 2) "he will sell land to his followers"
