## I was living happily ... in my palace ##

These two phrases mean basically the same thing. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## But a dream I had ... my mind troubled me ##

Both "dream" and "visions" are parallel because they mean the same thing. Also, "afraid" and "troubled me" are parallel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
