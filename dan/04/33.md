# General Information:

Verses 19-33 use the third person to describe the punishment of Nebuchadnezzar. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-123person]])

# This decree against Nebuchadnezzar was carried out immediately

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "This decree against Nebuchadnezzar happened immediately" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# He was driven away from people

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "People chased him away from them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# his nails became like birds' claws

"his fingernails looked like birds' claws"
