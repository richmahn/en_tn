## translationNotes

* Daniel continues talking to the king.
* **Just as you saw** - Nebuchadnezzar saw that the feet had been made of clay and iron. He did not see the process of making the feet.
* **were partly made of baked clay and partly made of iron** - AT: "were a mixture of baked clay and iron" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **they will not hold together** - AT: "they will not be united"
