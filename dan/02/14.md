## translationNotes

* **Arioch** - (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **prudence and discretion** - "caution and careful judgement." These two words mean basically the same thing. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **bodyguard** - This is a group of people whose job is to protect the king.
* **who had come to kill** - "who the king had sent out to kill"
* **requested an appointment with the king** - "asked for a set time to meet with the king"
