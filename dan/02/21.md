# General Information:

These verses are also part of Daniel's prayer.

# he removes kings

"he takes away kings' authority to rule"

# places kings on their thrones

Here being on the "throne" refers to ruling over a kingdom. AT: "makes new kings rule over their kingdoms" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# the light lives with him

"the light comes from where God is"
