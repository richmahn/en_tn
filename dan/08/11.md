## It became great, even as great as ##

"The goat's horn raised itself up as high as" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## The regular burnt offering was taken away from him ##

"The horn took away the regular burnt offering from the divine commander" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]]) and (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## and the place of the sanctuary was polluted ##

"and it made the place of the sanctuary unclean" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]]) and (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## The horn will throw truth to the ground ##

This means that the horn will ignore truth and godliness. AT: "The horn will disregard truth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]]) and (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])
