## translationNotes

* **It became great, even as great as** - "The goat's horn raised itself up as high as" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **The regular burnt offering was taken away from him** - "The horn took away the regular burnt offering from the divine commander" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) and (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **and the place of the sanctuary was polluted** - "and it made the place of the sanctuary unclean" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]]) and (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **The horn will throw truth to the ground** - This means that the horn will ignore truth and godliness. AT: "The horn will disregard truth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]]) and (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
