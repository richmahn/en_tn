# a deep sleep

This is a type of sleep when someone is sleeping heavily and does not wake up easily.

# the time of wrath

This refers to the time when God will judge. This can be made explicit. AT: "the time when God judges in anger" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

# the appointed time for the end

"the time when the world will end"
