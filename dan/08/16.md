# a man's voice calling between the banks of the Ulai Canal

Here a man is being referred to by his voice. Alternate translation: "a man calling from the Ulai Canal" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

# Ulai Canal

A canal is a narrow man-made waterway. See how you translated this name in [Daniel 8:2](./02.md).
