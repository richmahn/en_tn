## I will make ##

Here "I" refers to Yahweh.

## will be crushed to pieces...will be burned up ##

AT: "I will crush to pieces...I will burn up" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## I will pull her building stones ##

Here "her" refers to the city of Samaria.

## For from the gifts to her prostitution she gathered them, and as a prostitute's pay they will return ##

Often worshiping idols included having sex with a prostitute at the pagan temple. (see: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])
