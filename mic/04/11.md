# General Information:

Jerusalem will defeat her enemies.

# Let her be defiled

This can be translated in active form. Alternate translation: "Let us defile her" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# let our eyes gloat over Zion

The eye is a synecdoche for the whole person. Alternate translation: "let us gloat over Zion" or "let us enjoy watching the invaders destroy Zion" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
