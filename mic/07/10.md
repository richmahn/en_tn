## translationNotes

* **Then my enemy** - Micah refers not only to himself. He means the enemy of all the people of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Where is Yahweh your God?** - The enemy uses a question to mock the people of Israel. AT: "Yahweh your God cannot help you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **My eyes** - AT: "I" or "We" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **will look at her** - Here "her" refers to the enemies who harmed the people of Israel.
* **she will be trampled down** - AT: "their enemies will trample them down" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
