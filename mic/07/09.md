## translationNotes

* **Because I sinned** - Micah is not only referring to himself. He means all the people of Israel have sinned. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **until he pleads my cause, and executes judgment for me** - This refers to when God decides he has punished his people enough. Then he will punish the people of the other nations who harmed the people of Israel.
* **He will bring me to the light** - AT: "He will come and save us from our enemies. It will be like bringing a person in the dark into the light" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
