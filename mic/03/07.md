# The seers will be put to shame, and the diviners will be confused

This can be translated in active form. Alternate translation: "I will cause the seers to be ashamed, and I will confuse the diviners" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# All of them will cover their lips

Here "lips" represents speaking. Alternate translation: "They shall no longer speak" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# there is no answer from me

"I will be silent"
