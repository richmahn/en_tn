## translationNotes

* **The lips of the wise scatter knowledge about** - AT: "Wise men spread knowledge everywhere by what they say" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **not so the hearts of fools** - AT: "but the hearts of fools do not scatter knowledge about" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **the hearts of fools** - AT: "fools" or "foolish people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Yahweh hates the sacrifices of the wicked** - AT: "Yahweh hates it when wicked people sacrifice to him" or "Yahweh hates it when the wicked worship him"
* **the upright** - See how you translated this word in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:03:31]].
* **is his delight** - AT: "pleases him" or "is Yahweh's delight"
