## translationNotes

* **miserable** - "very unhappy" or "really bad"
* **a cheerful heart has an unending feast** - This compares the joy of a cheerful attitude to the joy of a feast. AT: "the person who has a cheerful heart enjoys life as if life were an unending feast" (See [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **an unending feast** - AT: "a feast that will continue forever"
* **great treasure** - See how you translated this in [[:en:bible:notes:pro:15:05]].
* **confusion** - "trouble" or "panic"
