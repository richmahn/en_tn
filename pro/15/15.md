## miserable ##

"very unhappy" or "really bad"

## a cheerful heart has an unending feast ##

This compares the joy of a cheerful attitude to the joy of a feast. AT: "the person who has a cheerful heart enjoys life as if life were an unending feast" (See [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## an unending feast ##

AT: "a feast that will continue forever"
