# he has shed someone's blood

Here "blood" represents a person's life. To "shed blood" means to murder someone. Alternate translation: "he has killed someone" or "he has murdered someone" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# fugitive

a person who is running away to avoid being captured

# until death

"until he dies." This means for the rest of his life. Alternate translation: "all of his life" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
