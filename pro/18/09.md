## is slack ##

"is lazy" or "is not interested"

## is a brother to ##

AT: "is closely related to" or "is very similar to" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## The name of Yahweh is a strong tower ##

AT: "Yahweh protects like a strong tower" (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## runs into it and is safe ##

This expands the metaphor of Yahweh as a strong tower. AT: "is like a person who runs into a tower to protect himself" (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
