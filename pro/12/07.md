# Wicked people are overthrown

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "People will overthrow the wicked people" or "People will remove the wicked people from power" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# house

The term "house" is often used figuratively in the Bible to refer to a person's ancestors, descendants or other relatives. Alternate translation: "family" or "descendants" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
