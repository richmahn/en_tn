## An evil person is trapped by his wicked talk ##

"The wicked things an evil person says will trap him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## just as the work of his hands rewards him ##

"just as the good work he does rewards him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
