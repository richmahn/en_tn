## translationNotes

* **not hear the cry of the poor** - "not listen to the outcry of the poor" AT: "not pay attention to how the poor suffer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **he will not be heard** - AT: "no one will hear him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **A gift in secret ... a concealed gift takes ...** - These two phrases repeat the same idea to emphasize how effective this can be. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **appeases anger** - AT: "turns anger into peace"
