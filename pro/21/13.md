## not hear the cry of the poor ##

"not listen to the outcry of the poor" AT: "not pay attention to how the poor suffer" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## he will not be heard ##

AT: "no one will hear him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## A gift in secret ... a concealed gift takes ... ##

These two phrases repeat the same idea to emphasize how effective this can be. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## appeases anger ##

AT: "turns anger into peace"
