AT: "When rulers do what is just" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## wanders from ##

AT: "walks away from" or "abandons"

## the way of understanding ##

AT: "the wise and just way of living" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## he will rest in the assembly of the dead ##

"he will remain in the assembly of dead spirits" AT: "he will rest in the place where dead spirits come together to rest"
