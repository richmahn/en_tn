## There is no wisdom, understanding, or advice ##

These three words refer to the same thing. AT: "Absolutely no human knowledge" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## The horse is prepared for the day of battle ##

AT: "Soldiers prepare horses for the day of battle" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## the day of battle ##

AT: "when there is a battle" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
