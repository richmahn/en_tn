## your eyes light upon the money ##

AT: "you notice the money" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## suddenly it takes up wings and flies off to the sky like an eagle ##

This describes how quickly money or worldly riches can disappear, out of sight, as if it has grown wings like an eagle. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
