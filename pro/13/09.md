## translationNotes

* **The light ... rejoices** - The light stands for the righteous person himself and is described as experiencing the human emotion of joy. This can also be expressed as a simile, as the UDB does. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **the lamp of the wicked will be put out** - The lamp of the wicked stands for the wicked person himself. The lamp being put out means that something bad will happen to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Pride only breeds conflict** - "Pride always causes conflict"
* **listen to** - AT: "heed" or "follow"
* **good advice** - suggestions that are helpful, profitable
