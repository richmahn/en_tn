## The light ... rejoices ##

The light stands for the righteous person himself and is described as experiencing the human emotion of joy. This can also be expressed as a simile, as the UDB does. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## the lamp of the wicked will be put out ##

The lamp of the wicked stands for the wicked person himself. The lamp being put out means that something bad will happen to him. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
