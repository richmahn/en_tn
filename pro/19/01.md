## Better is a poor person ##

AT: "It is better to be a poor person"

## is perverse in speech ##

## to have desire without knowledge ##

AT: "for a person to be without knowledge" or "to work hard without knowing what you are doing"

## the one who runs too fast misses the path ##

AT: "the one who acts too quickly makes mistakes" or "the one who acts too quickly makes poor choices" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
