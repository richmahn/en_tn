## translationNotes

* **brings shame and reproach** - Possible meanings are 1) he brings it to himself (UDB) or 2) to his family.
* **If you cease to hear instruction** - "If you stop paying attention to instruction" or "if you stop obeying instruction"
* **you will stray from** - AT: "you will abandon" or "you will turn your back on"
