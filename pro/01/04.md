## translationNotes

* **those who have not been taught** - "people who are lacking in experience or understanding" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **discretion** - knowing how to speak and act without making people uncomfortable
* **Let the wise listen** - "People who are already wise should also pay attention"
* **let the discerning get guidance** - "people who have understanding should let these words guide them"
* **riddles** - sayings that one can understand only after thinking about them
