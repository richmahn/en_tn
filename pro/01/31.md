Verse 33 ends Wisdom's statement that began in [Proverbs 1:20](./20.md).

## eat the fruit of their ways ##

"live with the results of what they have done" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## with the fruit of their schemes they will be filled ##

"the results of their plans will be all they possess"

## are killed when they turn away ##

"die because they refuse to learn"

## indifference ##

refusal to do what needs to be done
