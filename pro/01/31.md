## translationNotes

* Verse 33 ends Wisdom's statement that began in [[en/bible/notes/pro/01/20]].
* **eat the fruit of their ways** - "live with the results of what they have done" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **with the fruit of their schemes they will be filled** - "the results of their plans will be all they possess"
* **are killed when they turn away** - "die because they refuse to learn"
* **indifference** - refusal to do what needs to be done
