## Wisdom ##

The writer speaks about "wisdom" as if it were a woman who built her house. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## her animals ##

AT: "meat" or "animal meat" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## mixed her wine ##

In ancient Israel, people often mixed wine with water. AT: "prepared her drinks"

## she has set her table ##

"she has prepared her table" AT: "she has put the whole dinner on her table"
