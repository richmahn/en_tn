## translationNotes

* **they go down to the inner parts of the body ** - Words of gossip go down into the deepest part of a person. This sentence is equivalent to Proverbs [[en/bible/notes/pro/18/07]].
* **The glaze overlaying an earthen vessel is like burning lips and an evil heart** - AT: "People who say nice things while thinking about doing evil things are like a nice glaze covering a container made of earth or clay" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
