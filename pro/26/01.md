## translationNotes

* **Like snow in summer or rain in harvest, so a fool does not deserve honor** - AT: "so honor is not fitting for a fool" Honor is out of place for a fool, like the untimely arrival of snow and rain are out of place. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **so an undeserved curse does not alight ** - AT: "so a causeless curse does not have power"
