## Like snow in summer or rain in harvest, so a fool does not deserve honor ##

AT: "so honor is not fitting for a fool" Honor is out of place for a fool, like the untimely arrival of snow and rain are out of place. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## so an undeserved curse does not alight  ##

AT: "so a causeless curse does not have power"
