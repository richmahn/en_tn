## for there are seven abominations in his heart ##

AT: "for his heart is completely filled with evil thoughts and plans" The number "7" means completeness in this case.

## Though his hatred is covered with deception ##

AT: "though deception covers his hatred" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## his wickedness will be exposed in public meetings ##

AT: "the community of Israel will discover his wickedness" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
