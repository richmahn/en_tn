# with your lips

The lips are a metonym for the words a person speaks. AT: "by what you say" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# pay him back

This is an idiom. "take revenge against him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
