The author describes wisdom as a woman. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## precious ##

"very valuable" or "costly"

## nothing you desire can compare to her ##

"wisdom is better than anything else you desire" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

## her right hand...her left hand ##

"one hand...her other hand" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]])
