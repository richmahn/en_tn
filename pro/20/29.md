## translationNotes

* **Blows that make a wound ... and beatings make ...** - These two phrases basically mean the same thing and are repeated for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **cleanse away evil** - AT: "make a person less evil"
* **make the innermost parts clean** - AT: "make a person better in his innermost parts"
