## a youth is known by his actions ##

AT: "people know a young man by his actions" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## his conduct ##

"his deeds" AT: "what he does"

## pure and upright ##

These two words basically mean the same thing and emphasize how good this young person is. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## Ears that hear and eyes that see ##

The same thought is repeated to emphasize that Yahweh made all our senses. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])
