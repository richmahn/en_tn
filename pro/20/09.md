## translationNotes

* **Who can say, "I have kept my heart clean, I am free from my sin?"** - AT: "No one can say that his heart is clean and that he is free from sin" or "everyone has to say that his heart is not clean and that he is a sinner" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **my heart** - AT: "myself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **I am free from sin** - AT: "I have not sinned"
