## The spirit of a person ... all his inmost parts ##

A person's spirit is like a lamp that Yahweh has given us to see. AT: "Yahweh has given us a spirit to understand our deepest selves, just as a lamp makes you see in the dark" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

to keep safe from harm or loss
