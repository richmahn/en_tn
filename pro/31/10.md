# Who can find a capable wife?

The writer asks a question to show that he is beginning a new section. AT: "Not many men can find a capable wife." or "Not many men can find a wife who is able to do many things well." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

# Her value is far more than jewels

"She is more precious than jewels"

# he will never be poor

This litotes can be stated positively. AT: "he will always have what he needs" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])
