## the fruit of her hands ##

AT: "the money she has earned" or "the result of her hard work" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## She dresses herself with strength ##

AT: "She prepares herself for hard work" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## and makes her arms strong ##

AT: "and she strengthens her arms by doing her work"
