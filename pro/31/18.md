## translationNotes

* **perceives** - "discerns" or "identifies"
* **all night long her lamp is not extinguished** - This refers to her working long into the night while it is dark. She does not work the entire night, from dusk to dawn, this is an exaggeration. AT: "She burns a lamp through the night as she works" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]])
* **spindle** - a thin rod or stick with pointed ends that is used in making thread
