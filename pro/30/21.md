## Under three things the earth trembles, and the fourth it cannot endure: ##

The use of the numbers 'three' and 'four' here is likely a poetic device. AT: "There are some things that make the earth tremble, that it cannot endure. Four of there are:"

## a fool when he is filled with food ##

AT: "a fool when he fills himself with food" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
