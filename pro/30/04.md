## Who has ...? ##

There are 4 questions in this verse beginning with "Who has". These can be written as a statement if necessary. AT: "No one has ..." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## gathered up the wind in hollow of his hands ##

AT: "has caught the wind in his hands"

## has established all the ends of the earth ##

AT: "has set up the limits for where the earth ends" or "has marked the boundaries for the ends of the earth"

## What is his name and what is the name of his son? Surely you know? ##

AT: "Tell me his name and the name of his son, if you know them"
