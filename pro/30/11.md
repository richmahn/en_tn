## A generation that curses their father and does not bless their mother ##

"A generation of people who curse their fathers and do not bless their mothers"

## are pure in their own eyes ##

AT: "believes they are pure" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## they are not washed of their filth ##

AT: "they are not cleansed from their sins" or "they are still dirty with their sins" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
