## And now ##

This is to focus the attention of the son on the conclusion of this lesson.

## Do not let your heart turn aside to her ways ##

AT: "Let your heart stay far away from the ways of the adulterous woman" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]]) Possible meanings are 1) "Stay far away from her" or 2) "Do not behave as she does."

## your heart ##

AT: "yourself" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## do not go astray into her paths ##

This means the same as the sentence before it. It strengthens the first warning. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## do not go astray into ##

AT: "stay far away from" or "stay on the right path, and avoid"
