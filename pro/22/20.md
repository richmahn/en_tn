## translationNotes

* **Have I not written for you** - The answer to this question is "yes." AT: "I have written for you" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **instruction and knowledge** - These two words mean basically the same thing and emphasize the content of the thirty sayings. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **to those who sent you** - This implies that the person listening is or will be a messenger, a function similar to a diplomat or a negociator.
