## translationNotes

* **thorns and snares** - These two words together describe the dangers of the path. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **snare** - a trap to catch animals
* **whoever guards his soul** - "anyone who protects himself" AT: "whoever is careful with himself" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **the way he should go** - AT: "how he should live" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
