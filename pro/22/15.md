## translationNotes

* **Foolishness is bound up in the heart of a child** - AT: "The heart of a child is full of foolish things"
* **the rod of discipline** - AT: "discipline" or "a strict upbringing" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **drives it far away** - AT: "will take away the foolishness" or "will make a child wise"
* **to increase his wealth** - "to become richer" or "to gain more money"
* **gives to the rich** - AT: "gives money to the rich"
* **will come to poverty** - "will become poor"
