## translationNotes

* **crooked** - not straight; deformed; dishonest; deceitful
* **Put crooked speech away from you** - AT: "Never tell lies"
* **and put corrupt talk far from you** - AT: "don't ever say things that are not true"
* **Let your eyes look straight ahead** - AT: "keep remembering your goal" (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **and fix your gaze straight before you** - AT: 'and plan to keep doing the right things" (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
