## translationNotes

* **Hold on to discipline, do not let it go** - AT: Continue to obey what you have been taught." (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **for it is your life** - This phrase emphasizes the importance of discipline by equating it to a person's life.
* **Avoid it, do not go on it; turn away from it and go another way** - AT: "Be careful not to do evil. Instead do good." (See [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
