## Make a level path for your foot ##

AT: "Make a level path to walk on" or "Prepare well what you want to do" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## level ##

"smooth" or "even" AT: "easy to walk upon"

## a level path ... your ways ##

A path or way refers to the manner in which you live.

## secure ##

"safe"

## Do not turn aside to the right or to the left ##

"Do not turn and go off the path"

## turn your foot away from evil ##

AT: "Turn away from evil" or  "keep away from evil"

## your foot ##

"yourself" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
