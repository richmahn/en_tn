The father continues teaching his children.

## acquire ##

"work hard to gain for yourself" or "get"

## do not forget ##

"remember" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

## do not reject ##

"accept"

## do not abandon wisdom ##

"hold tightly to wisdom" or "work hard to become wise

## she will watch...love her...she will keep ##

Being wise is having a mother or loving woman to guard and protect you. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
