## translationNotes

* **Out of the mouth of those doing right** - "From the righteous man's words" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **the perverse tongue will be cut out** - "God will shut the mouths of people who say what is not true" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **lips of those who do right know what is acceptable** - "righteous people know what to say that is acceptable" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **mouth of the wicked** - "the words of the wicked" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
