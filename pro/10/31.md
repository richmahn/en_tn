## Out of the mouth of those doing right ##

"From the righteous man's words" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the perverse tongue will be cut out ##

"God will shut the mouths of people who say what is not true" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## lips of those who do right know what is acceptable ##

"righteous people know what to say that is acceptable" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## mouth of the wicked ##

"the words of the wicked" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
