# The wage ... the profit

These terms normally refer to the money a worker earns. Here they represents the results of either doing what is right or doing what is wrong. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

# There is a path to life for the one who follows discipline

"The person who obeys wise instruction will have a long and happy life"

# but the one who rejects correction is led astray

This can be stated in active form. AT: "but the one who does not obey wise instruction will not have a good life" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
