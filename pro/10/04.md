# A lazy hand

"Hand" represents the strength and ability of a person. AT: "A person unwilling to work" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# hand of the diligent

"Hand" represents the strength and ability of a person. AT: "person who works hard" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
