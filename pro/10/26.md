## translationNotes

* **Like vinegar on the teeth and smoke in the eyes, so is the sluggard to those who send him** - Sending a lazy person to accomplish a task is irritating and unpleasant. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
* **vinegar** - a sour liquid used to flavor or preserve foods
* **the years of the wicked** - "the lifetime of the evil person"
