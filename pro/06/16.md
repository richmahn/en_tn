# six things that Yahweh hates, seven that

This whole verse is a parallelism that emphasizes that God hates several things and not just one. AT: "six things that Yahwah hates; seven things that" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

# that are disgusting to him

"that make him feel disgust" or "that make you disgusting according to him"
