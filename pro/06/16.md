## six things that Yahweh hates, seven that ##

"six things that Yahwah hates; seven things that" This whole verse is a parallelism that emphasizes that God hates several things and not just one. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## that are disgusting to him ##
