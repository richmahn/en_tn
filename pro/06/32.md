## The one ##

AT: "The person" or "The man"

## what he deserves ##

AT: "the appropriate punishment for what he has done"

## his disgrace ##

AT: "the memory of his shameful act" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## will not be wiped away ##

AT: "will not be forgotten" or "will always remain" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])
