## on his lips ##

AT: "on him" or "with him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the prudent ##

a person who has good judgment or sense

## his own way ##

AT: "himself" or "how he lives" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the folly of fools is deception ##

The foolishness of fools is that they think they are wise, when they are not.
