## translationNotes

* **The one … the one** - AT: "The person … the person"
* **the poor** - "poor people"
* **Do not those who plot evil go astray?** - The implicit answer to this question is "yes". AT: "Those who plot evil will go astray" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **who plot evil** - AT: "who make evil plans" or "who make plans to do evil things"
* **will receive covenant faithfulness and trustworthiness** - AT: "will receive God's covenant faithfulness and enjoy his trustworthiness"
