# the one who

"the person who"

# pursues evil

"chases after evil" or "seeks to do evil"

# whose hearts are perverse

"Heart" represents the feelings, attitudes and motivations of a person. AT: "who have wicked thoughts" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
