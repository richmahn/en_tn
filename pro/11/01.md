Many verses in Chapter 11 are contrasting parallelisms (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## Yahweh ##

"God hates deceiving scales" or "God hates it when people are deceitful"

## but he delights in a precise weight ##

"but he delights in honest ways" or "but he is happy when people are honest"

## When pride comes then comes disgrace ##

"When a person is full of pride then he will suffer shame"

## but with humility comes wisdom ##

"but when a person is humble, he is wise"
