## this boasting … will not be silenced… ##

Paul will continue to boast through out all of Achaia which is modern day southern Greece. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])

## And why? Because I do not love you?  ##

AT: "I will continue to keep you from paying for my needs, because this shows others that I love you." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
