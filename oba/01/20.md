## The exiles of this host of the people of Israel ##

"Those of the host of the people of Israel who were in exile"

## host ##

a very large group of people

## Zarephath ##

the name of a Phoenician city on the coast of the Mediterranean between Tyre and Sidon (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Sepharad ##

the name of a place whose location is unknown.
