Jesus continues to teach his disciples how to pray

## our daily bread ##

This can be translated as "the food we need each day." Bread was in inexpensive food that people ate everyday. It is used here to refer to food in general.

## forgive us our sins ##

"Forgive us for sinning against you" or "Forgive our sins"

## for we ourselves forgive ##

"since we also forgive"

## who is in debt to us ##

"who have sinned against us" or "who have done wrong things to us"

## Do not lead us into temptation ##

This is a figure of speech that means "Lead us away from temptation." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-litotes]])
