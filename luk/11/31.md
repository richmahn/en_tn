## Queen of the South ##

This refers to the Queen of Sheba. Sheba was a kingdom south of Israel.

## will rise up in the judgment with ##

"will also stand up and judge"

## she came from the ends of the earth ##

"she came a very great distance." "From the ends of the earth" is an idiom that means "from a place very far away." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## someone greater than Solomon is here ##

The rebuke that Jesus wanted them to understand was "but you have not listened to me."
