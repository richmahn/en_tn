## He traveled  ##

<b>He traveled <b>- "John traveled"

## preaching a baptism of repentance ##

This could be translated as "preached that people should be baptized to show that they were repenting of their sins."

## for the forgiveness of sins ##

"so that their sins would be forgiven" or "so that God would forgive their sins." The repentance was for the forgiveness of sins.
