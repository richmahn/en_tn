## translationNotes

* **they fled** - "they quickly ran away"
* **found the man from whom the demons had gone out** - "saw the man whom the demons had left"
* **He was clothed** - "He was wearing clothes"
* **in his right mind** - "he was sane"  or "he was behaving normally"
* **sitting at the feet of Jesus** - This can also be translated as "sitting on the ground, listening to Jesus."
* **they were afraid** - "they were afraid of Jesus"
