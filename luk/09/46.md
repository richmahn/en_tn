## among them ##

"among the disciples"

## reasoning in their hearts ##

"thinking about individually" or "considering in their private thoughts"

## in my name ##

This refers to a person doing something as a representative of Jesus. AT: "because of me" (UDB) (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the one who sent me ##

"God, who sent me" (UDB)
