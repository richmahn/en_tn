(Jesus continues telling his story.)

## And he cried out and said ##

"and the rich man called out to say" or "he yelled out to Abraham"

## have mercy on me ##

This can be translated as "please have pity on me," or "please be merciful to me."

## and send Lazarus ##

This can be translated as "by sending Lazarus" or "Please send Lazarus to me" or "and tell Lazarus to come to me."

## he may dip the tip of his finger ##

This indicates the smallness of the amount requested. This can be translated as "he may wet the tip of his finger."

## I am in anguish in this flame ##

"I am in terrible pain in this flame" or "I am suffering terribly in this fire"
