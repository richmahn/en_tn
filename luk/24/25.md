## Jesus said to them ##

Use the dual form of "them."

## slow of heart ##

"your hearts are dull and slow to respond"

## Was it not necessary ##

This is a rhetorical question that means "It was necessary." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]]) Jesus did right by suffering and would have done wrong by not suffering.

## to enter into his glory ##

This refers to the time Jesus will show everyone his beauty and greatness and receive honor and worship.
