## translationNotes

* **A shower is coming** - "Rain is coming" or "It is going to rain" (UDB)
* **the earth and the heavens** - "the earth and the sky" or "the weather"
* **how is it that you do not know how to interpret the present time?** - This rhetorical question is used as a rebuke. It can be translated as "You should know how to interpret the present time." (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
