## translationNotes

* Jesus continues telling his parable.
* **the highways and hedges** - This refers to roads and paths outside of the city. It can be translated as "the main roads and paths outside of the city."
* **those men** - The word here for "men" means "male adults" and not just people in general.
* **who were first invited ** - "whom I first invited"
* **will taste my dinner** - "will enjoy the dinner I have prepared"
