# Therefore

"Because of this," referring to everything Jesus has said, beginning in [Luke 21:10](./10.md). (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/writing-connectingwords]])

# resolve in your hearts

Here "hearts" is a metonym for people's minds. AT: "make up your mind" or "decide firmly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# not to prepare your defense ahead of time

"not to figure out ahead of time what you will say in order to defend yourself against their accusations"
