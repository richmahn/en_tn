## during the Sabbath ##

"on a Sabbath day." Some languages would say "a Sabbath" because we do not know which particular Sabbath day it was.

## Behold ##

The word “behold” here alerts us to a new person in the story. Your language may have a way of doing this. English uses “There was a woman who …”

## an evil spirit of weakness ##

"an evil spirit that made her weak"
