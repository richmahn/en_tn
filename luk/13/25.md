(Jesus continues talking about entering into God's kingdom.)

## the owner of the house ##

This refers to God and can be translated as “God.”

## you will stand outside ##

Jesus was talking to a crowd. The form of "you" is plural (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]]). He is addressing them as if they will not enter through the door into the kingdom.

## Get away from me ##

"Go away from me"

## evil-doers ##

"people who do evil"
