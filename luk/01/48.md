Mary continues to praise God.

## lowly ##

"humble" or "poor" Mary did not have high social standing.

## paid attention ##

"thought about" or "remembered" or AT: "did not forget" This is not an issue of God's ability to remember, but his choosing to remember.

## from now on ##

“now and in the future”

## the Mighty One ##

AT: "God, the Powerful One"

## his name ##

Here "name" refers to the entire person of God. AT: "he" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
