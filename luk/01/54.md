## translationNotes

* This concludes Mary's praise to God.
* **He has helped** - AT: "The Lord has helped"
* **his servant Israel** - If readers confuse this with the the man named Israel, it could be translated as AT: "his servant, the nation of Israel" or AT: "Israel, his servants"
* **his descendants** - "Abraham's descendants"
* **just as he promised to our ancestors he would do** - AT: "because he promised our ancestors he would be merciful"
