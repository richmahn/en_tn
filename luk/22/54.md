## translationNotes

* **led him away** - "led Jesus away from the garden where they had arrested him"
* **they had kindled a fire** - This can also be translated as "some of them had kindled a fire." The fire was to keep them warm.
* **the middle of the courtyard** - This was the courtyard at the high priest's house. It had walls around it, but no roof.
