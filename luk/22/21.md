## translationNotes

* (Jesus continues speaking to his apostles.)
* **The one who betrays me** - "The one who will betray me"
* **For the Son of Man indeed goes** - "For, indeed, the Son of Man will go" or "For the Son of Man will die"
* **But woe to that man through whom he is betrayed!** - "But woe to the man who betrays the Son of Man!" or "But how terrible it will be for that man who betrays the Son of Man!"
