## translationNotes

* **he went back home with them** - "Jesus went back home with Mary and Joseph"
* **was obedient to them** - "obeyed them" or "was always obeying them"
* **treasured** - "carefully remembered" or "joyfully thought about." A treasure is something that is very valuable or precious. Mary considered the things her son did and said to be very precious. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **grow in wisdom and stature** - "become more wise and strong"
* **increased in favor with God and people** - This could be translated as "people liked him more and more, and God blessed him more and more."
