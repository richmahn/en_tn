## he went back home with them ##

"Jesus went back home with Mary and Joseph"

## was obedient to them ##

"obeyed them" or "was always obeying them"

## treasured all these things in her heart ##

"carefully remembered all these things" or "joyfully thought about all these things." A treasure is something that is very valuable or precious. Mary considered the things her son did and said to be very precious. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## grow in wisdom and stature ##

"become more wise and strong" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## increased in favor with God and people ##

This could be translated as "people liked him more and more, and God blessed him more and more."
