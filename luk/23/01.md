## The whole company of them ##

"All of the Jewish leaders"

## rose up ##

"stood" or "stood to their feet"

## before Pilate ##

"in front of Pilate." This could be translated as "to stand facing Pilate."

## perverting our nation ##

"causing trouble by telling lies to our people" (UDB)

## forbidding to give tribute ##

"telling them not to pay taxes" (UDB)
