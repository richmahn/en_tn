Habakkuk speaks to Yahweh about the Chaldeans.

## Are you not from ancient times, Yahweh my God, my Holy One? ##

Alternate translation: "You are eternal, Yahweh my God, my Holy One." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## them...them ##

the Chaldeans

## Rock ##

the protector of Israel (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## established them for correction ##

AT: "established them for the purpose of correcting Israel"
