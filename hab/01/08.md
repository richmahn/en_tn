## Their horses...their horses ##

the horses of the Chaldean soldiers

## their horsemen ##

The Chaldeans' soldiers riding the horses.

## leopards ##

large, swift cats (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])

## they fly like an eagle ##

"the horsemen ride as quickly as eagles fly" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## their multitudes go like a desert wind, and they gather captives like sand ##

The Chaldeans are many, and like the grains of sand the wind picks up in a storm are too many to count, so no one can count the people they capture and make prisoners. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
