## translationNotes

* **The king wept and cried loudly** - These two phrases share similar meanings and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **The king wept** - refers to King David who mourned or cried.
* **all the people** - This phrase refers to the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
