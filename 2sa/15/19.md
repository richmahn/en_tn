## translationNotes

* **Ittai** - The name of a man. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **Gittite** - This is the name of a people group.
* **Why did you go you with us, too?** - This is indicating that the king had not asked them to go along. AT: "I do not want to cause you to wonder around with us." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **Since you just left yesterday** - This is exaggeration used for emphasis. Ittai the Gittite had been with David at least 20 years.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
* **May loyalty and faithfulness go with you** - "May Yahweh bless you always."
