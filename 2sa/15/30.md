## translationNotes

* **barefoot** - wearing no shoes or sandals on his feet
* **his head covered** - a sign of mourning and shame
* **Ahithophel** - This is the name of a person. See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:15:11]].
* **conspirators** - people who join together against someone else
* **please turn Ahithophel's advice into foolishness** - AT: "please do not let  Ahithophel's plans to defeat me succeed"
