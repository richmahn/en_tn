## translationNotes

* **Michal** - This is the female name of David's wife (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **despised him in her heart** - Michal did not share David's joy over the arrival of the ark of the covenant in Jerusalem. AT: "didn't agree with him" or "she disliked his joy." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **her heart** - The word "heart" here represents Michal. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
