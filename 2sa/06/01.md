## all the chosen men of Israel ##

This refers to the army of the nation of Israel. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## Baalah ##

This is the name of a place. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## enthroned ##

to sit on a throne or a place of authority.

##  who sits enthroned over the cherubim  ##

AT: "who sits in his place of authority between the cherubim"
