## Yahweh has burst through my enemies before me like a bursting flood of water  ##

Here the victory that Yahweh brings is compared to  water overcoming its banks and covering the land and the destruction that causes." AT: "Yahweh had overwhelmed my enemies like a flood overwhelms the land." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
