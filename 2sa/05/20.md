# Baal Perazim

This is the name of a place. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Yahweh has burst through my enemies before me like a bursting flood of water

Here David speaks of the victory that Yahweh brought as if it were a flood of water that overflowed its banks and covered the land, causing destruction. AT: "Yahweh has overwhelmed my enemies like a flood overwhelms the land" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
