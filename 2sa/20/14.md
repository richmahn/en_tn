## translationNotes

* **to Abel, to Beth Maacah** - "Abel of Beth Maacha" or "Abel Beth Maacha." Both names refer to the same place. It is a city near the tribe of Dan. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **of the Berites** - This is the name of a people group. It may refer to the descendants of Bichri.
* **and also pursued Sheba** - "and also followed Sheba"
* **They caught up with him** - "Joab and the soldiers caught up with him"
* **against the city against the wall** - "against the city wall"
* **Listen, please listen** - This is repeated for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
