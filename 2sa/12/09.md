## You have killed Uriah the Hittite with the sword ##

David used the enemy army like a sword to kill Uriah the Hittite. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## the sword will never leave your house ##

Here the word "sword" refers to war. AT: "Your house will always have to fight in wars." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
