These verses contain three questions that are really statements (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Who knows whether or not Yahweh will be gracious to me, that the child may live? ##

The clear answer is: no one.

## now he is dead, so why should I fast? ##

The clear answer is that it would be of no use to fast any longer.

## Can I bring him back again? ##

The clear answer is: no.
