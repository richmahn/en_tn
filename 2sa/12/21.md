## translationNotes

* These verses contain three questions that are really statements (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **Who knows whether or not Yahweh will be gracious to me, that the child may live?** - The clear answer is: no one.
* **now he is dead, so why should I fast?** - The clear answer is that it would be of no use to fast any longer.
* **Can I bring him back again?** - The clear answer is: no.
