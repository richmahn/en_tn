* David continues to talk to Yahweh
* **the promise that you made ** - the covenant that was made with David and Israel before him by God
* **your servant and his family ** - This refers to David and the the nation Israel that made David their king (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **the people** - This refers to the nation of Israel. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
