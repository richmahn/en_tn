## And what nation is like your people Israel, the one nation on earth whom you, God, went and rescued for yourself? ##

This question is asked to encourage the audience to think deeply about something they already know. AT: "There is no nation like your people Israel" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## just as we have always heard ##

Here the "we" is referring to David and the nation of Israel.
