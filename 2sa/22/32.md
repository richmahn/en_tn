## translationNotes

* David continues his song to Yahweh.
* **For who is God except Yahweh? And who is a rock except our God?** - David uses questions to emphasize that there is no God except for Yahweh. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **who is a rock** - David compares Yahweh to a rock to emphasize his strength and ability to protect his people. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **he leads the blameless person on his path** - Yahweh keeps the blameless person safe and removes anything that may harm him.
