## lame in his feet ##

The word "feet" here refers to the ability to walk. AT: "he is unable to walk" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## Machir... Ammiel ##

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Lo Debar ##

This is the name of a place.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
