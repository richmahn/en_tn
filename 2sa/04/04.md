## five years old ##

the age of Jonathan's son at the time of his father's death (See:[[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])

## crippled in his feet  ##

This phrase means "unable to walk."

## nurse ##

a woman or girl who is hired to look after young children.

## became lame ##

This indicates that Mephibosheth was further injured so he was unable to walk.

## Mephibosheth ##

This is the male name of Jonathan's young son, the grandson of Saul (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
