## translationNotes

* **Ish Bosheth... Banal... Rechab... Rimmon** - These are male names. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **his hands became weak** - This phrase represents Ish Bosheth. AT: "Ish Bosheth became weak" or "Ish Bosheth lost all strength." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **(for Beeroth is also considered part of Benjamin, 3and the Beerothites fled to Gittaim and have been living there until this very time.)** - Here the author provides some background explanation. (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:writing_background]])
* **Beeroth... Gittaim** - These are the names of places.
