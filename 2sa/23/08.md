## Jeshbaal ##

This is the name of a man. Other versions read Josheb Basshebeth, Jashobeam, Ishbaal, or Ishbosheth, because various ancient manuscripts have these variations. Translators may choose to say this in a footnote to their translation (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Hachmonite ##

This is the name of a people group. AT: "the Tahchemonite" or "son of Hachmon"

## eight hundred ##

"800" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]])
