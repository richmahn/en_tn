## Three of the thirty ##

This is not the same three soldiers mentioned in 23:8-12.

## the thirty ##

"30" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-numbers]]) "the thirty bravest Israelite soldiers" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## cave of Adullam ##
