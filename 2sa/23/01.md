## translationNotes

* **Now** - This marks the beginning of a new section of the book.
* **these are the last words** - This refers to what David will say in 23:2-7.
* **the man who was highly honored. The one anointed by the God of Jacob** - AT: "the man whom the God of Jacob highly honored and anointed" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **anointed by the God of Jacob** - Anointing was pouring oil on a person's head, choosing who would serve God as king or priest. (See: symbolic Action)
* **psalmist** - This is a person who writes psalms or songs.
* **by me** - David is speaking.
* **and his word was on my tongue** - AT: "His Spirit gave me a message to speak" (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
