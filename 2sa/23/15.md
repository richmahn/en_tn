## translationNotes

* **broke through the army** - "fought their through the enemy army"
* **Should I drink the blood of men who have risked their lives?** - David uses a question for emphasis. He compares the water to blood because the men risked their lives to bring the water to him. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
