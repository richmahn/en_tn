## Should I drink the blood of men who have risked their lives? ##

David uses a question for emphasis. He compares the water to blood because the men risked their lives to bring the water to him. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
