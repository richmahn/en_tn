## Arameans ##

This is the name of a people-group (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Hadarezer... Shobach ##

These are the names of men.

## Helam ##

This is the name of a place.

## at their head ##

This phrase refers to a position of authority. AT: "leading them." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
