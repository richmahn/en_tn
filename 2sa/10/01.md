## Hanun... Nahash ##

These are the names of men. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## Do you really think that David is honoring your father because he has sent men to comfort you? ##

This question is indicating strong doubt with regard to David's intentions. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## Has not David sent his servants to you to look at the city, to spy it out, in order to overthrow it ##

This question is indicating strong doubt that David is not spying on them.  (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
