## translationNotes

* Joab continues to explain why the army was so close to the wall.
* **Abimelech** - This man named Abimelech was a son of Gideon. (See: [[:en/bible/notes/jdg/08/29]] and [[:en/bible/notes/jdg/09/52]])
* **Jerubbesheth** - This is another name for Gideon.
* **Did not a woman cast an upper millstone on him from the wall, so that he died at Thebez?** - This question is stating a fact.  (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **millstone** - A heavy stone that would roll, used to crush grain for making bread.
* **Thebez** - This is the name of a city. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
