## translationNotes

* **in the sight of all Israel** - Here "sight" refers to anyone being able to see this and know what was happening, though not everyone did see it. AT: "so that anyone can see it." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **as if a man heard from the mouth of God himself** - Here "mouth" represents the whole authority of God.  AT: "as if God had said it with his own mouth." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
* **viewed** - "understood" or "accepted"
