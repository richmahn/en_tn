## loyalty ##

a strong feeling of support and love

## Is this your loyalty to your friend? Why did you not go with him? ##

These questions are asked to criticize Hushai. AT: "You have been loyal to David, so you should be with him." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

## the one whom Yahweh ##

AT: "Absalom, whom Yahweh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
