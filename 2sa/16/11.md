## Leave him alone and let him curse, ##

AT: "Do not stop him from cursing me," (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## Perhaps Yahweh will look at the misery unleashed on me, and repay me with good for his cursing me today. ##

AT: "It may happen that Yahweh will look at the difficulty I have had,  and instead give me good for his cursing me today."
