# Rizpah ... Aiah

Rizpah was a woman and her father was Aiah. See how you translated these names in [2 Samuel 3:7](../03/07.md).

# Armoni and Mephibosheth ... Adriel ... Barzillai

These are names of men. This is not the same Mephibosheth as the son of Jonathan. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

# Merab

This is the name of a woman. See how you translated it in [2 Samuel 3:13](../03/13.md).

# Meholathite

This is the name of a people group. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
