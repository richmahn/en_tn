## translationNotes

* **heart afflicted him** - AT: "felt guilty" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **Now, Yahweh, take away your servant's guilt** - David refers to himself as "your servant." This is a polite way to speak to someone with greater authority. AT: "Please, Yahweh, take this guilt away from me, your servant"
