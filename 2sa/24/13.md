* **I am in deep trouble** - "I am in terrible trouble"
* **Let us fall into Yahweh's hands rather than into the hands of people** - Here "hands" refer to power or control. AT: "Let Yahweh and not people punish us" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
