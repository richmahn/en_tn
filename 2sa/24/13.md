# I am in deep trouble

"I am in terrible trouble"

# Let us fall into Yahweh's hands rather than into the hand of man

Here "hands" refer to power or control. AT: "Let Yahweh and not people punish us" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
