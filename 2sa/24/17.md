## translationNotes

* **I have sinned, and I have acted perversely** - These mean the same thing and are combined for emphasis. AT: "I have sinned terribly" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **But these sheep, what have they done?** - David uses a question and compares the people to sheep to emphasize that they have done nothing wrong. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **Please let your hand punish me** - Here "hand" refers to power. AT: "Please punish me" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
