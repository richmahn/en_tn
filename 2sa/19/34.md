## translationNotes

* **How many days are left in the years of my life, that I should go up with the king to Jerusalem?** - AT: "I am certainly not going to live many more years. There is no good reason for me to go up with the king to Jerusalem!" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **Why then should your servant be a burden to my master the king?** - AT: "You would have to work hard to take care of me, and I would not be able to do anything good for you. That would be wrong." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
