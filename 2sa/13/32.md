## translationNotes

* **Jonadab... Shimeah** - Translate these men's names as you did in [[en:bible:notes:2sa:13:03]].
* **Let not my master** - Jonadab is speaking to David but is calling him "my master" to show respect. "Do not"
* **do not take this report to heart** - Here "heart" represents David's belief. AT: "Do not believe this report." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
