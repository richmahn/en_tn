## Jonadab... Shimeah ##

Translate these men's names as you did in [2 Samuel 13:3](./03.md).

## Let not my master ##

Jonadab is speaking to David but is calling him "my master" to show respect. "Do not"

## do not take this report to heart ##

Here "heart" represents David's belief. AT: "Do not believe this report." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
