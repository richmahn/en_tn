## translationNotes

* (Paul ends his letter to Titus.)
* **All those** - "All the people"
* **those who love us in faith- ** - <b>those who love us in faith- <b> Possible meanings are 1) "the believers who love us" or 2) "the believers who love us because we share the same belief."
* **Grace be with all of you- ** - <b>Grace be with all of you- <b>  This was a common Christian greeting. It can also be translated as "May God's grace be with you" or "I ask that God will be gracious to all of you."
