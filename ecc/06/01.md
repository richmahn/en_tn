## translationNotes

* **it is severe for men** - AT: "it causes hardship for people"
* **riches, wealth** - These two words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_doublet]])
* **he lacks nothing** - AT: "he has everything"
* **God gives him no ability to enjoy it.** - To acquire riches or possessions and not be able to enjoy them is futile.
* **This is vapor, an evil affliction.** - To be unable to enjoy one's possessions is an evil affliction or curse.
