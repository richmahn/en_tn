## translationNotes

* **to fill his mouth** - AT: "to put food in his mouth" or "to eat" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **his appetite is not filled** - AT: "he does not satisfy his appetite" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **what advantage has the wise person over the fool?** - A wise person's wisdom doesn't entitle him to any additional comforts. AT: "the wise person has no advantage over the fool." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **What advantage does the poor man have even if he knows how to act in front of other people?** - AT: "The poor man has no advantage even if he knows how to act in front of other people." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
