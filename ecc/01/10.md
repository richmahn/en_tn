# Is there anything about which it may be said, 'Look, this is new'?

This rhetorical question is asked to emphasize that man cannot say there is anything new. This can be written as a statement. Alternate translation: "There is nothing about which it may be said, 'Look, this is new.'" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])

# about which it may be said

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "about which someone may say" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
