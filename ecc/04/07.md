## no son or brother ##

This person has no family.

## his eyes are not satisfied ##

AT: "he is not satisfied" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## "For whom am I toiling and depriving myself of pleasure?" ##

AT: "Will anyone benefit from me working hard and not enjoying myself?" or "My work does not benefit anyone and I am not enjoying myself." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
