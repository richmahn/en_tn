## translationNotes

* Instead of choosing the wise youth, the people choose the king's son who may not be any wiser.
* **alive and walking around** - The words "alive" and "walking around" mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **There is no end to all the people** - AT: "There are very many people" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_hyperbole]])
* **vapor and an attempt to shepherd the wind** - See how you translated this in [[en:bible:notes:ecc:01:12]].
