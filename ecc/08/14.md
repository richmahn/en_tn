## useless vapor ##

"meaningless" or "futility". Things that happen but shouldn't. Such as good things happening to wicked people and bad things happening to good people.

## something else that is done on the earth ##

AT: "something else that people do on the earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## for all the days of his life that God has given him ##

AT: “for as long as God allows him to live” (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])
