## translationNotes

* **useless vapor** - "meaningless" or "futility". Things that happen but shouldn't. Such as good things happening to wicked people and bad things happening to good people.
* **something else that is done on the earth** - AT: "something else that people do on the earth" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **for all the days of his life that God has given him** - AT: “for as long as God allows him to live” (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
