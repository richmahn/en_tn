## I said in my heart ##

AT: "I said to myself" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## I will test you with happiness ##

Here the word "you" refers himself. AT: "I will test myself with things that make me happy" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

## So enjoy pleasure ##

AT: "So I will enjoy things that please me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

## I said about laughter, "It is crazy" ##

AT: "I said that it is crazy to laugh at things" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-quotations]])

## What use is it? ##

AT: "It is useless" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rquestion]])
