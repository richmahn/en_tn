## translationNotes

* **Whatever my eyes desired** - AT: "Whatever I saw and desired" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]])
* **I did not withhold from them** - This can be stated positively. AT: "I got for myself"
* **I did not withhold my heart from any pleasure** - This can be stated positively. AT: "I allowed myself to enjoy everything that made me happy" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_synecdoche]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_abstractnouns]])
* **my heart rejoiced** - AT: "I rejoiced"
