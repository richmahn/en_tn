## Whatever my eyes desired ##

AT: "Whatever I saw and desired" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## I did not withhold from them ##

This can be stated positively. AT: "I got for myself"

## I did not withhold my heart from any pleasure ##

This can be stated positively. AT: "I allowed myself to enjoy everything that made me happy" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

## my heart rejoiced ##

AT: "I rejoiced"
