## translationNotes

* **I thought about all this in my mind** - AT: "I thought very deeply about all this"
* **They are all in God's hands** - Here the word "they" refers to "the righteous and wise people" as well as "their deeds."
* **in God's hands** - Here the word "hands" refers to power and authority. AT: "under God's control" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
