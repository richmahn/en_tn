## translationNotes

* **every word that is spoken** - AT: "everything that people say" (UDB) (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **you know yourself** - "you yourself know." Here "yourself" is used to emphasize that "you know." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_rpronouns]])
* **in your own heart** - AT: "in your own thoughts" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
