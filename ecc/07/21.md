## every word that is spoken ##

AT: "everything that people say" (UDB) (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## you know yourself ##

"you yourself know." Here "yourself" is used to emphasize that "you know." (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-rpronouns]])

## in your own heart ##

AT: "in your own thoughts" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
