## translationNotes

* **they have gone away looking for many difficulties** - Possible meanings are 1) "they have made many sinful plans" or 2) "they have made their own lives difficult."
* **they have gone away** - Here the word "they" refers to "humanity." (See: [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_singularpronouns]])
