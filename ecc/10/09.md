# cuts out stones

This refers working in a quarry and cutting larger stones.

# can be hurt by them

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "those stones can hurt him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# is endangered by it

This can be stated in active form. Alternate translation: "the wood may injure him" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
