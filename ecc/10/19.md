# People prepare food for laughter

The word "laughter" can be expressed as a verb. Alternate translation: "People prepare food in order to laugh" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

# wine brings enjoyment to life

The word "enjoyment" can be expressed as a verb. Alternate translation: "wine helps people to enjoy life" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])

# money fills the need for everything

Possible meanings are 1) "money provides for every need" or 2) "money provides for both food and wine"
