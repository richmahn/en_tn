## speak with your mouth ##

To make a promise or vow to God.

## Do not be too quick...do not let your heart be too quick ##

These two phrases mean the same thing. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## let your words be few ##

AT: "do not say too much"
