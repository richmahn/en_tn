## Look ##

"pay attention" or "listen up"

## good and suitable ##

These two words mean basically the same thing and are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])

## during the days of this life that God has given us ##

AT: "as long as God allows us to live" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## man's assignment ##

Possible meanings are 1) "man's reward" or 2) "man's lot in life."
