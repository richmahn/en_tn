## translationNotes

* **the women in Zion, the virgins in the cities of Judah** - AT; "all the women, young and old, in Jerusalem and in the cities of Judah" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]])
* **They hung princes by their own hands** - Possible meanings are 1) "With their own hands, they hung princes" or 2) they tied each prince's hands together with one end of a rope and tied the other end so the prince's feet could not touch the ground.
