## the women in Zion, the virgins in the cities of Judah ##

AT; "all the women, young and old, in Jerusalem and in the cities of Judah" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## They hung princes by their own hands ##

Possible meanings are 1) "With their own hands, they hung princes" or 2) they tied each prince's hands together with one end of a rope and tied the other end so the prince's feet could not touch the ground.
