## the lips of those that rose up against me ##

AT: "the words that those who rose up against me spoke" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## those that rose up against me ##

AT: "those who attack me" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## their sitting or their rising up ##

AT: "When they sit down and when they get up" or "Whenever they do anything" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-merism]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-abstractnouns]])
