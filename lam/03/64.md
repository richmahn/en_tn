## Pay back to them, Yahweh, as much harm as what their hands have done ##

AT: "They have made me suffer, Yahweh, so please make them suffer now"

## what their hands have done ##

AT: "what they have done" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

AT: "Make them deeply afraid" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])
