## translationNotes

* **the one who waits for him** - Possible meanings are 1) "all those who depend on him" (UDB) or 2) "the one who waits patiently for him to act."
* **the life that seeks him** - AT: "the person who seeks him." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_parallelism]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **bear the yoke** - "suffer" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **laid it upon him** - "has laid the yoke upon him"
* **put his mouth in the dust** - AT: "bown down with his mouth on the ground" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
