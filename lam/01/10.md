## has put his hand on ##

AT: "has taken possession of" or "has stolen" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## treasures ##

"wealth"

## she has seen ##

The word "she" refers to Jerusalem. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-personification]])

## you had commanded ##

The word "you" refers to Yahweh. This means the author or the city is still speaking, after starting in [Lamentations 1:8](./08.md).
