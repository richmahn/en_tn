## translationNotes

* **Hear me groan** - Jerusalem continues to speak like a person. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **my adversity** - "my misery" or "my injury" or "my disaster"
* **you have done it** - Here the word "you" refers to Yahweh.
* **Bring the day that** - AT: "Today, do what" or "Bring about soon what" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
