## translationNotes

* **scattered them** - scattered the prophets and priests
* **look on them with favor** - AT: "accept them" or "approve of them" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_idiom]])
* **No one receives ... they show no concern** - AT: "No one receives ... no one shows concern" or "People do not receive ... people do not show concern"
