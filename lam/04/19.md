## translationNotes

* **The breath in our nostrils** - This is a title for the king, who provides life to his people like breath keeps the body alive. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **was taken in their pits** - Here "pits" refers to the enemies' trap, their plan to catch the king. AT: "the enemies made plans to capture our king, and they did capture him" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]], [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
