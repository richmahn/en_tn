## translationNotes

* **iniquity ... ** - Possible meanings are 1) "sin ... sin " or 2) "punishment for the sin ... punishment."
* **the daughter of my people** - This is a poetic name for Jerusalem, which is spoken of here as if it were a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **who was overthrown in a moment** - AT: "whom God destroyed in a moment" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **though no hands seized her** - AT: "though no human soldiers captured her" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
