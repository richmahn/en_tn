## translationNotes

* The writer begins to address Jerusalem.
* **What can I say ...?** - AT: "There is nothing that I can say ...." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **daughter of Jerusalem** - This is a poetic name for Jerusalem, which is spoken of here as if it were a woman. (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]])
* **What will I compare to you ...?** - AT: "There is nothing to which I can compare you ...." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
* **Who can heal you?** - AT: "No one can heal you." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_rquestion]])
