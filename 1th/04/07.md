# God did not call us to uncleanness, but to holiness

This can be stated in positive form. Alternate translation: "God called us to cleanness and holiness" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublenegatives]])

# God did not call us

The word "us" refers to all believers. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-inclusive]])
