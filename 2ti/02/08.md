## translationNotes

* **according to my gospel message** - "that is my gospel"
* **by which I suffer** - "the gospel message for which I suffer"
* **chained** - "imprisoned"  (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **the word of God is not chained** - "is not restricted" or "is not imprisoned" or "has full freedom"
* **for those who are chosen** - "for the people whom God has chosen"
* **may obtain the salvation** - "will receive salvation" or "God will save them"
* **with eternal glory** - "glorifying God forever" or "pointing people to God forever"
