## translationNotes

* **in meekness** - "meekly" or "gently"
* **educating** - "instructing" or "teaching" or "correcting"
* **give them repentance** - "help them to turn from their sin"
* **for the knowledge of the truth** - "so that they will know the truth"
* **they may regain their senses** - "they may no longer think wrong thoughts" or "they may start listening to God again"
* **the trap of the devil** - People who have fallen into <b>the trap of the devil</b>  is a metaphor for people who think they are following God when they are really following the devil. (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
* **having been captured by him for his will** - "the devil has captured them and now makes them do whatever he wants them to do" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]])
