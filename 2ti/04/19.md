## Greet  ##

<b>Greet <b>- "Say hello to ... for me" or "Tell ... that I am thinking of them"

## house of Onesiphorus ##

"family of Onesiphorus"

## Do your best to come ##

This can also be translated "Make a way to come."

## also Pudens, Linus, Claudia, and all the brothers  ##

<b>also Pudens, Linus, Claudia, and all the brothers <b>- "Pudens, Linus, Claudia and all the brothers also greet you"

## all the brothers ##

"all the believers here," male and female

## May the Lord be with your spirit ##

"I am asking God to make your spirit strong"

## May grace be with you  ##

<b>May grace be with you <b>- "I am asking the Lord to show his kindness to all of you there" or "I am asking the Lord to show favor to all of you there"
