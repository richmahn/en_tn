## dangerous times ##

These will be days, months, or even years when Christians will be in danger from the people Paul describes in verses 2-4.

## without natural affection ##

"not loving their own families"

## implacable ##

" not agreeing with anyone" or "not living in peace with anyone"

## slanderers ##

"false accusers"

## violent ##

"fierce" or "savage" or “they will often do things to hurt other people”

## not lovers of good ##

"haters of good"

## headstrong ##

"reckless"

## conceited ##

"they will think that they are better people than they are"
