# All scripture has been inspired by God

Some Bibles translate this as "All scripture is God-breathed." This means God produced the scripture through his Spirit by telling people what to write. This can be stated in active form. AT: "God has spoken all scripture by his Spirit" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# It is profitable

"It is useful" or "It is beneficial"

# for conviction

"for pointing out errors"

# for correction

"for fixing errors"

# for training in righteousness

"for training people to be righteous"

# the man of God

This means any believer in God whether male or female. AT: "all believers" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-gendernotations]])

# may be competent, equipped

"may be completely prepared"
