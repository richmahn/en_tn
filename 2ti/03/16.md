## All Scripture has been inspired by God ##

“God has spoken all the Scriptures by his Spirit, and it is profitable” (see UDB) or “all Scripture is God-breathed," produced by the Spirit of God. God told people what to write.

## profitable ##

“useful,” “beneficial”

## conviction ##

“pointing out error”

## correction ##

“fixing errors”

## training ##

“discipling” or “bringing up”

## competent ##

"complete"
