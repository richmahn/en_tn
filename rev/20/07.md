## at the four corners of the earth ##

See how you translated this in [Revelation 7:1](../07/01.md).

## Gog and Magog ##

These are the names that the prophet Ezekiel used to represent far away countries. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## They will be as many as the sand of the sea ##

This emphasizes the extremely large number of soldiers in Satan's army. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
