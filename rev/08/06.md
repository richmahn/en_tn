# General Information:

The seven angels sound the seven trumpets, one at a time.

# It was thrown down onto the earth

This can be stated in active form. AT: "The angel threw the hail and fire mixed with blood down onto the earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

# a third of it was burned up, a third of the trees were burned up, and all the green grass was burned up

This can be stated in active form. AT: "it burned up a third of the earth, a third of the trees, and all the green grass" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
