## locusts ##

insects that fly together in large groups (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-unknown]])

## power like that of scorpions  ##

Scorpions have the power to sting and poison other animals. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## scorpions ##

small animals with poisonous stingers like bees' stingers on their tails

## forehead ##

The top of the face, above the eyes.
