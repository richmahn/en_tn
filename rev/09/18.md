* **their tails were like snakes** - The horses' tails are compared to snakes. Possible meanings: 1) "their tails had heads like snakes" (UDB) or 2) "their tails were thin with a large end and looked similar to a snake." (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_simile]])
