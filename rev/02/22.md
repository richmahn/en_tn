## translationNotes

* **throw her into a sickbed** - AT: "I will make her sick" or "I will punish her with illness" (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_metaphor]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_metonymy]])
* **commit adultery** - "practice adultery"
* **they repent of what she has done** - AT: "they repent for being like her"
* **I will strike her children dead** - AT: "I will kill her children"
* **I am he who searches thoughts and desires** - AT: "I search thoughts and desires" or "I search minds and hearts"
