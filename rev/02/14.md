## Balak ##

This is the name of a king. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])

## who taught Balak to throw a stumbling block before the children of Israel ##

AT: "who showed Balak how to cause the people of Israel to sin"

## eat food sacrificed to idols ##

AT: "sacrifice food to idols and then eat it" (See: Active - Passive)

## be sexually immoral ##

AT: "sin sexually" or "commit sexual sin" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
