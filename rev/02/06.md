## translationNotes

* This continues the son of man's message to believers in Ephesus.
* **Nicolaitans** - the people who followed the teachings of a man named Nicolaus. (See: [[:en:ta:vol1:translate:translate_names]])
* **If you have an ear, listen** - spiritual ears. This means the person who can hear God and understand his message.
* **I will permit to eat** - "I will allow them to eat"
