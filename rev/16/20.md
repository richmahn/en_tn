# Connecting Statement:

This is part of the seventh bowl of God's wrath.

# the mountains were no longer found

The inability to see any mountains is metonymy expressing the idea that no mountains existed any longer. AT: "there were no longer any mountains" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

# a talent

You may convert this to a modern measure. AT: "33 kilograms" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-bweight]])
