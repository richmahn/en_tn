## I heard ##

Here "I" refers to John.

## Hallelujah ##

This word means "Praise God" or "Let us praise God."

## the great prostitute ##

This refers to the wicked people who rule over other people of the earth, leading them to worship false gods. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## who corrupted the earth ##

AT: "who corrupted the people of the earth" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
