## All the angels ... living creatures ##

AT: "All the angels stood around the throne and the elders and the four living creatures"

## the four living creatures ##

These are the four creatures mentioned in [Revelation 4:6](../04/06.md).

## Praise, glory ... be to our God ##

AT: "Our God is worthy of all praise, glory, wisdom, thanks, honor, power and strength"

## forever and ever ##

These two words are combined for emphasis. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-doublet]])
