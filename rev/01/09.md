## your...you ##

the believers in the seven churches (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-you]])

## who shares with you in the suffering and kingdom and patient endurance that are in Jesus ##

AT: "who shares with you in God's kingdom. I also suffer and patiently endures trial along with you because we belong to Jesus"

## because of the word of God ##

AT: "because I told about the word of God"

## in the Spirit ##

This means effected by God's Spirit. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-idiom]])

## the Lord's day ##

The day of worship for believers in Christ.

## loud voice like a trumpet ##

The voice was so loud it sounded like a trumpet. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## to Smyrna, to Pergamum, to Thyatira, to Sardis, to Philadelphia, and to Laodicea ##

These are names of cities in Asia which is modern Turkey. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/translate-names]])
