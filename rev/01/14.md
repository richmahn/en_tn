# wool

This is the hair of a sheep or goat. It was known to be very white.

# his eyes were like a flame of fire

His eyes are described as being full of light like a flame of fire. Alternate translation: "his eyes were glowing like a flame of fire" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])
