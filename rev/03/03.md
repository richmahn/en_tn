## wake up ##

This expression means they should start acting correctly. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## I will come as a thief ##

Jesus will come at an unexpected time, just as a thief is not expected. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-simile]])

## did not make their clothes dirty ##

Dirty clothes represent sin in their lives. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## dressed in white ##

White clothes represent a pure life. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
