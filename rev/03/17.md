## most miserable, pitiable, poor, blind and naked ##

These terms refer to a person who is in very bad spiritual condition, but does not know they their situation is so bad. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## become rich ##

This refers to becoming spiritually clean and having a good relationship with God. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])

## brilliant white garments ##

White garments represent spiritual purity. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
