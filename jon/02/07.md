## translationNotes

* This is a continuation of Jonah's prayer that started in [[:en:bible:notes:jon:02:01]].
* **I remembered Yahweh** - Since Jonah was praying to Yahweh, it might be more clear in some languages to say "I thought about you, Yahweh"  or "Yahweh, I thought about you." (UDB)
* **then my prayer came to you, to your holy temple** - "then you in your holy temple heard my prayer"
* **Those who pay attention to useless gods** - "People who pay attention to useless gods"
* **reject your faithfulness toward themselves** - "are rejecting you, who would be faithful to them"
