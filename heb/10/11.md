## Indeed ##

This connects [Hebrews 10:1](./01.md) with the words that follow.

## his enemies were humbled and made a stool for his feet ##

"God humbled and completely shamed Christ's enemies." It was a great shame for people to be put under other people's feet. (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])

## those who are sanctified ##

AT: "those whom God sanctified" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])
