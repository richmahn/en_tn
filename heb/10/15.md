# General Information:

This is a quotation from the prophet Jeremiah in the Old Testament.

# with them

"with my people"

# after those days

"when the time of the first covenant with my people has finished"

# I will put my laws in their hearts, and I will write them on their minds

People's hearts and minds are spoken of as if God's laws could be put or written on them. AT: "I will cause them to understand my laws and I will cause them to obey them" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metaphor]])
