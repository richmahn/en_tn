## as if you were with them...as if your bodies were being mistreated like theirs ##

Both these phrases encourage believers to think about other people's suffering as they would think about their own suffering. AT: "as if you were in prison with them and being mistreated" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-parallelism]])

## marriage bed ##

the sexual relationship between a husband and wife (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-euphemism]], [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
