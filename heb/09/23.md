## the copies of the things in heaven should be cleansed with these animal sacrifices ##

AT: "the priests should use these animal sacrifices to cleanse the copies of the things in heaven" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]] and [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-explicit]])

## the heavenly things themselves had to be cleansed with much better sacrifices ##

AT: "as for the heavenly things themselves, God had to cleanse them with much better sacrifices" than the sacrifices used to cleanse the earthly copies
