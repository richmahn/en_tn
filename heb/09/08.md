## the way...has not yet been revealed ##

AT: "God has not yet revealed the way" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-activepassive]])

## the most holy place ##

Possible meanings: 1) the inner room of the tabernacle on earth (see UDB) or 2) God's presence in heaven.

## the first tabernacle is still standing ##

Possible meanings: 1) "the outer room of the tabernacle is still standing (see UDB) or 2) "the sacrificial system continues" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-metonymy]])
