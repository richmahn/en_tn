## translationNotes

* The writer of Hebrews continues to describe the past leaders of God's people and their faith in God's promises.
* **Indeed** - "Actually" or "For sure" or "Pay attention to this:"
* **heavenly one** - "heavenly country" or "country in heaven"
* **God is not ashamed to be called their God** - AT: "God is happy to have them call him their God" or "...to have others say of them that he is...." (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_litotes]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
