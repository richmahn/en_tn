## translationNotes

* **they passed** - the Israelites passed
* **they were swallowed up** - AT: "the water swallowed them" or "they drowned" (See: [[en:ta:vol1:translate:figs_explicit]] and [[en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_personification]] and [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **they had been circled around for seven days** - AT: "the Israelites marched around them for seven days" (See: [[:en:ta:vol2:translate:figs_activepassive]])
* **had received the spies in safety** - AT: "hid the spies to keep them safe"
