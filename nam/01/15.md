## there is the feet of him who brings good news ##

"there is he who brings good news" or "someone is bringing good news" (See: [[rc://en/ta/man/translate/figs-synecdoche]])

## wicked one...he ##

Nahum refers to the Ninevites as though they were one person.

## he is completely cut off ##

Nahum speaks of a future event as though it has already happened see (UDB).
